Note: this is a deliberate exception, not a mechanical derivation — the same MC initial+final as [[忙]]/[[茫]]/[[莽]] (and, independently, 芒's own phonetic parent [[亡]]) would place 芒 at [ㄇㄚㄫ](../syllables/ㄇㄚㄫ.md) instead, which already has 13 tenants — the most crowded syllable in the corpus. Placed at ㄇ⼘ㄫ instead, matching the phonology [[鋒鋩]] had already assumed for this concept before 芒 had its own page.
