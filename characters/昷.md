## Notes
- [List of 指事](lookup/List%20of%20指事.md): a person (人) bathing in warm water (now written 囗) in a container (皿) full of warm water — original form of 溫 (OC \*quːn). Simplified from 𥁕 (囚 → 日). Alternatively, according to Xu Shen, an ideogrammic compound (會意 / 会意): 囚 (“prisoner”) + 皿 (“dish”) – feeding a prisoner from a dish. However, the shape in the oracle bones is different.
