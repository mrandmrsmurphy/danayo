## Notes
- 会意 of [[羽]] ("wings") + [[日]] ("sun") — a bird repeatedly flapping its wings; to learn to fly, to practice. In the modern glyph 日 has transformed into [[Radical 106|白]] ("white") — the field's stored `graphemic_classification` (白) reflects this modern surface form, distinct from the original etymological component 日.
