## Numeral
- ten thousand, myriad, 10,000
- [SKIP-3-2-1](lookup/SKIP/SKIP-3/SKIP-3-2-1.md) ([Stroke 03](lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2003.md))
- [List of 象形](lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): a simplified form of [[萬]], which originally depicted a scorpion; the creature's once-common abundance gave rise to the borrowed sense "myriad, ten-thousand."
- 106th most used character in Classical Chinese. Ancient [[Lookup/CC/initials/聲 微|ɱ]] + [[Lookup/CC/finals/韻 元合|ʉɐn]] → [ㄇㄛㄋ](syllables/ㄇㄛㄋ.md)
- [Grade 1](lookup/Grade%201.md), [HSK Beginner](lookup/HSK/HSK%20Beginner.md), [Jōyō - Kyōiku](lookup/Japanese/Jōyō%20-%20Kyōiku.md) (2), [Korean MS](lookup/Korean/Korean%20MS.md)
- The sound 몬 is overfull, so this character must always be written/spoken as "one-myriad," i.e. <ruby>[[一万]]<rt>ㄧㄊㄇㄛㄋ</rt></ruby>. The complicated original (non-shinjitai) character [[萬]] is retained as a 名字 for anti-falsification purposes.
