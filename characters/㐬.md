## Notes
- [List of 象形](lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): a newborn baby, shown upside down. The upper portion is an inverted 子. The three lower lines represent amniotic fluid or hair. The interpretation as fluid is supported by the comparison of 旒 (liú) and 游 (yóu), originally variants of one another.
