## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*kraː): oracle-bone form is semantic [[Radical 040|宀]] ("roof") + phonetic 𢑓 (xiá, "male pig"); the phonetic later corrupted to 豕 ("pig"), which Shuowen mistook for an abbreviated 豭 ("boar") — a folk etymology, since it misses the true original component 𢑓.
- [SKIP-2-3-7](lookup/SKIP/SKIP-2/SKIP-2-3-7.md) ([Stroke 10](lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2010.md))
