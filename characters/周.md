## Notes

- [List of 象形](lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): Oracle bone script: Pictogram (象形): a jade artifact with carved patterns. Original form of 彫 (OC *tɯːw, “to carve”). The character was later borrowed as a country name and 口 was added as a distinguishing mark. A semi-conservative version in which the carvings are visible is 𠕛. The top part of the character gradually became similar to 用 in the late Western Zhou period. [ 1 ] See also 黃, which could have a reference to a jade artifact.
