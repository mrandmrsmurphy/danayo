## Notes
- [List of 象形](../lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): depicts a person exhaling — the base figure shared with [[Radical 076|欠]] ("to yawn, lack"), with breath or saliva rendered as flanking dots (shown as 二 or 冫 depending on font — not literally "two" or "ice"). Shuowen Jiezi's traditional reading as a compound of phonetic 二 + semantic 欠 is refuted by oracle-bone forms; kept here as 象形 per modern paleography.
