## Notes
- 会意 (OC \*sri) of 𠂤 ([[堆]], "hills, mound" — an ancient variant form of 堆, no character page of its own in this vault) and 帀 (uncertain origin; Shuowen reads it as an inverted 之 "footprint," i.e. "to go around, encircle," also with no character page of its own) — a multitude encircling a mound, the original sense "army, troops"; later extended to "teacher, master," one who gathers and leads followers.
