## Notes
- 会意 of [[𠂇]] ("left hand," a pictograph originally resembling 又 "right hand") and [[Radical 048|工]] ("work") — a left hand holding a tool to assist. Later bronze forms show 工 added to an original bare 𠂇; also analyzable as phono-semantic, with 𠂇 supplying the sound.
