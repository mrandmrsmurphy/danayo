## Notes


In Dan'a'yo, **裡** and **裏** are longer treated as orthographic variants. It has two complementary layers: **Spatial/Abstract Interiority** vs. **Structural/Physical Reversals.**  This stems from it's original meaning as _"the interior of an article of clothing"_.

### 1. Interior, Inside, Within
**Function:** Productive Suffix | **Domain:** 3D Space, Abstract States, Time 
The character **裡** (using the side clothing radical **衤**) denotes the "state of being contained." It functions as a modern, productive suffix that converts a noun into a locative or state-based noun.
- **Semantic Scope:** * **Physical Containment:** The 3D space inside an object.
    - **Abstract/Mental Space:** Psychological or emotional "interiors."
    - **Temporal/Existential:** Within a period or a specific state of being.

**Common Compounds (Suffix Focus):**

| Term | Gloss | Meaning | 
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **箱裏** | _Xiānglǐ_ | Inside the box (Physical container) |
| **心裏** | _Xīnlǐ_ | In one's mind/heart (Abstract interior) | 
| **夢裏** | _Mènglǐ_ | Within a dream (Existential state) |
| **夜裏** | _Yèlǐ_ | During the night (Temporal containment) | 
| **宮裏** | _Gōnglǐ_ | Within the palace walls (Spatial domain) |

### 2. Reverse, Lining, BTS
**Function:** Primary Root / Prefix | **Domain:** 2D Surfaces, Hidden Layers, Subterfuge 
It also denotes the "flip side" or "hidden layer." It typically appears at the beginning of compounds to describe the nature of a surface or a secret action.
- **Semantic Scope:** * **Physical Lining:** The fabric or material on the underside of a garment.
    - **The "Back" Side:** The reverse side of a flat object (paper, maps, coins).
    - **Behind-the-Scenes (BTS):** Covert or unofficial actions occurring away from the "front".

**Common Compounds (Prefix/Initial Focus):** 

| Term | Gloss | Meaning | 
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **裏面** | _Lǐmiàn_ | The reverse side; the back of a 2D object. |
| **裏衣** | _Lǐyī_ | Undergarment; the lining of a robe. |
| **裏話** | _Lǐhuà_ | Inside story; the "true" version of events (BTS). |
| **裏通** | _Lǐtōng_ | Back-alley; secret communications or collusion. |
| **裏表** | _Lǐbiǎo_ | Two-faced; the relationship between front and back. |
