## Notes
- [SKIP-4-2-4](lookup/SKIP/SKIP-4/SKIP-4-2-4.md) ([Stroke 02](lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2002.md)) - [˙ㄚ˙](syllables/˙ㄚ˙.md)
- [List of 指事](lookup/List%20of%20指事.md) - a tool similar to a knife (Li Xueqin, 2012), perhaps a sickle; see also 辛. In some other versions both on oracle bones and bronzes, it looks like a huge pair of scissors used to cut weed. The original meaning of "mow, cut weed; sickle" is preserved in 刈. Unrelated to 爻. The sense "regulate, govern, orderly" is derived from "to mow, cut, reap"; this sense's origin is ambiguous, possibly from "to cut" > "to trim", etc. (Schuessler, 2007). If so, see 刈 (yì) for etymology.
