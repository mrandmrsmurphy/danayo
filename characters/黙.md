## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*mlɯːɡ): semantic [[Radical 094|犬]] ("dog") + phonetic [[Radical 203|黒]] (OC \*hmlɯːɡ, "black") — a dog keeping silent watch in the dark; silent, quiet, dark. In this Japanese shinjitai form, 黒 is further split at the bottom into 里 and 灬 purely for stroke simplification, not a change in composition.
