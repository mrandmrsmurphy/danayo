## Notes
- 会意 of a hand managing a tangled skein of thread (a simplified form of 𤔔, "hands working a spool") and [[Radical 005|乙]] ("hook, tool") — originally the methodical untangling of thread, hence both "to put in order" and, ironically, "disorder, chaos."
