## Notes
- for falsification prevention, use [[拾]]
- [List of 指事](lookup/List%20of%20指事.md) : Unlike 一, which was one horizontal stroke meaning one, 十 was originally simply a vertical stroke. In later forms 一 was added, forming a cross.  Possibly a pictogram (象形) for a needle, later written as 針 (OC \*kjum, \*kjums), borrowed phonetically for 十 (OC \*ɡjub, “ten”).
