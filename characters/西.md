## Notes
- [List of 象形](lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): a bag or basket, borrowed for phonetic value. Compare 東 (OC *toːŋ, "bundle > east"), 束 and 鹵. Traditionally explained as a pictogram of a bird settling into its nest, which by analogy with the setting of the sun means "west". This etymology has been disputed (Sears), but Sagart (2004) stands by it.
- [SKIP-4-6-1](lookup/SKIP/SKIP-4/SKIP-4-6-1.md) ([Stroke 06](lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2006.md))
- 143rd most used character in Classical Chinese. Ancient [[Lookup/CC/initials/聲 心|s]] + [[Lookup/CC/finals/韻 齊開|ei]] → [ㄙㄝㄧ](syllables/ㄙㄝㄧ.md)
- [Grade 1](lookup/Grade%201.md), [HSK Beginner](lookup/HSK/HSK%20Beginner.md), [Jōyō - Kyōiku](lookup/Japanese/Jōyō%20-%20Kyōiku.md), [Korean MS](lookup/Korean/Korean%20MS.md)
- 西 also serves as this vault's periodic-table abbreviation for seaborgium (Sg), functioning the way a chemical symbol letter does in a compound formula — see [[西博金]].
