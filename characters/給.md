## Notes

- 形声 (OC \*krub): semantic [[Radical 120|糸]] ("") + phonetic [[合]] (OC \*kuːb, \*ɡuːb) — "rich, abundant", perhaps with a reference to precious silk; today it also means "to give". The phonetic component means "to join, to unite", however it is likely unrelated to the original meaning.
