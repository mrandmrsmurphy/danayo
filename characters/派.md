## Notes
- 形声, OC \*m̥ʰreːɡs): semantic [[Radical 085|水]] (“water; river”) + phonetic 𠂢. 
- 𠂢 is the original pictogram for this character; 水 was added later to differentiate.  We do not retain the original character (no one does).  Off all the descendants made with it (哌, 挀, 派, 㭛, 眽, 䖰, 衇, 䤨, 覛), we retain only one: [脈](characters/脈.md)
