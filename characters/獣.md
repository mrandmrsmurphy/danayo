## Notes
- 会意 (OC *qʰljus): [[単|單]] ("hunting weapon") + [[Radical 094|犬]] ("dog") — "hunting"; the [[Radical 030|口]] elements were added later. The meaning shifted from the activity to its object — what is hunted (an exopassive of 狩 "to hunt") — and extends figuratively to a brutish, beastly person.
