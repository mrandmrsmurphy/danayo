## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*pɯɡ): semantic [虫](Radical%20142) + phonetic [畐](characters/畐.md) (OC \*pʰrɯɡ, \*bɯɡ). An extinct pictogram (象形) of a bat occurs in the oracle bone and bronze scripts; it is thought to write this word but is not ancestral to the character 蝠.
