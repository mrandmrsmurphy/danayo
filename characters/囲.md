## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*ɢʷɯls): semantic [[Radical 031|囗]] ("enclosure") + phonetic [[韋]] (OC \*ɢʷɯl) — 韋 itself originally meant "to surround" (later phonetically borrowed for "tanned leather"), with 囗 added to disambiguate the surrounding-enclosure sense.
