## Notes
- 会意 of [[Radical 053|广]] ("shelter; lean-to roof") + [[林]] ("two stalks; a stand of plants") — hemp stalks hung to dry under a shelter. Written with 广 + 林 following shinjitai/mainland convention; the traditional form uses 广 + 𣏟 (two hemp-stalk radicals), but stroke count and SKIP are identical across both forms.
- [SKIP-3-3-8](../lookup/SKIP/SKIP-3/SKIP-3-3-8.md) ([Stroke 11](../lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2011.md))
- 1328th most used character in Classical Chinese. Ancient [[Lookup/CC/initials/聲 明|m]] + [[Lookup/CC/finals/韻 麻二開|ɣa]] → [ㄇㄚ](../syllables/ㄇㄚ.md)
- [Grade 6](../lookup/Grade%206.md), [Old HSK 4](../lookup/HSK/Old%20HSK%204.md), [Jōyō - Kōtō](../lookup/Japanese/Jōyō%20-%20Kōtō.md), [Korean HS](../lookup/Korean/Korean%20HS.md)
