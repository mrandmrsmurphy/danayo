## Notes

- 形声 (OC \*ljɯs): semantic [[𡳿]] ("to go") + phonetic [[又]] (OC \*tjɯ) — to grasp, to hold. Phonetic 𡳿 (之), which represents a footprint pointing up, became 土 or 士 in the clerical script from the late Western Han to the Eastern Han, and semantic 又 became 寸 in Small Seal Script. The derivative 持 (OC *l'ɯ) refers to the original word.
