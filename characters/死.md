## Notes
- 会意 of [[Radical 078|歹]] ("death; remains of stripped bone") and 人 ("person," shown kneeling or bowing) — a mourner beside the dead.
- [SKIP-4-6-1](lookup/SKIP/SKIP-4/SKIP-4-6-1.md) ([Stroke 06](lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2006.md))
- 74th most used character in Classical Chinese. Ancient [[Lookup/CC/initials/聲 心|s]] + [[Lookup/CC/finals/韻 脂B三開|ɣiɪ]] → [ㄙㄧㄜ](syllables/ㄙㄧㄜ.md)
- [Grade 1](lookup/Grade%201.md), [HSK Beginner](lookup/HSK/HSK%20Beginner.md), [Jōyō - Kyōiku](lookup/Japanese/Jōyō%20-%20Kyōiku.md), [Korean MS](lookup/Korean/Korean%20MS.md)
- In Classical Chinese, this could take a direct object of "whom one did/would die for". This is not allowed in Dan'a'yo, and must take the postposition 于.
