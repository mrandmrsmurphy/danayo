## Notes
- 会意 of [[Radical 026|卩]] ("kneeling person") and [[Radical 160|辛]] ("tool") — originally [[Radical 026|卩]] + 䇂 ("sickle-like tool," corrupted to 辛 by the bronze-script period), depicting the administration of punishment by a ruler; extended senses include "law," "ruler, monarch," and (this vault's primary sense, matching alias 闢) "to open up, dispel."
- [SKIP-1-6-7](../lookup/SKIP/SKIP-1/SKIP-1-6-7.md) ([Stroke 13](../lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2013.md))
