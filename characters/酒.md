## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*ʔsluʔ): semantic [[Radical 085|水]] ("water") + phonetic [[酉]] (OC \*luʔ) — 酉 was the original pictogram for "wine" (today reused as the 10th earthly branch); 水 was added later to distinguish the alcohol sense.
