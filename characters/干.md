## Notes
- [List of 象形](../lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): a shield, feather ornaments visible on top in some oracle-bone forms (an alternate theory holds it depicts a bifurcated hunting weapon, a variant of 單). 干's own true native identity is "shield" — distinct from [[characters/乾 (char)|乾]] ("heavenly/dry," pure yang and its absence-of-moisture extension) and [[幹]] ("trunk/core"), both of which only borrow 干's glyph as a Simplified Chinese stand-in and were disentangled from it 2026-07-17.
