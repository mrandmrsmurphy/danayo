## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*zli): semantic [瓦](../lookup/Radicals/Radical%20098.md) ("tile, earthenware") + phonetic [[次]] ("order; sequence").
- [SKIP-2-6-5](../lookup/SKIP/SKIP-2/SKIP-2-6-5.md) ([Stroke 11](../lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2011.md))
- Ancient [[Lookup/CC/initials/聲 從|d͡z]] + [[Lookup/CC/finals/韻 脂A三開|iɪ]] → [ㄑㄧ](../syllables/ㄑㄧ.md). Note: this is a deliberate exception, not a mechanical derivation — the same MC initial+final as [[自]]/[[茨]] would place 瓷 at [ㄐㄧㄜ](../syllables/ㄐㄧㄜ.md) instead. Placed at ㄑㄧ specifically to keep "porcelain" auditorily distinct from [[磁]] ("magnetism," ㄐㄧ) per 毎字明意 — real Japanese/Korean orthography conflates the two (磁器/자기) via a historical semantic extension from the Cizhou 磁州 kilns, but Dan'a'yo deliberately keeps them apart.
