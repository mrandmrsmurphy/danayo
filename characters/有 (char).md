## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*ɢʷɯʔ): semantic [[Radical 074|月]] ("meat," an old form of 肉) + phonetic 又 ("hand; again," OC \*ɢʷɯs) — grasping meat in the hand, extended to mean "to have, to possess."
- [SKIP-3-2-4](lookup/SKIP/SKIP-3/SKIP-3-2-4.md) ([Stroke 06](lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2006.md))
