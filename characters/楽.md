## Notes
- [List of 会意](../lookup/List%20of%20会意.md)): [絲](糸.md) (“string”) + [[Radical 075|木]] (“wood”) – strings on a piece of wood, i.e. a wooden musical instrument.  The oracle bone form shows two strings connected to the wood. In the bronze inscriptions, a 白 (OC \*braːɡ) component was added in between the two strings, which may either represent the thumb (fiddling) or a phonetic part.
