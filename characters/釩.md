## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*pʰomʔ): semantic [金](Radical%20167) (“metal”) + phonetic [凡](characters/凡.md) (OC \*bom).
- Originally some kind of metal vessel or cup, now taken over for Vanadium
- Pronunciation shamelessly swapped places with [[礬]] (alum).  This character is meant to resemble international "**van**adium".
- It is encouraged to spell this word ㄪㄚㄋ when possible.
