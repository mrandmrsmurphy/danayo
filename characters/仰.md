## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*ŋaŋʔ, \*ŋaŋs): semantic [[Radical 009|亻]] ("person") + phonetic [[卯|卬]] (OC \*ŋaːŋ, \*ŋaŋʔ) — 卬 itself means "to raise the head, look up," the sense retained once 亻 was added for clarity. It has no character page of its own in this vault; [[卯]] carries it as an alias purely on the strength of an unrelated, obscure "variant form" sense (Wiktionary's Etymology 4 for 卬), not because 卯 and 仰 share any meaning here.
