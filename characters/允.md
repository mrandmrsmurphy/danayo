## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*lunʔ): semantic [[Radical 010|儿]] ("person") + phonetic [[以 (char)|㠯]] (OC \*ljɯʔ) — this is Shuowen Jiezi's traditional analysis, kept as the vault's classification per established precedent (cf. [[厠]]); modern paleography instead reads 允 as 象形, a person with hands held behind the back (⿱厶儿), possibly the original character for 逡巡 ("to hesitate, hover").
