## Notes
- 形声 (OC *t͡saːns): semantic [[Radical 154|貝]] ("money, valuables") + phonetic [[兟]] (OC *srin) — to support someone with money; "help, support, assist."
- [SKIP-2-8-7](lookup/SKIP/SKIP-2/SKIP-2-8-7.md) ([Stroke 15](lookup/Stroke/Stroke%2015.md))
- 1316th most used character in Classical Chinese. Ancient [[Lookup/CC/initials/聲 精|t͡s]] + [[Lookup/CC/finals/韻 寒|ɑn]] → [ㄐㄚㄋ](syllables/ㄐㄚㄋ.md)
- [Grade 4](lookup/Grade%204.md), [HSK No](lookup/HSK/HSK%20No.md), [Jōyō - Kyōiku](lookup/Japanese/Jōyō%20-%20Kyōiku.md) (5), [Korean HS](lookup/Korean/Korean%20HS.md)
- Originally written 賛, an ideogrammic compound (会意) of 㚘 and 貝 ("money") — to help with money.
