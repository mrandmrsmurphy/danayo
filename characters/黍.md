## Notes
- [List of 象形](lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): . A cereal 禾 with water 水 or kernels under it. The water may symbolize the wine made from millet or the fact that millet is sticky and water-resistant; otherwise, given that millet is a resistant plant that can grow on dry ground, the "water" component remains unexplained.
