## Notes
- 形声 (OC \*sn̥ʰiːn): semantic [[Radical 001|一]] ("one, numeral marker") + phonetic [[Radical 009|人]] (OC \*njin, "person") — the Old Chinese pronunciations of 千 and 人 were close; an extra stroke was added through 人 to distinguish the numeral, and the phonetic role survives into 年's own derived form. (An earlier version of this bullet wrongly named 遷 as the phonetic component — 遷/迁 only shares this same broad phonetic neighborhood, it isn't 千's actual derivation; corrected 2026-07-17 per Wiktionary.)
