## To coin or decide

Color concepts with no existing Dan'a'yo word:

- **sky blue / light blue** — Japanese has 水色 (みずいろ, "water color"); [[水色]] is the natural choice
- **maroon / chestnut** — Japanese/Chinese 栗色 (kuri-iro, "chestnut color"); [[栗色]] is natural and transparent
- **beige / cream** — no clean CJKV single-word equivalent; options:
	- [[乳色]] ("milk color") — simple and cross-linguistically transparent
	- [[象牙色]] ("ivory color") — more formal, three characters
	- Recommendation: [[乳色]]
- **lavender** — Japanese 薄紫/淡紫; [[淡紫]] ("pale purple") is the simplest Dan'a'yo option
