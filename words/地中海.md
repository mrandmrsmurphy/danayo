## Notes
- [地 (char)](../characters/地%20(char).md) "land" + [中 (char)](../characters/中%20(char).md) "middle" + [海](../characters/海.md) "sea" — the Mediterranean Sea, a calque of Latin *Mare Mediterraneum* ("sea in the middle of the land"), first documented in Matteo Ricci's 1602 world map (坤輿萬國全圖).
