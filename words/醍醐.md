## Notes
- [醍](../characters/醍.md) "essential oil" + [醐](../characters/醐.md) "purest cream"
- This word is the viable form of [醍](../characters/醍.md): the character's meaning (essential oil, clarified butter) is identical, but 醍 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 醍醐 is its `stand_in` compound.
