## Notes
- [蘭](../characters/蘭.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Seventh month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Wild orchids (蘭) bloom in late summer with a refined, subtle fragrance. Confucius compared the orchid to the virtuous person who cultivates excellence in obscurity. The Qixi Festival (七夕) falls this month.
