## Notes
- [菊 (char)](../characters/菊%20(char).md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Ninth month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Chrysanthemums (菊花) are the classic flower of autumn — they bloom after all others have faded, symbolizing resilience and integrity. The Double Ninth Festival (重陽節, 9th day of the 9th month) is when people wear chrysanthemums and drink chrysanthemum wine.
