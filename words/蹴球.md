## Notes
- [蹴](characters/蹴%20(char).md) "kick" + [球](characters/球%20(char).md) "ball"
There is a similar division to Asia as exists in the West, between terms like 'soccer' and 'football'. J/K use 蹴球 (lt. kick ball) while C uses 'foot ball'/足球. All these terms should be considered as ambiguous as English "football" in an international context, and should probably be followed by ㄙㄚㄎㄎㄚ_sakka_ or ㄆㄨㄊㄅㄛㄌㄜ_putbolü_.
