## Notes
- [浴](../characters/浴.md) "bathe" + [槽](../characters/槽.md) "vat, trough" — bathtub, bath. Mandarin and Cantonese use a different word (浴缸) for this referent, but Korean independently attests 욕조 as the genuine hanja reading of these exact characters, clearing "more than Japanese pedigree" on its own.
