## Notes
- [元](../characters/元.md) "first, origin" + [年 (char)](../characters/年%20(char).md) "year"
- The inaugural year of an imperial reign or calendar era. Used especially in Japanese historiography to mark the first year of an emperor's era name (元号), e.g. 令和元年 (Reiwa 1, 2019).
