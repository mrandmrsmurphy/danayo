## Notes
- [柏 (char)](../characters/柏%20(char).md) + [克 (char)](../characters/克%20(char).md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Berkeley*, after the University of California, Berkeley, where the element was discovered. 柏克 renders the first two syllables of 柏克萊, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Berkeley," truncated rather than spelled out in full. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (バークリウム, 버클륨).
