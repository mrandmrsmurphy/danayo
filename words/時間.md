## Notes
- [時 (char)](../characters/時%20(char).md) "when" + [間](../characters/間.md) "between, among" — literally "the space/interval of time." Neither character's own `stand_in` points here (時's own points to bare `時`, 間's own to `之間`); 時間 is an independent compound. `kwin: true` per the AND-rule: both constituent characters are individually `kwin: true`.
- **Corrected a severe wrong-word conflation**: `mandarin`/`cantonese` (`xiǎoshí`/`siu2 si4`) held the reading of an entirely different word, 小時/小时 ("an hour," a duration unit — 小 "small" + 時), not 時間 itself ("time," the general abstract concept) — corrected to `shíjiān`/`si4 gaan1`, matching `characters/間.md`'s own stored `jiān`/`gaan1`. The `aliases` field had also wrongly listed `小時`/`小时`/`鐘頭`/`钟头` as if they were alternate spellings of 時間 — these are genuinely different words (both meaning "an hour," not "time" in general) with no dedicated word page of their own yet, not orthographic variants — removed, keeping only the real simplified variant `时间`.
- A clean, directly parallel compound in meaning across the sphere once corrected — Mandarin shíjiān, Cantonese si4 gaan1, Japanese じかん, Korean 시간, and Vietnamese thời gian all mean "time" as a general concept.
