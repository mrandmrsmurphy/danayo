## Notes
- [俸](../characters/俸.md) "salary" + [祿](../characters/祿.md) "wealth; official emolument"
- This word is the viable form of [祿](../characters/祿.md): the character's meaning (official salary) is identical, but 祿 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 俸祿 is its `stand_in` compound.
