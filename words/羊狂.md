## Notes
- [зҫҠ](../characters/зҫҠ.md) (stand-in for дҪҜ, "feign") + [зӢӮ](../characters/зӢӮ.md) "mad" вҖ” to feign madness; pretend to be insane. A classical Chinese term describing a historical survival strategy used by officials and scholars to avoid political danger. Attested in Mandarin; other languages not yet confirmed.
