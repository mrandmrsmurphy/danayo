## Notes
- [荷](../characters/荷.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Sixth month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. The lotus (荷花) blooms at the height of summer, rising pure and fragrant from muddy water. In Buddhist and Daoist imagery the lotus represents purity amid impurity — an apt symbol for the sweltering heart of summer.
