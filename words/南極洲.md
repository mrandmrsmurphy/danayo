## Notes
- [南](../characters/南.md) "south" + [極 (char)](../characters/極%20(char).md) "pole, extreme" + [洲 (char)](../characters/洲%20(char).md) "continent" — Antarctica, the continent surrounding the South Pole. Standard in Mandarin and Vietnamese, though Vietnamese reverses the word order (Châu Nam Cực, "continent of the South Pole," classifier-first). Japanese and Korean instead say 南極大陸/남극 대륙 ("Antarctic continent"), so no 日本語/한국어 reading is given here.
