## Notes
- [詞](../characters/詞.md) "word, part of speech" + [彙 (char)](../characters/彙%20(char).md) "category, collection" — the total stock of words in a language. Attested in Mandarin (詞彙/词汇) and Vietnamese (từ vựng, using these same characters); Japanese and Korean instead favor 語彙 (語 rather than 詞) for this concept, so those readings are omitted here.
