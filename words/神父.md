## Notes
- [神 (char)](../characters/神%20(char).md) "god, spirit" + [父](../characters/父.md) "father"
- The Eastern Orthodox (and also Catholic) title for a priest, addressed as "Father." Distinct from [[司鈬]] (Catholic ordained priest) and [[牧師]] (Protestant pastor).
