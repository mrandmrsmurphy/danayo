## Notes
- [途](../characters/途.md) "en route" + [中 (char)](../characters/中%20(char).md) "-ing; midst"
- 途中 is the stand-in word for [途](../characters/途.md): the character's meaning (en route; on the way) is identical, but 途 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 途中 is its `stand_in` compound.
