## Notes
- [臭 (char)](../characters/臭%20(char).md) "stinky" + [膣 (char)](../characters/膣%20(char).md) (vulgar slang for vagina) — "bitch." A Dan'a'yo-original coinage: curse words are notoriously hard to pin down internationally, and 膣 is already in that category by itself (the non-vulgar anatomical term is [[陰道]]), so without neologism Dan'a'yo would have no curse words at all. The mandarin/cantonese/japanese/korean/vietnamese fields above give only the mechanical reading of the characters in each system, not a claim of real-world usage — except that this exact compound does genuinely exist as profanity in Singapore Teochew.
