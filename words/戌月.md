## Notes
- [戌](../characters/戌.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Ninth month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the eleventh Earthly Branch 戌 (dog).

Late autumn. The Double Ninth Festival (重陽節) falls here, when chrysanthemums are in full bloom and people climb hills to look back on the year. The world turns golden and begins to rest.
