## Notes
**Form.** 孫金 is [孫](characters/孫.md) + [金 (char)](characters/金%20(char).md) “metal,” within lanthanide naming.

**Added motivation.** The choice of 孫 is not primarily genealogical. It is **onomastic and cultural**:

- [[孫子]] (Sunzi)
- [[孫悟空]] (Sun Wukong)

So 孫 functions as a **name-bearing graph** associated with figures of **cunning, ingenuity, and boundary-crossing action**.

**Connection to Prometheus.** Prometheus is not just “a transmitter” but a **trickster-benefactor**:

- steals fire from the gods
- uses intelligence and cunning
- benefits humanity through transgression

This aligns more closely with:

- 孫子 → strategic cunning, intelligence
- 孫悟空 → rebellious, boundary-breaking, clever acquisition of power

So the mapping becomes:

- Prometheus → clever transgressor who brings power to humans
- archetype → trickster/strategist
- graph → 孫 (via culturally loaded exemplars)

**Type of formation.**  
A **cultural–mythological analogy via name association**:

- not a direct calque
- not purely phonetic
- but a **symbolic alignment of archetypes across traditions**

**Fit within the system.**

1. Provides a unique, simple symbol.
2. Avoids overused domains (fire, light, creation).
3. Extends the system into **cross-cultural mythic equivalence**, which we have already used (護金, 穀金).

**Comparative CJKV forms.**

- Modern Chinese: 钷 (phonetic)
- Japanese: プロメチウム (phonetic)
- Korean: 프로메튬 (phonetic)
- Vietnamese: prometi (phonetic)

Modern forms are phonetic; 孫金 is a semantic reinterpretation.

**Clarity and issues.**

- The mapping is highly **non-transparent without explanation**.
- It depends on recognizing the cultural weight of 孫 in specific names, not on the standalone meaning “grandchild.”
- This makes it one of the most **interpretive and least recoverable** names.

**Assessment.**  
With this explanation, the choice is coherent: we are encoding **Prometheus-as-trickster/intellectual rebel**, not Prometheus-as-fire. That is a defensible reading.

- **mythological analogy via culturally salient names**

**Conclusion.**  
孫金 is internally consistent once the intended association (Sunzi / Sun Wukong) is understood. It is a valid but high-context choice, relying on cultural analogy rather than direct semantic reconstruction.
