## Notes
- [転](../characters/転.md) "change, turn" + [化 (char)](../characters/化%20(char).md) "-ification, change"
- This word is the viable form of [転](../characters/転.md): the character's meaning (to change; to transform) is identical, but 転 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 転化 is its `stand_in` compound.
