## Notes
- [怪](../characters/怪.md) "strange" + [異](../characters/異.md) "different"
- This word is the viable form of [怪](../characters/怪.md): the character's meaning (strange, suspicious) is identical, but 怪 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 怪異 is its `stand_in` compound.
