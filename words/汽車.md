## Notes
- [汽](../characters/汽.md) "vapor, steam" + [車 (char)](../characters/車%20(char).md) "cart, car" — neither character's own `stand_in` points here (汽's own points to `蒸汽`, 車's own to bare `車`); 汽車 is an independent compound. `kwin: false` per the AND-rule: both `characters/汽.md` and `characters/車 (char).md` are individually `kwin: false`.
- **A deliberate Dan'a'yo sense choice, not a mistake — the same pattern as [[汽水]]**: this compound is dedicated here to Japanese 汽車 (kisha)'s literal, older sense of "steam train, locomotive," with general "train" instead routed to [[列車]] and "automobile" to [[自動車]] (both already distinct entries on `characters/車 (char).md`). Mandarin 汽车/汽車 qìchē, however, has grammaticalized into the everyday standard word for "car, automobile" — an entirely different, unrelated sense from "steam train" (Mandarin's own word for the latter is 火车/火車, [[火車]]). This vault deliberately keeps 汽車 narrow (matching Japanese) rather than importing Mandarin's "automobile" sense, to avoid collision with 自動車.
