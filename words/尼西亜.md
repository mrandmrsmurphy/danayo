## Notes
- [尼](../characters/尼.md) + [西](../characters/西.md) + [亜](../characters/亜.md) — phonetic transcription of *Nicaea*, the ancient city in Bithynia (modern İznik, Turkey).
- Site of the First Council of Nicaea (325 AD), which produced the 尼西亜信経.
