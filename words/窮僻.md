## Notes
- [窮](../characters/窮.md) "poverty" + [僻](../characters/僻.md) "remote"
- This word is the viable form of [僻](../characters/僻.md): the character's meaning (remote, out-of-the-way) is identical, but 僻 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 窮僻 is its `stand_in` compound.
