## Notes
- [西](../characters/西.md) "west" + [蔵](../characters/蔵.md) "hide, store"

西蔵 is the Dan'a'yo name for **Tibet**, built the same way as Mandarin 西藏 (Xīzàng) and Japanese 西蔵 (Seizō) — literally "western storehouse/treasury," reflecting an old exonym for the region west of China. 蔵/藏 is a graphic variant pair (蔵 is the Japanese shinjitai form of 藏); both spellings are listed as aliases since either may be encountered.
