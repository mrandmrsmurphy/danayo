## Notes
- [気 (char)](../characters/気%20(char).md) "feeling, energy" + [候](../characters/候.md) "climate, expectation"
- This word is the viable form of [候](../characters/候.md): the character's meaning (climate) is identical, but 候 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 気候 is its `stand_in` compound.
