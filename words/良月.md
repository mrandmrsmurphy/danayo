## Notes
- [良](../characters/良.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Tenth month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Unlike the other eleven months, 良 (good, fine, kind) is not a plant name. The harvest is complete, the granaries are full, the world is at rest — simply a "fine" month. It is also called 小陽春 ("little spring of yang"), a rare warm spell that can return in early winter.  Some traditions call it [[露月]] "dew month"
