## Notes
- [暖 (char)](../characters/暖%20(char).md) "warm" + [簾](../characters/簾.md) "curtain"
- This word is the viable form of [簾](../characters/簾.md): the character's meaning (screen, curtain) is identical, but 簾 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 暖簾 is its `stand_in` compound.
