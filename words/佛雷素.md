## Notes
- [佛 (char)](../characters/佛%20(char).md) + [雷 (char)](../characters/雷%20(char).md) + [素](../characters/素.md) "element" — a phonetic transliteration of *Flerov*, after the Flerov Laboratory of Nuclear Reactions (itself named for physicist Georgy Flyorov), truncated to two syllables to keep the name compact rather than spelling out the full transliteration the way Mandarin's borrowed-character system would. Japanese and Korean instead simply borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (フレロビウム, 플레로븀).
