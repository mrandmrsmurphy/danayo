## Notes
- [環 (char)](../characters/環%20(char).md) "ring" + [状](../characters/状.md) "shape, form" — ring-shaped, annular, or concentric, as in 環状構造 ("concentric structure") or 環状道路 ("ring road"). The Korean reading 환상 is a homophone of, but unrelated in hanja to, 幻想 ("fantasy, illusion").
