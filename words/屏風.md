## Notes
- [屏](../characters/屏.md) "folding screen" + [風 (char)](../characters/風%20(char).md) "wind"
- This word is the viable form of [屏](../characters/屏.md): the character's meaning (folding screen) is identical, but 屏 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 屏風 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese reading びょうぶ is a fused reading, not the concatenation of component pronunciations.
