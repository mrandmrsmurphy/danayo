## Notes
- [膳](../characters/膳.md) "meal" + [食 (char)](../characters/食%20(char).md) "eat"
- This word is the viable form of [膳](../characters/膳.md): the character's meaning (meal) is identical, but 膳 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs it with 食 ("to eat"), making the meaning explicit.
- Japanese omitted: 膳食 is not standard in Japanese (食事 is preferred).
