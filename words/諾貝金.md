## Notes
- [諾 (char)](../characters/諾%20(char).md) + [貝](../characters/貝.md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Nobel*, after chemist and Nobel Institute founder Alfred Nobel, in whose institute the element was first claimed to be discovered. 諾貝 renders the first two syllables of 諾貝爾, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Nobel," truncated rather than spelled out in full. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (ノーベリウム, 노벨륨).
