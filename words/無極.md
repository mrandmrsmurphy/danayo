## Notes
- [無 (char)](../characters/無%20(char).md) "lack" + [極 (char)](../characters/極%20(char).md) "extreme"

In Daoist cosmology, 無極 ("without ultimate" or "limitless") denotes the primordial undifferentiated state that precedes even [[太極]] (the "Great Ultimate"). It represents pure potentiality before any polarity or differentiation arises. See also [[混沌]].
