## Notes
- [要](../characters/要.md) "important, need" + [求](../characters/求.md) "request"

要求 is the stand-in for [求](../characters/求.md), which is too bounded to appear alone in Dan'a'yo discourse. As discussed at [[要請]], 要求 is the forceful counterpart to that word: 求 "to seek, pursue" gives 要求 the sense of a **demand or claim** — a personal or unilateral assertion of what one is owed or wants done — as opposed to 要請's formal, polite appeal.
