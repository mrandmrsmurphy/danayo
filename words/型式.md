## Notes
- [型](../characters/型.md) "model, pattern" + [式](../characters/式.md) "style"
- This word is the viable form of [型](../characters/型.md): the character's meaning (model; type) is identical, but 型 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 型式 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese reading かたしき is a mixed kun+on reading (型=かた, 式=しき); the pure on-reading would be けいしき.
