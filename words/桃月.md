## Notes
- [桃](../characters/桃.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Third month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Peach blossoms (桃花) are one of the most beloved symbols in Chinese poetry — fragrant, fleeting, and brilliant pink. The famous Peach Blossom Spring (桃花源) of Tao Yuanming evokes this month's dreamlike quality.
