## Notes
- [燐](../characters/燐.md) "phosphorus" + [火 (char)](../characters/火%20(char).md) "fire" — will-o'-the-wisp, the eerie light caused by combusting phosphine from decomposing matter; the scientific-register counterpart to the folkloric [[鬼火]] ("ghost fire"). Attested across Mandarin and Cantonese (via the variant form 磷火, merged into 燐 in this vault).
