## Notes
- [[俊]] ("outstanding, talented") + [馬 (char)](../characters/馬%20(char).md) ("horse") — a fine, spirited horse; an excellent steed. The real-world word is 駿馬 (jùnmǎ/しゅんめ/tuấn mã), well-attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, and Vietnamese, but 駿 is not itself a Dan'a'yo character — it is aliased onto [[俊]], which shares its phonetic root and a closely related sense ("outstanding" applied to a person vs. to a horse). 俊馬 is the in-language respelling, using 俊 in 駿's place the same way 俊 already stands in for 駿 across the vault.
