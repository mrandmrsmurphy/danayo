## Notes
- [寿](../characters/寿.md) "long life" + [着](../characters/着%20(char).md) "wear" — the real-world spelling 躊躇 adds 足 ("foot") to both graphemes, but this is a 聯綿詞 (disyllabic binome): the whole word is indivisible, neither syllable carries independent meaning, and 足 functions only as a category marker (pacing/hesitation) shared across the pair, not as live semantic content. Since 躊 and 躇 never occur outside this one word, Dan'a'yo does not mint separate characters for them — it spells the word directly with the bare phonetic hosts 壽/著 (aliased onto [[寿]] and [[着 (char)|着]] respectively), and records 躊躇/踌躇 as the real-world alias.
