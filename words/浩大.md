## Notes
- [浩](../characters/浩.md) "vast, great" + [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "big"
- This word is the viable form of [浩](../characters/浩.md): the character's meaning (vast; great; abundant) is identical, but 浩 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 浩大 is its `stand_in` compound.
