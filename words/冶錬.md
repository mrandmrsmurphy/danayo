## Notes
- [冶](../characters/冶.md) "smelt, cast" + [錬](../characters/錬.md) "temper, refine" — a synonym compound generalizing over both components' more precise senses; no longer a `stand_in` for either, since [[鍛冶]] (smithing, casting apparatus) and [[鍛錬]] (temper, forge; train/discipline) now each carry the distinct nuance 冶錬 used to blur together — both, notably, via the same third character 鍛, which legitimately spans both senses on its own.
