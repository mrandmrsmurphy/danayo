## Notes
- [寛](../characters/寛.md) "lenient, wide, broad" + [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "big"
- This word is the viable form of [寛](../characters/寛.md): the character's meaning (lenient; magnanimous; broad) is identical, but 寛 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 寬大 is its `stand_in` compound.
