## Etymology
- [乾 (char)](../characters/乾%20(char).md) "dry" + [酪](../characters/酪.md) "cheese; dairy; fermented drink" — cheese, a dried/aged dairy product. Written 干酪 in Simplified Chinese; 乾 is the real character here, not 干 ("shield").
