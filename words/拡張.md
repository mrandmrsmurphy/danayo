## Notes
- [拡](../characters/拡.md) "expand, enlarge" + [張](../characters/張.md) "stretch, extend"

This word is the stand-in for [張](../characters/張.md), which is too bounded to appear alone in Dan'a'yo discourse; 拡張 carries the same sense of stretching or extending outward.

拡張 denotes expansion — the increase of scope, scale, territory, or capacity. The compound combines two characters both meaning "to stretch or extend," giving the compound an intensified sense of enlargement. In Chinese (擴張, kuòzhāng), the compound is used in medical contexts (血管擴張, vasodilation), political contexts (勢力擴張, expansion of influence), and everyday speech. In Japanese (かくちょう, kakuchō), the word is extremely productive in technical and computing vocabulary: 拡張子 (file extension), 拡張機能 (extended functionality), 拡張パック (expansion pack). The computing sense reflects the compound's underlying idea of stretching the original beyond its initial limits.
