## Notes
- [寂](../characters/寂.md) "silent, quiet, still" + [寞](../characters/寞.md) "lonely, desolate" — stand-in for [[寞]], which cannot appear independently. 寂 also appears here, but only lends its ordinary "quiet" sense; its own precise Dan'a'yo definition now lives in [[寂滅]] (the Buddhist "nirvana, cessation" register), leaving 寂寞 as plain, everyday "lonely, desolate."
