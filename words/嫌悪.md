## Notes
- [嫌](../characters/嫌.md) "detest, hate" + [悪 (char)](../characters/悪%20(char).md) "bad, evil"
- This word is the viable form of [嫌](../characters/嫌.md): the character's meaning (to detest; to loathe) is identical, but 嫌 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 嫌悪 is its `stand_in` compound.
- The 悪 here uses its secondary reading wù (to hate), not è (evil); hence the Mandarin xiánwù and Cantonese wu3 rather than the "bad/evil" readings.
