## Notes
- [窒](../characters/窒.md) "suffocate; choke" + [息](../characters/息.md) "breathe"
- 窒息 is the stand-in word for [窒](../characters/窒.md): the character's meaning (to suffocate; to choke) is identical, but 窒 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 窒息 is its `stand_in` compound.
