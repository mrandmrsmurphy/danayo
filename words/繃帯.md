## Notes
- [繃](../characters/繃.md) "bandage" + [帯 (char)](../characters/帯%20(char).md) "sash; belt"
- This word is the viable form of [繃](../characters/繃.md): the character's meaning (bandage) is identical, but 繃 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs it with 帯 (the thing a bandage physically is) to make it usable.
