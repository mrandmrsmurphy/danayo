## Notes
- [奥 (char)](../characters/奥%20(char).md) + [加](../characters/加.md) + [素](../characters/素.md) "element" — a transliteration of *Oganessian*, after physicist Yuri Oganessian. 奥加 renders the first two syllables of his name, truncated to keep the coinage compact rather than transliterating the name in full the way Mandarin's borrowed-character system would. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (オガネソン, 오가네손).
