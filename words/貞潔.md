## Notes
- [貞](../characters/貞.md) "chaste; loyal" + [潔](../characters/潔.md) "clean; pure"
- 貞潔 is the stand-in word for [貞](../characters/貞.md): the character's meaning (chaste; pure) is identical, but 貞 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 貞潔 is its `stand_in` compound.
