## Notes
- [逓](../characters/逓.md) "hand over" + [送 (char)](../characters/送%20(char).md) "give"
- This word is the viable form of [逓](../characters/逓.md): the character's meaning (to hand over, deliver) is identical, but 逓 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 逓送 is its `stand_in` compound.
