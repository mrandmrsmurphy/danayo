## Notes
- [会 (char)](../characters/会%20(char).md) "meet, gather" + [話 (char)](../characters/話%20(char).md) "speak on, talk about" — an independent compound; neither character's own `stand_in` points here (both are bare self-standing characters)

Mandarin huìhuà, Cantonese wui6 waa6-2, Japanese かいわ (kaiwa), Korean 회화 (hoehwa), and Vietnamese hội thoại are all the standard, everyday word for "conversation" across the sphere (会話文/회화문, "dialogue text/conversational sentences," a common language-learning-material term; 日常会話, "everyday conversation").
