## Notes
- [従 (char)](../characters/従%20(char).md) "follow; from" + [前 (char)](../characters/前%20(char).md) "before, preceding"

従前 means "formerly, as before, as previously" — describing what was the case before some change or reference point. It is a formal, literary adverb. In Chinese (从前, cóngqián), the simplified form carries both the formal sense of "previously" and the nostalgic sense of "once upon a time" — appearing in classical story openings (从前有一个人... "Once there was a person...") and in everyday speech.

In Korean (종전), the word is an exact homophone of 終戦 (종전, "end of war"), a historically charged term in Korean memory referring to the liberation of Korea in 1945. The 従前 meaning (formerly) and the 終戰 meaning (armistice, end of hostilities) are distinguished only by context and character.
