## Notes
- [杜](../characters/杜.md) "careless; fabricating" + 撰 (MC 崇母 + 仙韻 → ㄐ⼶ㄋ; no independent vault entry) "to compose; write"
- This word is the viable form of [杜](../characters/杜.md): the character's sense of "fabricated, careless" is historically derived from this compound, which originally described the poorly composed writings of the Song-dynasty poet 杜默 (Dù Mò). 撰 ("to write; compose") licenses the compound.
- In Japanese, the compound survives as ずさん (*zuzan* → *zusan*), meaning "sloppy; slapdash."
