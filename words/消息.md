## Notes
- [消 (char)](../characters/消%20(char).md) "vanish, die out, melt away" + [息](../characters/息.md) "tell news, breathe" — neither character's own `stand_in` points here (消's own points to bare `消`, 息's own to `気息`); 消息 is an independent compound. `kwin: false` per the AND-rule: `characters/消 (char).md` is `kwin: false`, `characters/息.md` is `kwin: true` → compound `false`.
- **Content removed**: `mandarin` previously comma-jammed three readings (`xiāoxi, xiāoxī, xiāoxí`) — kept just `xiāoxi`, the standard, common neutral-tone reading; `xiāoxí` in particular does not match any attested pronunciation of 息 (`xī`).
- Mandarin xiāoxi, Cantonese siu1 sik1, Japanese しょうそく, and Korean 소식 all preserve the Sino compound directly as "news, tidings." Vietnamese tin tức is a native/hybrid construction rather than a direct Sino-Vietnamese calque, but is genuinely the correct, standard everyday term for "news."
