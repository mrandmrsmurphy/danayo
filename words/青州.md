## Notes
- [青 (char)](../characters/青%20(char).md) "blue-green" + [州 (char)](../characters/州%20(char).md) — Qing Province, the third of the [[九州]]

青州 occupied the eastern coastal region — roughly modern Shandong peninsula — where the eastern mountains meet the sea. The *Tribute of Yu* notes its proximity to the sea (海濱) and its characteristic soils and products. The name persisted through the imperial era as a regional designation; in the Three Kingdoms period, 青州 soldiers (青州兵) famously formed part of Cao Cao's force. The modern city of Qingzhou in Shandong preserves the ancient name.
