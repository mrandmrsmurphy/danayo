## Definition
**欧羅巴州** / **欧州** (Ōuluóbāzhōu / Ōuzhōu) _n._ The European continent as a geographic and political entity; Europe defined by its physical boundaries, nations, and territorial extent. The westernmost peninsula of the Eurasian landmass, conventionally bounded by the Atlantic Ocean, Arctic Ocean, Mediterranean Sea, and (variously defined) the Ural Mountains or other eastern limits. Emphasizes spatial, cartographic, and geopolitical dimensions.

_Usage_: Preferred for geographic, political, demographic, or administrative contexts. Example: "欧州聯盟" (European Union), "欧州地圖" (map of Europe).
