## Notes
- [隠](../characters/隠.md) "hide" + [匿](../characters/匿.md) "hide"
- This word is the viable form of [匿](../characters/匿.md): the character's meaning (to hide, conceal) is identical, but 匿 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 隠匿 is its `stand_in` compound.
