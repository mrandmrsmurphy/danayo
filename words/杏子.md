## Notes
- [杏](../characters/杏.md) "apricot" + [子](../characters/子.md) "thing" (nominalizer)
- 杏子 is the stand-in for [杏](../characters/杏.md), which is too bounded to stand alone.
- Japanese reading あんず is a fused form, not a concatenation of 杏 + 子.
