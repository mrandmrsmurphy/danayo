## Notes
- [正 (char)](../characters/正%20(char).md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
First month of the "year" (varies from country to country)

According to historical accounts like the Guangyun, the pronunciation actually changed because of a naming taboo.

The First Emperor of Qin, Qin Shi Huang, had the personal name Ying Zheng (嬴政).

Since his name used the character 政 (zhèng), which sounds identical to the standard pronunciation of 正, people were forbidden from saying it.

To avoid disrespecting the Emperor, the pronunciation for "the first month" was shifted from the 4th tone (zhèng) to the 1st tone (zhēng).
