## Notes
- [胴](../characters/胴.md) "torso" + [体](../characters/体.md) "body"
- This word is the viable form of [胴](../characters/胴.md): the character's meaning (trunk/torso) is identical, but 胴 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs a near-synonym 体 to make it usable.
- Extended to the body of vehicles: the fuselage of an aircraft, hull of a ship, etc.
