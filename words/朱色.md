## Notes
- [朱](../characters/朱.md) "cinnabar, vermillion" + [色](../characters/色.md) "color"

朱 is the color of cinnabar (朱砂, mercury sulfide), a vivid orange-red distinct from 紅 (crimson) and 赤 (general red). It is the color of traditional Chinese seal ink, the lacquerwork of imperial furniture and architecture, and the painted walls of palace complexes and temple gates. The Forbidden City's walls are 朱色; the Shinto torii gate is 朱色. The character 朱 itself depicts a tree trunk with a mark at the center — the red heartwood — giving it an etymological basis in material rather than abstraction.

This is the red of authority, ritual, and official sanction. Where 紅 is warm and celebratory, 朱 is formal and sacred.
