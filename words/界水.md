## Notes
- [界](../characters/界.md) "boundary" + [水 (char)](../characters/水%20(char).md) "water" — a body of water that marks or constitutes a boundary, rather than a measured ocean in its own right. Attested in Classical Chinese geomantic writing, e.g. 葬書's "氣乘風則散，界水則止" ("qi disperses on the wind, but stops at boundary waters"). Used of the mythological Four Seas ([[四海]]) of ancient Chinese cosmology, conceived as directional boundary waters rather than measured oceans.
