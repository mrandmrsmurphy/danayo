## Notes
- [鞀](../characters/鞀.md) "pellet drum" + [鼓 (char)](../characters/鼓%20(char).md) "drum"
- 鞀鼓 is the stand-in word for [鞀](../characters/鞀.md): the character's meaning (a pellet drum) is identical, but 鞀 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 鞀鼓 is its `stand_in` compound.
