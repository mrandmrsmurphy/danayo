## Notes
- [適](../characters/適.md) "suitable" + [応 (char)](../characters/応%20(char).md) "respond" — to respond by becoming suitable, i.e. to adapt or adjust to changed circumstances. Used both as a noun ("adaptation") and, with a light verbalizer, as a verb ("to adapt"), parallel to [[継承]].
