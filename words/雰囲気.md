## Notes
- [雰](../characters/雰.md) "atmosphere" + [囲](../characters/囲.md) "encircle" + [気 (char)](../characters/気%20(char).md) "feeling"
- This word is the viable form of [雰](../characters/雰.md): the character's meaning (atmosphere, ambience) is identical, but 雰 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 雰囲気 is its `stand_in` compound.
- A Japanese coinage; no unified Mandarin/Cantonese reading.
