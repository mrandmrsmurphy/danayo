## Notes
- [洗 (char)](../characters/洗%20(char).md) "to wash; to cleanse" + [礼 (char)](../characters/礼%20(char).md) "rite; ceremony; propriety" — the washing rite; the sacrament of initiation by water into the Christian community

洗礼 is the standard Sinosphere term for baptism, covering immersion, pouring (affusion), and sprinkling (aspersion) depending on the tradition. The compound directly reflects the Greek βάπτισμα (baptisma, "dipping/washing") and the Latin baptismus. The Korean 세례 (洗礼) and Japanese 洗礼 (せんれい) both follow the character reading.

In the Dan'a'yo [[基督敎]] lexipedia, 洗礼 is listed alongside [[聖餐]] under [[聖礼]] (sacraments) — the two rites recognized as dominical sacraments across most Protestant traditions (and two of the seven sacraments in Catholic and Orthodox theology). The Vietnamese tẩy lễ mirrors the compound: tẩy (洗 = to wash) + lễ (礼 = rite).
