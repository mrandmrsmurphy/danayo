## Notes
- [原](../characters/原.md) "origin, source" + [罪 (char)](../characters/罪%20(char).md) "sin, guilt"
- The Christian theological concept of the inherited sinful nature of humanity, tracing to the Fall. Entered East Asian languages through 17th-century missionary texts.
