## Notes
- [子](../characters/子.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Eleventh month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the first Earthly Branch 子 (rat).

Contains the winter solstice (冬至). 子 is the hour of midnight — the deepest darkness, which is also the moment the new yang energy is born. The rat's quickness and fertility signal that new life stirs even at the year's lowest point.
