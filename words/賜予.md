## Notes
- [賜](../characters/賜.md) "grant, bestow" + [予](../characters/予.md) "beforehand"

賜予 "to bestow, to grant" pairs near-synonymous verbs of giving — 賜 (formal, often used of a superior granting to an inferior, as in imperial gifts) and 予 (here used in its older sense "to give," distinct from its more common modern sense "beforehand"). The Japanese reading しよ corresponds to 賜与, a formal/administrative term for an official grant or allowance; 사여 is the parallel Sino-Korean term. 賜予 also serves as the **stand-in** for [[賜]], which is too bounded to appear alone in Dan'a'yo discourse.
