## Notes
- [酉](../characters/酉.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Eighth month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the tenth Earthly Branch 酉 (rooster).

Mid-autumn and harvest season. 酉 is the hour of 5–7 p.m. — the sun descending, the day's work complete — mirroring the harvest close of the growing year. The Mid-Autumn Festival (中秋節) falls in this month.
