## Notes
- [体](../characters/体.md) "group, body, form" + [育 (char)](../characters/育%20(char).md) "nurture, educate" — an independent compound; neither character's own `stand_in` points here (体's is `体系`, 育's is bare `育`)

Mandarin tǐyù, Cantonese tai2 juk6, Japanese たいいく (taiiku), Korean 체육 (cheyuk), and Vietnamese thể dục are all the standard word for "physical education" as a school subject (体育館/체육관, "gymnasium"; thể dục thể thao, "physical culture and sports," a set phrase). No native displacement or homophone collision found — a clean compound with directly parallel usage in all five languages.
