## Notes
- [合 (char)](../characters/合%20(char).md) "join, combine" + [成 (char)](../characters/成%20(char).md) "become, form" — to join parts together into a new whole: synthesis, compounding.

The word ranges from the chemical (合成繊維, "synthetic fiber," not yet created) to the linguistic: 合成詞 is the standard term for a compound word built from multiple characters, the direct counterpart to [[単字]] (a word that is just one character on its own). The grammar book uses this contrast directly when explaining [[重畳]] (reduplication): a 単字 reduplicates as a whole, while a character that only forms a word as part of a 合成詞 has just its main character reduplicated. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese.
