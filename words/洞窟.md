## Notes
- [洞](../characters/洞.md) "cave" + [窟 (char)](../characters/窟%20(char).md) "cavern"
- This word is the viable form of [洞](../characters/洞.md): the character's meaning (cave) is identical, but 洞 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs two near-synonyms for cave to make it usable.
