## Notes
- [閙](../characters/閙.md) "noisy; disturbance" + [事 (char)](../characters/事%20(char).md) "matter"
- This word is the viable form of [閙](../characters/閙.md): the character's meaning (noisy, to cause trouble) is identical, but 閙 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 閙事 is its `stand_in` compound.
