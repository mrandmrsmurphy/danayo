## Definition
_n._ 
1. The cultural, historical, and civilizational concept of Europe; Europa as understood through its classical heritage, mythology, and shared cultural traditions. Emphasizes the human and historical dimensions—the peoples, languages, arts, philosophies, and religious traditions that have developed on the European peninsula since antiquity.
2. The Phoenician princess Europa (Εὐρώπη) of Greek mythology.

_Usage_: Preferred when discussing European culture, civilization, history, or identity. Example: "欧羅巴_古典遺產" (Europa's classical heritage).

Distinct from [[欧洲]]
