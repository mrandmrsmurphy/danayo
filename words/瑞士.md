## Notes
- [瑞](../characters/瑞.md) + [士 (char)](../characters/士%20(char).md) — a Mandarin/Cantonese phonetic transcription of "Switzerland" (from French "Suisse"), first attested in an 1848–49 Chinese geographical text. Japanese and Korean instead use the katakana/hangul loan スイス/스위스 rather than this kanji/hanja form (Korean 서사 is attested but obsolete). Vietnamese Thuỵ Sĩ is the standard modern Vietnamese name, directly cognate with this compound.
