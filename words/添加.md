## Notes
- [添](../characters/添.md) "add to" + [加](../characters/加.md) "increase"
- This word is the viable form of [添](../characters/添.md): the character's meaning (to add to) is identical, but 添 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 添加 is its `stand_in` compound.
