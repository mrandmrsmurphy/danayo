## Notes
- [停](../characters/停.md) "halt, stop" + [止](../characters/止.md) "stop" — a synonym compound generalizing over both of its components' more precise senses; no longer a `stand_in` for either, since [[停留]] (staying in place) and [[中止]] (discontinuing an action) now each carry one of the two distinct nuances 停止 used to blur together.
