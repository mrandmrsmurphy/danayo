## Notes
- [面](../characters/面.md) "face" + [疱](../characters/疱.md) "acne"
- This word is the viable form of [疱](../characters/疱.md): the character's meaning (pustule; pimple) is identical, but 疱 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 面疱 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese uses にきび (nikibi), a native word for pimple/acne; the Sino-Japanese on-yomi reading めんぽう (menpō) also exists but is less common.
