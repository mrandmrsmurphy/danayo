## Notes
- [辰](../characters/辰.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Third month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the fifth Earthly Branch 辰 (dragon).

The height of spring. The dragon is the only mythological creature among the twelve branches, signaling the month's grandeur and the full arrival of warmth.
