## Notes
- [広 (char)](../characters/広%20(char).md) "broad, wide" + [土 (char)](../characters/土%20(char).md) "land, soil" — vast territory, extensive land. The first half of the Classical Chinese idiom 廣土眾民 ("vast territory and numerous people"), from *Mencius* (孟子, 盡心上): "廣土眾民，君子欲之，所樂不存焉" ("a wide territory and a numerous people are what the superior man desires, but what he delights in is not here"). Also independently attested as a standalone word in Japanese (広土, こうど).
