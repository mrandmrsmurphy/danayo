## Notes
- [八 (char)](../characters/八%20(char).md) "eight" + [千](../characters/千.md) "thousand"

八千 is eight thousand (8,000). In Japanese (はっせん, hassen), 八 undergoes 音便 (onbin) sound change: はち→はっ before せん, a regular pattern seen across number compounds (六千 → ろくせん, 八百 → はっぴゃく).
