## Notes
- [火 (char)](../characters/火%20(char).md) "fire" + [焰](../characters/焰.md) "flame"
- This word is the viable form of [焰](../characters/焰.md): the character's meaning (flame) is identical, but 焰 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 火 ("fire") as the head noun licenses the compound.
