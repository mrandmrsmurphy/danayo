## Notes
- From [[真 (char)|真]] "genuine/true" + [[実]] "substance/reality": the compound appears across all five source languages as the standard word for truth or reality (Japanese しんじつ, Korean 진실, Mandarin zhēnshí 真實, Vietnamese chân thật). The Dan'a'yo form uses the Japanese shinjitai 実 rather than the traditional 實.
- Stand-in for the bound character [[実]].
