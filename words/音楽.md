## Ancient
As one of the <ruby>[六芸](/words/六芸.md)<rt>ㄌㄨㄎ·ㄝ</rt></ruby>, there were clear divisions to this art:
- **雲門大卷 (_Yúnmén_):** From the era of the Yellow Emperor.
- **大咸 (_Dàxián_):** From the era of Emperor Yao.
- **大韶 (_Dàsháo_):** From the era of Emperor Shun (famously praised by Confucius).
- **大夏 (_Dàxià_):** From the era of Yu the Great.
- **大濩 (_Dàhuò_):** From the era of Tang of Shang.
- **大武 (_Dàwǔ_):** Celebrating the founding of the Zhou Dynasty by King Wu.
