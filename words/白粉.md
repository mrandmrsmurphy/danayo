## Notes
- [白](../characters/白%20(char).md) "white" + [粉](../characters/粉%20(char).md) "powder" — white cosmetic powder; by extension, any fine white powder or paint.

Mandarin, Cantonese, and Korean all share a real second sense that has nothing to do with cosmetics: 白粉/백분 is common drug-culture slang for heroin (alongside synonyms like 白麵兒), the powder's colour and texture giving it the same nickname across all three. Japanese はくふん never picked up that slang sense — Japanese drug slang for heroin instead reaches for シャブ, S, or plain katakana ヘロイン. Vietnamese bạch phấn also stays confined to the literal cosmetic sense; Vietnamese drug slang for heroin uses a different compound entirely, bạch phiến ("white flake/slice," 白片), not a cognate of 白粉 at all.
