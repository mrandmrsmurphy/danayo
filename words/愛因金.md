## Notes
- [愛 (char)](../characters/愛%20(char).md) + [因](../characters/因.md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Einstein*, after physicist Albert Einstein. 愛因 renders the first two syllables of 愛因斯坦, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Einstein," truncated rather than spelled out in full. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (アインスタイニウム, 아인슈타이늄).
