## Notes
- [玻](../characters/玻.md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Bohr*, after physicist Niels Bohr. 玻 is the standard Mandarin transliteration syllable for "Bohr" (as in 玻爾), used alone here rather than the fuller two-character form, in keeping with this system's preference for compact one-syllable eponyms where the source name itself is monosyllabic. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (ボーリウム, 보륨).
