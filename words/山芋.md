## Notes
- [山 (char)](../characters/山%20(char).md) "mountain" + [芋](../characters/芋.md) "taro"

The Japanese reading やまいも uses the native kun'yomi (mountain + potato) rather than the Sino-Japanese on'yomi サンウ. Refers to the yam *Dioscorea japonica* in Japan; in Chinese dialectal usage the referent varies (sweet potato, taro, or potato depending on region).
