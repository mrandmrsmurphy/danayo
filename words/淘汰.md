## Notes
- [淘](../characters/淘.md) "wash" + [汰](../characters/汰.md) "scour"
- This word is the viable form of [汰](../characters/汰.md): the character's meaning (to scour, eliminate) is identical, but 汰 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 淘汰 is its `stand_in` compound.
