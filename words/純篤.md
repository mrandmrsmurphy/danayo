## Notes
- [純](../characters/純.md) "pure" + [篤](../characters/篤.md) "sincere"
- This word is the viable form of [篤](../characters/篤.md): the character's meaning (sincere, earnest) is identical, but 篤 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 純篤 is its `stand_in` compound.
