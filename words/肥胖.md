## Notes
- [肥](../characters/肥.md) "plump" + [胖](../characters/胖.md) "fat"
- This word is the viable form of [胖](../characters/胖.md): the character's meaning (fat) is identical, but 胖 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs it with a near-synonym 肥 to make it usable.
- Japanese ひはん is the mechanical Sino-Japanese reading but not standard usage (and collides with 批判 "criticism" in Japanese — irrelevant to Dan'a'yo).
