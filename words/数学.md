## Ancient
Ancient arts (following _The Nine Chapters_) divided math into nine type:
- **方田 (_Fāngtián_):** Land surveying and area.
- **粟米 (_Sùmǐ_):** Exchange of grains and trade ratios.
- **差分 (_Chāfēn_):** Proportional distribution (taxation).
- **少廣 (_Shǎoguǎng_):** Square and cube roots.
- **商功 (_Shānggōng_):** Engineering (calculating volumes for walls/canals).
- **均輸 (_Jūnshū_):** Fair taxation based on distance and population.
- **盈不足 (_Yíngbùzú_):** "Excess and deficit" (linear equations).
- **方程 (_Fāngchéng_):** Simultaneous linear equations.
- **勾股 (_Gōugǔ_):** Right-angled triangles (Pythagorean theorem).
