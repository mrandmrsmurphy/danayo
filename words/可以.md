## Notes
- [可 (char)](../characters/可%20(char).md) "able to, may" + [以 (char)](../characters/以%20(char).md) "by means of, with" — Dan'a'yo's preposition for permission, distinct from the bare prefix [可](../characters/可%20(char).md) which marks ability/capacity. 可以 is one of the most common words in Mandarin, where it does the same double duty (能力 and 許可) that Dan'a'yo splits between the bare prefix and this preposition. Attested across Mandarin and Cantonese.
