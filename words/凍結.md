## Notes
- [凍](../characters/凍.md) "freeze" + [結 (char)](../characters/結%20(char).md) "tie; join" — stand-in for [[凍]], which cannot appear independently

`pos: 性詞` matches `characters/凍.md`'s own stored category — not a bug, unlike the `事詞` corrections applied to [[呼吸]]/[[戦闘]]/[[回転]]/[[落下]] earlier this sweep, where the constituent characters were clearly verb-like.

Mandarin dòngjié, Cantonese dung3 git3, Japanese とうけつ (tōketsu), Korean 동결 (donggyeol), and Vietnamese đông kết (verified via search) all cover both senses in parallel — the literal physical "freeze" (water into ice) and a shared figurative extension to "freeze" financial assets or a process (資産凍結/자산동결/đóng băng tài khoản, "asset freeze"), the same metaphor independently applied across the whole sphere.
