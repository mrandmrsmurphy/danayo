## Notes
- [辟](../characters/辟.md) (stand-in for 霹) + [歴](../characters/歴.md) (stand-in for 靂) — a thunderclap, thunderbolt. The full form 霹靂 is attested across all five languages, including the famous Japanese idiom 青天の霹靂 ("a bolt from the blue").
