## Notes
- [内](../characters/内.md) "inside, internal" + [乱](../characters/乱.md) "chaos, disorder" — internal disorder or rebellion within a state, as opposed to a war against an external power. A standard pan-Sinospheric pairing with war against an outside power in historical writing about the causes of political transformation, alongside foreign invasion and social reform.
