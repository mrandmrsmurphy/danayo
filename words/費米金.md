## Notes
- [費 (char)](../characters/費%20(char).md) + [米 (char)](../characters/米%20(char).md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Fermi*, after physicist Enrico Fermi. 費米 is the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Fermi" (as in 費米子, "fermion"), used here in full since it is already only two syllables. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (フェルミウム, 페르뮴).
