## Notes
- [楊](../characters/楊.md) "poplar, aspen" + [柳 (char)](../characters/柳%20(char).md) "willow"
- 楊柳 is the stand-in word for [楊](../characters/楊.md) ("poplar, aspen tree"), which is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo.
- The Japanese reading やなぎ (yanagi) is an irregular fused reading, not a concatenation of the component readings (YOU + RYUU).
