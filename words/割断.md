## Notes
- [割 (char)](../characters/割%20(char).md) "cut" + [断](../characters/断.md) "cut off"
- This word is the viable form of [断](../characters/断.md): the character's meaning (to cut off) is identical, but 断 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 割断 is its `stand_in` compound.
