## Notes
- [尼](../characters/尼.md) + [羅](../characters/羅.md) + [河](../characters/河.md) — a Mandarin/Cantonese phonetic transcription of "Nile," the longest river in Africa, flowing north through Khartoum and Cairo into the Mediterranean. Japanese and Korean instead call it ナイル川/나일강 (a hybrid of the English/French name "Nile" plus the native word for "river"), so no kanji/hanja-compositional reading is given for those columns.
