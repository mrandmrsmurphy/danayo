## Notes
- [小 (char)](../characters/小%20(char).md) "small" + [学](../characters/学.md) "learn" + [校](../characters/校.md) "school" — the standard Japanese term for an elementary school, built by appending 校 to [[小学]]. Mandarin does not use this longer form for the institution; it simply says 小学 on its own, so 小学校 should not be treated as a Mandarin synonym or alias of 小学 despite the obvious overlap in characters — the two are related but not interchangeable across languages.
