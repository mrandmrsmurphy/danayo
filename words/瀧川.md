## Notes
- [瀧](../characters/瀧.md) "waterfall, rapids" + [川 (char)](../characters/川%20(char).md) "river"
- 瀧川 is a Japanese place name and surname meaning "waterfall river" or "rapids river."
- Note: Japanese reads たきがわ (Takigawa) — a native compound where 瀧 (taki) means waterfall or rapids and 川 (gawa) is the native word for river. The Dan'a'yo reading ㄌㄛㄫㄑ⺢ㄋ derives from the Middle Chinese phonological values of each character and bears no resemblance to the Japanese.
