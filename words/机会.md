## Notes
- [机](../characters/机.md) "opportunity, occasion" + [会 (char)](../characters/会%20(char).md) "meet, gather" — a favorable juncture where things come together. This word is the viable form of [机](../characters/机.md): 机 (Dan'a'yo form of 機) alone is too bound to stand as a Dan'a'yo word, and 机会 is its `stand_in` compound, deliberately chosen to keep 机/機's sense the "critical juncture, opportunity" one — its original meaning was the trigger-mechanism of a crossbow, hence "pivotal moment" (cf. 枢机, 生机) — in contrast to [[机械]] (the physical "machine, mechanism" sense that [[械]] stands in for instead).

機's semantic range is genuinely wide: 機会/机会 (opportunity), 危機 (crisis, "dangerous juncture"), 好機 (good opportunity), 転機 (turning point) all draw on the "critical moment" sense, while 機械/机械 (machine) and 機関/机関 (organ, institution) draw on the later "mechanism" extension — the two branches of the same word that split apart into 机会 and 机械 as separate Dan'a'yo stand-ins.
