## Notes
- [仲](../characters/仲.md) + [秋 (char)](../characters/秋%20(char).md)
Second month of autumn. Contains the autumn equinox ([[秋分]]) and the Mid-Autumn Festival ([[中秋節]]). Corresponds to [[酉月]] and [[桂月]] — the osmanthus and the harvest moon. 중추절 is the Korean name for Chuseok, also in this month.
