## Notes
- [太 (char)](../characters/太%20(char).md) "great" + [初](../characters/初.md) "beginning"

太初 is the second of four primordial stages in the _Liezi_ (列子) cosmogony — the beginning of qi (氣), when breath/energy first emerges but form has not yet appeared. The sequence runs: [[太易]] → [[太初]] → [[太始]] → [[太素]]. See also [[天地]], [[混沌]].
