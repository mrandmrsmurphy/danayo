## Notes
- [銀 (char)](../characters/銀%20(char).md) "silver" + [色](../characters/色.md) "color"

Silver color: a cool, reflective grey-white. Like [[金色]], 銀色 belongs to the pair of precious-metal colors used in East Asian decorative art. The pairing 金銀 (gold and silver) is a fixed compound appearing in Buddhist ritual objects, metalwork, and poetry. In modern usage, 銀色 is common in product design and technology aesthetics (silver electronics, silver vehicles).
