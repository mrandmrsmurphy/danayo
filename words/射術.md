## Notes
- [射](characters/射.md) "shooting" + [術 (char)](characters/術%20(char).md) "skill"
- This neologism was coined because China uses 箭術 / 箭术 or 射箭, while Japanese archery is called [[弓道]] and so J/K use 弓術 and describe Western archery as 洋弓
1. archery (non-Western), one of the [[六芸]]
## Ancient kinds
- **白矢 (_Báishǐ_):** The arrow goes through the target so the white head shows on the other side.
- **參連 (_Cānlián_):** Rapid fire; the second arrow is released before the first hits.
- **剡注 (_Yǎnzhù_):** A high-speed, flat trajectory.
- **襄尺 (_Xiāngchǐ_):** The lord and the archer stand a specific distance apart to show respect.
- **井儀 (_Jǐngyí_):** Four arrows hitting the target in a square pattern.
