## Notes
- [申](../characters/申.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Seventh month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the ninth Earthly Branch 申 (monkey).

The first month of autumn. 申 also means "to report" or "to stretch out," fitting a month in which the year begins to extend toward its close. The Qixi Festival (七夕) falls in this month.
