## Notes
- [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "great; large" + [概 (char)](../characters/概%20(char).md) "rough idea; generality" (alias: 槪)

大概 functions in three grammatical roles. Most commonly it is an **adverb**: *dàgài* means "for the most part; in general; approximately; probably" — used either to hedge a statement's precision ("it's roughly this way") or its certainty ("it's probably the case"). As a **noun** it means "a general idea; a rough outline" — 大概 of the situation. As an **adjective** it means "approximate; rough" — a 大概 estimate.

The alias 大槪 uses 槪, the kyūjitai / older Chinese form of 概, carried as an alias of 概 (char) in the vault.

Nearly synonymous in its adverbial sense with [[大略]] ("roughly; approximately"), though 大概 is considerably more common in everyday speech and carries the additional nuance of epistemic probability ("probably") that 大略 does not.
