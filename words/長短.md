## Notes
- [長 (char)](../characters/長%20(char).md) "long" + [短 (char)](../characters/短%20(char).md) "short" — length, considered as a contrast between long and short; in Korean also extends to rhythmic stress and timing (장단, "rhythm/tempo"). Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese.
