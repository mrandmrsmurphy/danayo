## Notes
- [木 (char)](../characters/木%20(char).md) "tree" + [板](../characters/板.md) "plank"
- This word is the viable form of [板](../characters/板.md): the character's meaning (plank, board) is identical, but 板 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 木板 is its `stand_in` compound.
