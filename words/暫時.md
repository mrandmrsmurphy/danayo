## Notes
- [暫](../characters/暫.md) "temporary, momentary" + [時 (char)](../characters/時%20(char).md) "time"
- This word is the viable form of [暫](../characters/暫.md): the character's meaning (temporary; for a short time) is identical, but 暫 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 暫時 is its `stand_in` compound.
