## Notes
- [豹](../characters/豹.md) "leopard" + [猫 (char)](../characters/猫%20(char).md) "cat"
- This word is the viable form of [豹](../characters/豹.md): the character's meaning (leopard, panther) is identical, but 豹 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 豹猫 is its `stand_in` compound.
