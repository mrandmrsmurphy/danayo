## Notes
- [中 (char)](../characters/中%20(char).md) "middle" + [間](../characters/間.md) "between; interval; gap"

中間 is "middle; midway; intermediate" — 中 (middle) + 間 (between, among, interval, gap). The compound names the zone between two extremes — the midpoint on a spatial continuum, the intermediate stage between beginning and end, or the space between two entities. 間 contributes more than mere "between": it specifically evokes an interval, a gap, the space that exists between two defined points. 中間 is thus not merely "middle" but the in-between space — cf. 中心 (the center as core/hub) vs 中間 (the middle as interval between two sides).

Mandarin 中間 (zhōngjiān) covers spatial and temporal midpoints: 中間位置 (intermediate position), 中間人 (middleman; intermediary), 中間接口 (interface; middleware). Cantonese 中間 (zung1 gaan1). Japanese 中間 (ちゅうかん, chūkan): 中間管理職 (middle management), 中間試験 (midterm exam), 中間報告 (interim report). Korean 중간 (中間, junggan) is the everyday word: 중간 고사 (midterm exam), 중간 지점 (midpoint), 중간에 있다 (to be in the middle). Vietnamese trung gian (Sino-Vietnamese: 中=trung, 間=gian) is widely used for "intermediary; middleman": vai trò trung gian (role of intermediary).
