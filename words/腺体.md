## Notes
- [腺](../characters/腺.md) "gland" + [体](../characters/体.md) "body"
- This word is the viable form of [腺](../characters/腺.md): the character's meaning (gland) is identical, but 腺 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 体 ("body") grounds the gland in its anatomical context.
