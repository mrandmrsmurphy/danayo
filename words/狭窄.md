## Notes
- [狭](../characters/狭.md) "narrow; cramped" + [窄](../characters/窄.md) "narrow; tight"
- This word is the viable form of [窄](../characters/窄.md): the character's meaning (narrow, tight) is identical, but 窄 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 狭 ("narrow") is a near-synonym that doubles the meaning and licenses the compound.
