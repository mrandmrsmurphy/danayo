## Notes
- [北](../characters/北.md) "north" + [氷](../characters/氷.md) "ice" (alias 冰) + [洋](../characters/洋.md) "ocean" — the Arctic Ocean, attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (ほっぴょうよう, alongside the more common 北極海), Korean (북빙양, alongside 북극해), and Vietnamese (Bắc Băng Dương, with băng being the Sino-Vietnamese reading of 氷/冰).
