## Notes
- [正 (char)](../characters/正%20(char).md) "correct, proper" + [音](../characters/音.md) "sound" — the standard or prescriptively correct pronunciation of a character or word, as opposed to a regional or colloquial reading. Most famously the second word of 訓民正音 (Hunminjeongeum), the Korean document introducing Hangul. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese.
