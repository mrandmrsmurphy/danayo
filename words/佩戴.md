## Notes
- [佩](../characters/佩.md) "wear on waist" + [戴 (char)](../characters/戴%20(char).md) "wear on top"
- This word is the viable form of [佩](../characters/佩.md): the character's meaning (to wear accessories) is identical, but 佩 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 佩戴 is its `stand_in` compound.
