## Notes
- [飛 (char)](../characters/飛%20(char).md) "fly" + [翔](../characters/翔.md) "soar"
- This word is the viable form of [翔](../characters/翔.md): the character's meaning (to soar, glide) is identical, but 翔 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 飛 ("to fly") supplies the first syllable, grounding the compound in active flight.
