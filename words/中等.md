## Notes
- [中 (char)](../characters/中%20(char).md) "middle" + [等 (char)](../characters/等%20(char).md) "grade, rank" — intermediate; of middle rank or level

中等 occupies the middle position in the standard three-tier hierarchy of East Asian educational and administrative classification: 初等 (elementary) → 中等 (intermediate/secondary) → 高等 (advanced/higher). 中等教育 (secondary education) names the schooling between primary school and university; 中等学校 (secondary school) is the institutional form. The compound is equally at home outside education — 中等 income, 中等 difficulty, 中等 quality — wherever a scale calls for a midpoint.

The use of 等 as the second element in all five grade terms (初等, 中等, 優等, 劣等, 高等) reflects the original meaning of 等 as bamboo tablets arranged in a ranked series — a bureaucratic and scholarly technology of classification. 中等 is the default expectation: neither praiseworthy nor censurable, the unmarked middle.
