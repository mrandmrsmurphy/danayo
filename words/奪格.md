## Notes
- [奪](../characters/奪.md) "seize, take away" + [格 (char)](../characters/格%20(char).md) "case (grammatical)" — the case marking a source from which something is taken or departs.

The grammar book's own comparison table glosses this same case (marked in Dan'a'yo by 由) as 離格 ("departure case") rather than 奪格, an internal naming inconsistency within the source text — both terms describe the same case and are interchangeable, with 奪格 being the broken link this entry resolves.
