## Notes
- [駐](../characters/駐.md) "stop-over; stationed" + [屯 (char)](../characters/屯%20(char).md) "village"
- This word is the viable form of [駐](../characters/駐.md): the character's meaning (to be stationed) is identical, but 駐 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 駐屯 is its `stand_in` compound.
