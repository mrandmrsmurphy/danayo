## Notes
- [午](../characters/午.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Fifth month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the seventh Earthly Branch 午 (horse).

The fifth month contains the summer solstice (夏至) and the Duanwu Festival (端午節). 午 also means noon — the peak of the day — making this the peak month of the solar year.
