## Notes
- [未 (char)](../characters/未%20(char).md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Sixth month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the eighth Earthly Branch 未 (goat).

Midsummer. 未 also carries the meaning "not yet" — the harvest has not yet come, but the year's fullness is building. The goat's association with gentleness and abundance fits the lush, heavy heat of this month.
