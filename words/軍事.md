## Notes
- [軍](../characters/軍.md) "armed forces" + [事 (char)](../characters/事%20(char).md) "matter, affair" — military affairs collectively (strategy, forces, and related matters), as opposed to a single armed force itself.

軍事/군사/quân sự/ぐんじ stay closely aligned across the whole family, functioning as the standard, everyday-to-technical term for "military affairs" without notable drift — used equally in ordinary news reporting (軍事演習, "military exercises") and in formal policy or academic writing in all four languages.
