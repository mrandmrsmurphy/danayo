## Notes
- [消 (char)](../characters/消%20(char).md) "vanish, die out" + [耗](../characters/耗.md) "deplete, waste"
- This word is the viable form of [耗](../characters/耗.md): the character's meaning (to deplete; to consume) is identical, but 耗 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 消耗 is its `stand_in` compound.
