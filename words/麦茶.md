## Notes
- [麦](../characters/麦.md) "barley, wheat" + [茶 (char)](../characters/茶%20(char).md) "tea" — roasted barley tea, a staple beverage across East Asia. Attested across Mandarin (also as 大麦茶/[[大麦]]+茶), Cantonese, and Japanese (むぎちゃ). Korea uses the native word 보리차 rather than this hanja form, so Korean is left blank.
