## Notes
- [勃](../characters/勃.md) "rise suddenly" + [勃](../characters/勃.md) "rise suddenly" (reduplication)

勃勃 is a reduplicated intensifier, combining 勃 ("sudden rise, vigorous energy") with itself to convey an overflowing, surging vitality. In Mandarin it rarely appears alone; it functions as the predicate complement in fixed patterns where a noun names the kind of energy and 勃勃 characterizes its abundance: 生机勃勃 (bursting with vitality), 朝气勃勃 (full of youthful energy), 野心勃勃 (bristling with ambition). The last example shows that the energy need not be positive — 勃勃 amplifies whatever force it modifies, whether benign or threatening.

In Japanese (ぼつぼつ), the on'yomi reduplication is literary and unusual; the compound is primarily a Chinese term. The character 勃 (Dan'a'yo 注音: ㄅㄛㄊ) is highly bounded and typically appears only inside fixed compounds.
