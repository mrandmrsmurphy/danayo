## Notes
- [阿 (char)](../characters/阿%20(char).md) + [僧](../characters/僧.md) + [祇](../characters/祇.md) — a phonetic transliteration, carrying no meaning of its own beyond the borrowed sound. Sanskrit असङ्ख्येय (asaṅkhyeya, "incalculable"), an immeasurably vast number used throughout Buddhist sutras to describe spans of time, numbers of beings, and the length of a bodhisattva's path. Inherited identically into Japanese (あそうぎ), Korean (아승기), and Vietnamese (a-tăng-kỳ) via the shared Chinese Buddhist canon, the same mechanism as [[夜叉]] (yaksha).
