## Notes
- [慈](characters/慈.md) "kind" + [姑](characters/姑.md) "aunt".  Concerning the name of the plant, Compendium of Materia Medica says:  一根歲生十二子，如慈姑之乳諸子，故以名之。 One arrowroot yields twelve fruits, as a __kind mother_ gives birth to many sons, hence it is so called.
