## Notes
- [丑](../characters/丑.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Twelfth and final month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the second Earthly Branch 丑 (ox).

The last month before 寅月 begins the new year. Deep winter — the ox's patient, slow-moving nature suits a month of waiting and preparation. The 臘八 Festival and the year-end sacrifice (臘) fall in this month's counterpart in the poetic calendar.
