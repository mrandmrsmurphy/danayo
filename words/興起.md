## Notes
- [興 (char)](../characters/興%20(char).md) "to flourish, be roused" + [起 (char)](../characters/起%20(char).md) "to rise, arise" — to rise up, emerge, or flourish, especially of states, dynasties, or movements (帝国興起 "the rise of empires"). Less commonly carries the literary sense of being seized by a sudden impulse.
