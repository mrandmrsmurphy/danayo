## Notes
- [昆 (char)](../characters/昆%20(char).md) + [布](../characters/布.md) — kelp/seaweed. Attested as a real word (not just Japan-only) in Mandarin and Cantonese, where it is especially used in traditional Chinese medicine contexts. Japanese こんぶ (kombu) is the familiar dashi kelp. Korean 곤포 is the genuine Sino-Korean term recorded in the Donguibogam, alongside the more everyday native word 다시마. No Vietnamese attestation found.
