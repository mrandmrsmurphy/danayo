## Notes
- [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "big" + [麦](../characters/麦.md) "wheat, barley"
- This word is the viable form of [麦](../characters/麦.md): the character's meaning (grain; barley/wheat) is identical in this compound, but 麦 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 大麦 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese reads this おおむぎ (ōmugi), a native-Japanese compound (大 = おお, 麦 = むぎ) rather than the Sino-Japanese on-yomi concatenation.
