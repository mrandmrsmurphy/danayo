## Notes
- [鎬](../characters/鎬.md) "stove" + [京](../characters/京.md) "capital"
- This word is the viable form of [鎬](../characters/鎬.md): the character's primary meaning in Dan'a'yo is carried by its compound. 鎬京 is its `stand_in` compound, referring to the ancient Zhou capital (modern Xi'an) and by extension any royal capital.
