## Notes
- [完](../characters/完.md) "complete" + [璧](../characters/璧.md) "flawless jade"
- This word is the viable form of [璧](../characters/璧.md): the character's meaning (perfect, flawless) is identical, but 璧 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 完璧 is its `stand_in` compound.
