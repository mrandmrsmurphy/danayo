## Notes
- From [[甚]] "extremely/very" + [[様 (char)|様]] "appearance/manner": literally "what kind of appearance," functioning as an interrogative meaning "how" or "of what sort." Parallels the Cantonese/Mandarin 怎樣 (zěnyàng) and the Classical Chinese 如何 pattern. The Dan'a'yo form follows the Cantonese usage most closely (點樣 / 甚樣).
- Stand-in for the bound character [[甚]].
