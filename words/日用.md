## Notes
- [日 (char)](../characters/日%20(char).md) "day" + [用](../characters/用.md) "use" — daily use, everyday (use); attributive, as in 日用品 ("daily necessities") or 日用語 ("everyday vocabulary"). Attested across Mandarin, Japanese (にちよう), Korean (일용), and Vietnamese (nhật dụng). Forms half of the Dan'a'yo chengyu [[日用必備]].
