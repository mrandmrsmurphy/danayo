## Notes
- [望](../characters/望.md) "hope; gaze" + [楼](../characters/楼.md) "watchtower"
- This word is the viable form of [楼](../characters/楼.md): the character's meaning (watchtower) is identical, but 楼 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 望楼 is its `stand_in` compound.
