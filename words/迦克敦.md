## Notes
- 迦 + [克 (char)](../characters/克%20(char).md) + [敦](../characters/敦.md) — phonetic transcription of *Chalcedon*, the ancient city on the Bosphorus (modern Kadıköy, Istanbul).
- Site of the Council of Chalcedon (451 AD). Note: 迦 does not yet have a character file; its Dan'a'yo phonology (ㄍㄚ) is inferred from Korean 가 and Japanese か.
