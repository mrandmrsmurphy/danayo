## Notes
- [䦧 (char)](../characters/䦧%20(char).md) "quarrel, discord" + [神 (char)](../characters/神%20(char).md) "deity, god" + [星 (char)](../characters/星%20(char).md) "star" — the star of the goddess of discord; named for Ἔρις (Eris), the Greek goddess of strife, whose throwing of the golden apple sparked the Trojan War. The character 䦧 (alias 鬩, "to quarrel") was chosen to render her name's meaning directly rather than phonetically.
