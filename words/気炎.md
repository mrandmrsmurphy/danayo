## Notes
- [気 (char)](../characters/気%20(char).md) "feeling" + [炎](../characters/炎.md) "flame, inflammation"

Carries both senses of the Chinese 氣焰: "arrogance, bluster" (derogatory) and "imposing force, fighting spirit" (neutral or positive). The Japanese きえん tends toward the latter sense.
