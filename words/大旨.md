## Notes
- [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "great" + [旨](../characters/旨.md) "purpose" — the main idea or gist of something, a synonym of [[主旨]]. Attested in Mandarin and Cantonese with this noun sense. Japanese has 大旨 too, but as a different word — an adverb (おおむね, "generally, roughly") rather than this noun sense — so that field is left blank rather than forcing a mismatched reading. Korean's 대지 turns out to correspond to a different hanja (大地, "land"), not this word, so that field is blank too.
