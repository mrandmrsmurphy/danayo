## Notes
- [達 (char)](../characters/達%20(char).md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Darmstadt*, the German city where the GSI Helmholtz Centre that discovered the element is located. 達 is the first syllable of 達姆施塔特, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Darmstadt," truncated rather than spelled out in full, in the same pattern as 田納素 (Tennessine, from 田納西) and 莫斯素 (Moscovium, from 莫斯科). Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (ダームスタチウム, 다름스타튬).
