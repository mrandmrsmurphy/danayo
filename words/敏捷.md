## Notes
- [敏 (char)](../characters/敏%20(char).md) "quick; sharp; clever" + [捷](../characters/捷.md) "swift; victorious" — stand-in for [[捷]], which cannot appear independently
- A near-synonym compound pairing two characters that both gravitate toward "speed of mind and body": 敏 leans toward mental quickness (alertness, perceptiveness), 捷 toward physical swiftness and the swift outcome of success (its other major sense, "victory," survives in words like 大捷 "great victory"). Together they describe someone who is nimble in both senses — quick on their feet and quick on the uptake. The compound functions as a な-adjective in Japanese (びんしょう, binshō, "agile, alert") and as the Sino-Korean noun-root 민첩 (mincheop), which underlies the everyday adjective 민첩하다 "to be agile."
