## Notes
- [部 (char)](../characters/部%20(char).md) "part, section" + [位](../characters/位.md) "position, location"

部位 names a specific region or site within a larger whole — most often a part of the body (an injection site, the location of a wound or tattoo) but also any localized area of a machine, a building, or a process. 部, a 形声 compound of semantic 邑 and phonetic 咅, is the basic word for "part, section, department," already richly developed in [[部 (char)]]'s Words section through compounds like [[腹部]] ("abdomen"), [[腰部]] ("waist"), and [[部門]]/[[部隊]] ("department," "military unit").

The distinction between 部 alone and 部位 mirrors that between 位 alone and its many compounds in this batch: 部 names a *part* in the abstract (as in [[全部]] "the whole" vs. a 部 of it), while 部位 pins that part down to a specific *location* — precisely the sense 位 contributes throughout this list, from the measured points of [[水位]]/[[方位]] to the anatomical specificity of [[穴位]] (acupuncture points), to which 部位 is the general anatomical term.
