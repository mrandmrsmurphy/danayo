## Notes
- [杏](../characters/杏.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Second month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Apricot trees (杏) are among the earliest to bloom in spring, their pink-white blossoms appearing before the leaves. Their fragile beauty made them a symbol of early spring's brief, unreliable warmth.
