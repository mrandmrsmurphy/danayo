## Notes
- [胡](../characters/胡.md) "reckless" + [蝶](../characters/蝶.md) "butterfly"
- This word is the viable form of [蝶](../characters/蝶.md): the character's meaning (butterfly) is identical, but 蝶 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 胡蝶 is its `stand_in` compound.
