## Notes
- [稗](../characters/稗.md) "millet" + [子](../characters/子.md) (nominal suffix)
- This word is the viable form of [稗](../characters/稗.md): the character's meaning (barnyard millet) is identical, but 稗 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 子 functions here as a nominal suffix, as is common in Chinese plant and grain names.
- Japanese and Korean readings omitted: mechanical Sino readings (べし, 패자) create significant collisions with unrelated words in those languages.
