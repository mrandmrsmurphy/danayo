## Notes
- [要](../characters/要.md) "important, essential" + [塞](../characters/塞.md) "to block, frontier post"

要塞 means "fortress, stronghold" — a fortified position built at a militarily essential (要) chokepoint or border crossing (塞). 要, a character classified under 女 graphemically despite its modern abstract sense, means "important, essential, to need" — already seen in [[要素]] ("element, factor") and [[要求]] ("demand, request," both cross-linked from [[要]]'s own Notes).

塞 carries two quite different senses depending on context and pronunciation: as "to block, to stop up, to obstruct" (its primary frontmatter sense, seen in [[梗塞]], "infarction/obstruction," cross-linked from [[塞]]'s Words section); and, in words like 要塞 and 邊塞 ("border fortress"), as "a fortified frontier post" — historically the garrisoned passes and walls (including stretches of the Great Wall) that "blocked" invasion routes at strategically essential points. 要塞 thus combines both ideas at once: a position that is essential (要) precisely *because* it blocks (塞) an enemy's path — the etymological logic of "chokepoint" in English follows an almost identical metaphor.
