## Notes
- [缶](../characters/缶.md) "jar, can" + [頭 (char)](../characters/頭%20(char).md) "head; top"
- This word is the viable form of [缶](../characters/缶.md): the character means a jar or can, but is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo. 缶頭 is its `stand_in` compound. Dan'a'yo 缶 is aliased to 罐; the CJKV readings follow 罐頭.
