## Notes
- [名 (char)](../characters/名%20(char).md) "name" + [誉](../characters/誉.md) "reputation"
- This word is the viable form of [誉](../characters/誉.md): the character's meaning (reputation, fame) is identical, but 誉 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs it with 名 ("name"), evoking the classical idea that one's name and one's reputation are inseparable.
- Also used attributively: "honorary" (as in 名誉職, honorary position).
