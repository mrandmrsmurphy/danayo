## Notes
- [西](../characters/西.md) "west" + [洋](../characters/洋.md) "ocean" — literally "the western ocean," referring to Europe and (by extension) the Western world, as seen from East Asia. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese.
