## Notes
- A neologism was necessary because of the sharp CV vs JK split
	- Chinese use the novel character 氫 / 氢 for "light-gas"
	- The old Vietnamese term is from 氫氣 / 氢气 "light gas"
	- J/K/T use 水素 = "water element" from the same etymology as hydrogen
	- We split the difference.  See [Periodic Table](lexipedia/Periodic%20Table.md).
- [軽 (char)](characters/軽%20(char).md) "light" + [[素]] "element"
