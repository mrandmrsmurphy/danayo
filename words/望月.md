## Notes
- [望](../characters/望.md) "hope; gaze" + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md) "moon" — the full moon, traditionally the 15th night of the lunar month. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (もちづき/ぼうげつ, less common than 満月 but real), and Korean (망월, a literary Sino-Korean synonym alongside native 보름달).
