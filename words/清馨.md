## Notes
- [清](../characters/清.md) "pure, clear" + [馨](../characters/馨.md) "fragrant"
- This word is the viable form of [馨](../characters/馨.md): the character's meaning (fragrant; aromatic) is identical, but 馨 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 清馨 is its `stand_in` compound.
