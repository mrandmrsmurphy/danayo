## Notes
- [蘇](../characters/蘇.md) + [格 (char)](../characters/格%20(char).md) + [蘭](../characters/蘭.md) — a Mandarin/Cantonese phonetic transcription of "Scotland," the constituent country of the United Kingdom occupying the northern third of the island of Great Britain. Japanese and Korean instead use the katakana/hangul loans スコットランド/스코틀랜드 rather than this kanji/hanja form (Wiktionary marks 蘇格蘭 as an obsolete Japanese spelling), so no on'yomi/Sino-Korean reading is given here. Vietnamese retains the older Sino-Vietnamese name Tô Cách Lan alongside the modern English borrowing "Scotland."
