## Notes
- [莫 (char)](../characters/莫%20(char).md) + [斯](../characters/斯.md) + [素](../characters/素.md) "element" — a transliteration of *Moscow*, after the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research in Moscow Oblast. 莫斯 is the first two syllables of 莫斯科, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Moscow," truncated here rather than spelled out in full. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (モスコビウム, 모스코븀).
