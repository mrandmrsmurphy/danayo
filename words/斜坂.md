## Notes
- [斜](../characters/斜.md) "slant" + [坂](../characters/坂.md) "slope"
- This word is the viable form of [坂](../characters/坂.md): the character's meaning (slope, incline) is identical, but 坂 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. Notably, 斜 is itself bounded (its own `stand_in` is [[傾斜]]) — two bounded characters legitimizing each other.
- Not attested on Wiktionary; CJKV readings are inferred from components. Japanese and Korean omitted as the compound is not standard in those languages.
