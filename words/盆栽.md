## Notes
- [盆 (char)](../characters/盆%20(char).md) "basin" + [栽](../characters/栽.md) "plant, cultivate" — the art of growing miniature trees in shallow trays; the Chinese term penzai (盆栽) is the historical source of the better-known Japanese loanword "bonsai." Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (ぼんさい), and Korean (분재). Vietnamese has an analogous but distinct art form, hòn non bộ, rather than a direct translation, so that field is left blank.
