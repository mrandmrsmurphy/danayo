## Notes
- [媒](../characters/媒.md) "intermediary, matchmaker" + [体](../characters/体.md) "body, form" — literally "intermediary body," a vessel or channel through which something is carried or transmitted. In modern usage this is the standard Chinese and Japanese word for "medium/media" in the communications sense (新聞媒体 "news media," マスメディア calques aside), extending the older sense of 媒 as a go-between to the idea of a carrier of information or signal.

Korean 매체 follows the same Sino-reading and the same range of senses. Vietnamese does not use a calque of this compound — modern Vietnamese instead favors phương tiện truyền thông ("means of mass communication") for "media" — so no Vietnamese reading is given here.
