## Notes
- [単](../characters/単.md) "simple; single; sole" + [独](../characters/独.md) "alone; independent" — acting or existing by oneself, without accompaniment or assistance

単独 is used throughout the Sinosphere for independent action or singular existence: a 単独行動 is a solo operation, 単独飛行 a solo flight, 単独会見 a private one-on-one audience. The word contrasts with 共同 (joint, collaborative) and 合作 (cooperative). Its closest English equivalents shift by context: "alone," "solo," "single-handed," "unilateral," or "standalone."
