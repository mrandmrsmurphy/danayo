## Etymology
- [乾 (char)](../characters/乾%20(char).md) "dry" + [燥](../characters/燥.md) "parched, dry, arid" — 乾's unified sense (pure yang, absence of moisture) applied to its "dry" application; written 干燥 in Simplified Chinese, the simplification this vault's `干` character represents, but 乾 is the real character here, not 干 ("shield").
