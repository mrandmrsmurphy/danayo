## Notes
- [槐](../characters/槐.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Fourth month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. The pagoda tree (槐, Japanese pagodatree / Chinese scholar tree) was traditionally planted at government offices and academies. Its cream-colored blossoms in early summer gave this month an association with scholarly ambition and civil service.
