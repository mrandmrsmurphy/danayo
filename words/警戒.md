> [!warning] Homophones
> [[警戒]] (ㄍ⼶ㄫㄍ⼶) is a homophone of [[境界]] "boundary, zone"

## Notes
- [警](../characters/警.md) "watch out" + [戒](../characters/戒.md) "warn, admonish" — stand-in for both [[警]] and [[戒]], neither of which can appear independently.

Vietnamese quietly avoids a collision here: a literal Sino-Vietnamese rendering of 警戒 would use 警 (cảnh) + 戒 (giới), producing "cảnh giới" — but that exact form is already taken by the unrelated homophone [[境界]] ("realm, spiritual level," using a different first character, 境, that happens to share the same Vietnamese reading cảnh). So real Vietnamese instead expresses "vigilant, on guard" as cảnh giác, swapping in 覺 (giác, "to perceive, awaken") for the second syllable — keeping the meaning distinct from cảnh giới even though Dan'a'yo, Mandarin, Cantonese, and Korean all let 警戒 and 境界 collide as true homophones.
