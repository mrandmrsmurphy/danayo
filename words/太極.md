## Notes
- [太 (char)](../characters/太%20(char).md) "great" + [極 (char)](../characters/極%20(char).md) "extreme"

In Daoist and Neo-Confucian cosmology, 太極 is the "Supreme Ultimate" — the primordial unity from which yin (陰) and yang (陽) first differentiate. It emerges from [[無極]] and [[混沌]], and is the generative source of all things. The concept is central to the [[易経]] and to the practice of 太極拳 (t'ai chi ch'uan).
