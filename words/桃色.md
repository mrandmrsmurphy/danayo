## Notes
- [桃](../characters/桃.md) "peach" + [色](../characters/色.md) "color"

桃色 is the color of a ripe peach blossom: a warm, soft pink. In standard Chinese and Korean usage it is a neutral color term equivalent to "pink" or "peach." In Japanese, ももいろ (momo-iro) carries an additional layer: it has long been used as a euphemism for the erotic (cf. ピンク映画, pink films; ピンク業界), a usage parallel to the English "pink" in certain registers. The association with 桃花源 (Peach Blossom Spring) gives the color a faint utopian or romantic literary resonance in Chinese contexts.
