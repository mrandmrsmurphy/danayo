## Notes
- [第 (char)](../characters/第%20(char).md) "-th (ordinal prefix)" + [八 (char)](../characters/八%20(char).md) "eight"

第八 is the eighth in a series. The prefix 第 turns cardinal numbers into ordinals across all CJKV languages; 第八 follows the same pattern as 第一, 第二, 第三, etc.
