## Notes
- [同](../characters/同.md) "same" + [僚](../characters/僚.md) "colleague; companion"
- 同僚 is the stand-in word for [僚](../characters/僚.md): the character's meaning (colleague) is identical, but 僚 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 同僚 is its `stand_in` compound.
