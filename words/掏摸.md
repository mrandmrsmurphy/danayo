## Notes
- [掏](../characters/掏.md) "pickpocket" + 摸 "feel; grope"
- 掏摸 is the stand-in word for [掏](../characters/掏.md): the character's meaning (to pickpocket) is identical, but 掏 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 掏摸 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Note: Japanese reading すり (suri) is a special fused reading, not a concatenation of component readings.
