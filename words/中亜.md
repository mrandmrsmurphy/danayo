## Notes
- [中 (char)](../characters/中%20(char).md) "middle, central" + [亜](../characters/亜.md) "Asia" — Central Asia, the landlocked region comprising Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan. Standard in Mandarin (中亚) and Vietnamese (Trung Á); Japanese and Korean more commonly say 中央アジア/중앙아시아 rather than this shorter kanji/hanja form, though the reading given here is the regular on'yomi/Sino-Korean concatenation.
