## Notes
- [兗](../characters/兗.md) + [州 (char)](../characters/州%20(char).md) — one of the legendary [[九州]] (Nine Provinces) of ancient China, traditionally located in what is now southwestern Shandong and eastern Henan. Also the name of the modern Yanzhou district, Jining, Shandong. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (えんしゅう), Korean (연주), and Vietnamese (Duyện Châu).
