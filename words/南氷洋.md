## Notes
- [南](../characters/南.md) "south" + [氷](../characters/氷.md) "ice" (alias 冰) + [洋](../characters/洋.md) "ocean" — the Southern/Antarctic Ocean, attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (なんぴょうよう, an older name for 南極海), Korean (남빙양, alongside 남극해), and Vietnamese (Nam Băng Dương, with băng being the Sino-Vietnamese reading of 氷/冰).
