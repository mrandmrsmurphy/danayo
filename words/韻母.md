## Notes
- [韻](../characters/韻.md) "rhyme" + [母](../characters/母.md) "mother, base" — in traditional Chinese phonology, the part of a syllable remaining after the initial consonant (声母) is removed: the medial, nucleus, and coda taken together. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese as a technical term of historical phonology.
