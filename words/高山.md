## Notes
- [高 (char)](../characters/高%20(char).md) "high" + [山 (char)](../characters/山%20(char).md) "mountain" — a high mountain, or the alpine/highland terrain generally. The same two characters also name the Japanese city of Takayama (高山, たかやま), a separate proper-noun reading not used here.
