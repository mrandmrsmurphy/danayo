## Notes
- [蓬](../characters/蓬.md) "mugwort" + [莱](../characters/莱.md) "Penglai"
- This word is the viable form of [莱](../characters/莱.md): the character's meaning (Penglai) is identical, but 莱 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 蓬 supplies the compound's first syllable, lending the mythical name its full form.
- In classical Chinese mythology, the island paradise of the immortals located in the eastern sea — one of the 三神山. Also used figuratively for any fairyland or paradise.
