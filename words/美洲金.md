## Notes
- [美 (char)](../characters/美%20(char).md) + [洲 (char)](../characters/洲%20(char).md) "continent" (as in [[美洲]] "the Americas") + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — *Americium* is named directly after the Americas, by analogy with europium. Unlike most of the actinides discovered at Berkeley, this one calques the etymology rather than transliterating a surname, the same logic used for 日本素 (Nihonium, "named after Japan"). Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (アメリシウム, 아메리슘).
