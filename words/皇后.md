## Notes
- [皇](../characters/皇.md) "emperor" + [后](../characters/后.md) "empress"
- This word is the viable form of [后](../characters/后.md): the character's meaning (empress) is identical, but 后 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 皇后 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese reading こうごう uses GO (the secondary on-yomi of 后) rather than the expected KOU, yielding an elongated second syllable.
