## Notes
- [愛 (char)](../characters/愛%20(char).md) + [爾 (char)](../characters/爾%20(char).md) + [蘭](../characters/蘭.md) — a Mandarin/Cantonese phonetic transcription of "Ireland," the island and state in northwestern Europe. Japanese and Korean instead use the katakana/hangul loans アイルランド/아일랜드 rather than this kanji/hanja form, so no on'yomi/Sino-Korean reading is given here. Vietnamese retains the Sino-Vietnamese name Ái Nhĩ Lan alongside the modern English borrowing "Ireland." Combines with [[北]] ("north") and [[共和國|共和国]] ("republic") to distinguish [[北]]愛爾蘭 (Northern Ireland, part of the [[連合王国]]) from 愛爾蘭[[共和國|共和国]] (the Republic of Ireland).
