## Notes
- [栗 (char)](../characters/栗%20(char).md) "chestnut" + [色](../characters/色.md) "color"

栗色 is the color of the chestnut shell: a deep reddish-brown with warm undertones, darker than 茶色 and redder than 褐. In Japanese, くりいろ (kuri-iro) is an established traditional color name. The Korean 두음법칙 (initial sound rule) shifts 栗 from 률 to 율 at word start, giving 율색 (yul-saek) rather than 률색.
