## Notes
- [対](../characters/対.md) "to face, oppose" + [外](../characters/外.md) "outside" — foreign, external, oriented toward the outside; the antonym of 対内 ("internal, domestic," not yet a vault entry). Forms compounds such as 対外政策 ("foreign policy"), 対外貿易 ("foreign trade"), and [[対外戦争]] ("foreign war").
