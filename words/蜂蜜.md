## Notes
- [蜂 (char)](../characters/蜂%20(char).md) "bee, wasp, hornet" + [蜜](../characters/蜜.md) "honey"

蜂蜜 "bee-honey" is honey itself, paralleling [[蜂巣]] "bee-nest" (beehive). As with 蜂巣, 蜂's reading shifts from the bound character form fong (ㄈㄛㄫ/뽕) to the word-initial form pong (ㄆㄛㄫ/퐁), matching the standalone word [[蜂]]. The Mandarin, Cantonese, and Japanese readings (fēngmì, fung1 mat6, はちみつ) are all transparent compounds of "bee" + "honey," while the alias 蜜蜂 reverses the character order to instead mean "honeybee" (the insect itself, not its product) — a common source of confusion since both orders are attested.
