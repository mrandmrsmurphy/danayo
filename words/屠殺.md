## Notes
- [屠](../characters/屠.md) "butcher; slaughter" + [殺 (char)](../characters/殺%20(char).md) "kill"
- This word is the viable form of [屠](../characters/屠.md): the character's meaning (to slaughter) is identical, but 屠 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 殺 ("to kill") reinforces and licenses it as a compound.
