## Notes
- [獅](../characters/獅.md) "lion" + [城](../characters/城.md) "city, walled city" — the Lion City; a literary Chinese rendering of Malay *Singapura* (singa = lion, pura = city). Singapore's name and its Chinese sobriquet share the same underlying etymology.
