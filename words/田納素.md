## Notes
- [田](../characters/田.md) + [納 (char)](../characters/納%20(char).md) + [素](../characters/素.md) "element" — a transliteration of *Tennessee*, after Oak Ridge National Laboratory and the other Tennessee institutions that co-discovered the element. 田納 is the first two syllables of 田納西, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Tennessee," truncated rather than spelled out in full. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (テネシン, 테네신).
