So broad!  Use 文言文 for Literary Chinese (basically A.D.) and 古典漢語 for Classical Chinese (basically B.C.)
## Etymology
- [漢](../characters/漢.md) "Han (Chinese)" + [文](../characters/文.md) "culture"
- Notice that, by homophony, this block 韓文 being used for Hangǔl, so we use <ruby>[諺文](/words/諺文.md)<rt>ㄋ⼶ㄋㄇㄨㄋ</rt></ruby> instead
