## Notes
- [蜂 (char)](../characters/蜂%20(char).md) "bee, wasp, hornet" + [巣](../characters/巣.md) "nest"

蜂巣 "bee-nest" is the beehive or honeycomb, a straightforward concatenation of [蜂 (char)](../characters/蜂%20(char).md) (fong/뽕/ㄈㄛㄫ) and [巣](../characters/巣.md) (jau/잣/ㄐㄚㄨ). The Japanese reading はちのす reflects the native compound 蜂の巣 (literally "the bee's nest"); without the genitive の, はちす would instead read as 蓮 "lotus" — an unrelated homophone trap. Korean has two equally common terms: 봉소 (Sino-Korean, from 蜂巢) and 벌집 (native "bee-house").
