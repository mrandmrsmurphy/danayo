## Notes
- [薄](../characters/薄.md) "thin" + [膜](../characters/膜.md) "membrane"
- This word is the viable form of [膜](../characters/膜.md): the character's meaning (membrane; thin layer) is identical, but 膜 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 薄膜 is its `stand_in` compound.
