## Notes
- 掩 (MC 影母 + 鹽韻 → ㄝㄇ; no independent vault entry) "to cover; to conceal" + [襲](../characters/襲.md) "to attack"
- This word is the viable form of [襲](../characters/襲.md): the character's meaning (to attack) is identical, but 襲 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 掩 ("to conceal; to ambush") specifies the manner of attack, making it a near-synonym legitimizer compound.
