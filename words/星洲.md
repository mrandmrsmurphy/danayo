## Notes
- [星 (char)](../characters/星%20(char).md) "star" + [洲 (char)](../characters/洲%20(char).md) "islet, sandbar" — the Starry Islet; a classical Chinese sobriquet for Singapore, evoking its island setting. The name is preserved in the masthead of 聯合早報, Singapore's major Chinese-language daily.
