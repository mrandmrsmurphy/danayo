## Notes
- [卯](../characters/卯.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Second month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the fourth Earthly Branch 卯 (rabbit).

Early spring; the earth warms and life reappears. The rabbit's association with the moon made 卯月 auspicious for planting.
