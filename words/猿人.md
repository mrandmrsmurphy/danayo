## Notes
- [猿](../characters/猿.md) "ape" + [人 (char)](../characters/人%20(char).md) "person" — apeman, an early hominid (e.g. 北京猿人, Peking Man). Attested in Mandarin and Japanese. Korean 猿人 is only a hanja stub pointing to the unrelated homophone 원인 (原因, "cause"), with no genuine independent usage for this meaning, so that field is left blank.
