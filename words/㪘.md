## Notes
- [㪘 (char)](../characters/㪘%20(char).md) "gather, collect"

㪘 (canonical traditional form 斂, simplified 敛) means to gather in, collect, or restrain — particularly to draw something inward or to accumulate. The character is highly bounded and does not appear alone in ordinary discourse; it is most active in compounds: 收斂 (shōuliǎn, to converge, to restrain oneself; in mathematics, convergence), 斂財 (liǎncái, to amass wealth, often with connotations of greed), 斂容 (to compose one's expression), and 入斂 (to shroud the dead, to lay in a coffin).

The core semantic range spans two directions: gathering inward (collecting, accumulating, restraining) and the preparation of the dead for burial (the body gathered and wrapped). In Japanese (れん, ren), the on'yomi reading appears in compounds such as 収斂 (しゅうれん, convergence; astringency) and 斂葬 (rensō, burial). In Vietnamese, liễm is the Hán-Việt reading used in classical and religious writing. The Dan'a'yo form 㪘 uses the shinjitai / simplified structural simplification common to related characters with the 攴 radical.
