## Etymology
- [陶](../characters/陶.md) "pottery" + [瓷](../characters/瓷.md) "porcelain"

## Notes
- Real Japanese and Korean orthography spell this word 陶磁/도자, using [[磁]] instead of [[瓷]] — a historical extension of 磁's meaning from the Cizhou (磁州) kilns. Dan'a'yo keeps 磁 = magnetism and 瓷 = porcelain strictly separate (毎字明意), so this word is composed with 瓷 here; 陶磁 is recorded as an alias for the real-world spelling.
