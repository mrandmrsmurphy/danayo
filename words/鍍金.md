## Notes
- [鍍](../characters/鍍.md) "to coat; gild" + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "gold; metal"
- This word is the viable form of [鍍](../characters/鍍.md): the character's meaning (to gild) is identical, but 鍍 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 金 ("gold; metal") provides the object that completes the verb-object compound.
