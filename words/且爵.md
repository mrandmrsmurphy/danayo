## Notes
- [且 (char)](../characters/且%20(char).md) + [爵](../characters/爵.md) (stand-in for 嚼) — to chew, masticate. The real-world word is 咀嚼 (jǔjué/そしゃく/저작), well-attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, and Korean, but 咀 is an obscure character outside the Dan'a'yo set. 且爵 is the in-language respelling: 且, already a legal character, stands in for 咀 here purely as a graphemic substitute, the same way 爵 already stands in for 嚼. The 羅馬字/諺文/注音 columns are built mechanically from 且+爵's own bound forms per usual derivation; the foreign-language columns above record 咀嚼's actual cross-linguistic readings, since that is the word this entry represents.
