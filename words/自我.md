## Notes
### Definition
自我 denotes the conceptual or psychological self. It refers to the self as a center of consciousness, identity, or ego.

### Function
It operates at the philosophical and psychological level. It answers the question: what is the self as a subject of awareness and identity?

### Usage Principle
Use 自我 in discussions of self-consciousness, identity, personal development, or philosophical anthropology. Do not use it for simple reflexive reference or grammatical emphasis.  Not [[自己]], nor [[自身]].
