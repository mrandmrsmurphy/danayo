## Definition
**欧羅巴洲** / **欧洲** (Ōuluóbāzhōu / Ōuzhōu) _n._ The European continent as a geographic and political entity; Europe defined by its physical boundaries, nations, and territorial extent. The westernmost peninsula of the Eurasian landmass, conventionally bounded by the Atlantic Ocean, Arctic Ocean, Mediterranean Sea, and (variously defined) the Ural Mountains or other eastern limits. Emphasizes spatial, cartographic, and geopolitical dimensions.

_Usage_: Preferred for geographic, political, demographic, or administrative contexts. Example: "欧洲連盟" (European Union), "欧洲地圖" (map of Europe).  See also [[欧羅巴]].
