## Notes
- [交 (char)](../characters/交%20(char).md) "exchange, cross" + [替](../characters/替.md) "replace, substitute" — to take turns or swap places; alternation.

The grammar book uses 交替 in a specifically grammatical sense, describing how the [[繋辞]] copulas negate by suppletion — swapping in an entirely different verb form — rather than by the ordinary negating prefix: 否定交替, "negation alternation." Outside grammar, 交替 is the everyday word for shift-work and rotation (交替制, "shift system") across all of its source languages. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, and Korean.
