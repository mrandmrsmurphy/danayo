## Notes
- [騰](../characters/騰.md) "inflate; rise" + [貴](../characters/貴.md) "expensive; costly"
- 騰貴 is the stand-in word for [騰](../characters/騰.md): the character's meaning (to rise; to inflate) is identical, but 騰 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 騰貴 is its `stand_in` compound.
