## Notes
- [花](../characters/花.md) "flower" + [魁](../characters/魁.md) "oiran"
- This word is the stand-in for [[魁]], which is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo.
- The Japanese reading おいらん (oiran) is a jukujikun (special compound reading) not derived from standard on/kun readings.
