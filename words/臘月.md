## Notes
- [臘](../characters/臘.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Twelfth and final month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. 臘 refers to the ancient year-end sacrifice offered to the gods and ancestors. The 臘八 Festival (8th day) is marked by 臘八粥 (Laba congee), a thick porridge of grains and dried fruit. After 臘月 comes the New Year — this is the month of completion, offering, and preparation.
