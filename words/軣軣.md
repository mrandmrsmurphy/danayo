## Notes
- [軣](../characters/軣.md) "rumble; explosion" + [軣](../characters/軣.md) (ideophonic reduplication)
- (ideophonic) the sound or sensation of something large thundering, booming, or echoing — heavy machinery, explosions, drums, thunder, a train running powerfully.

軣軣 is the legitimizing compound for the otherwise bound, name-only character [[軣]]. Its standard written form across the Sinosphere is 轟轟 (the traditional/non-shinjitai shape, listed here as an alias); Dan'a'yo's canonical 軣 is the simplified shinjitai form. Best known as half of the chengyu 轟轟烈烈 ("vigorous and dynamic; spectacular," lit. "rumbling and fierce"), which pairs it with 烈烈.

Attested as a genuine Sino-Japanese onomatopoeia (漢語オノマトペ) in Japanese (ごうごう, very common — describing the roar of fire, a waterfall, a train, etc.), and as a real Sino-Korean compound in Korean (굉굉). No confirmed independent Vietnamese usage of the reduplicated form, so that field is left blank.
