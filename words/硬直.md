## Notes
- [硬 (char)](../characters/硬%20(char).md) "hard, stiff" + [直 (char)](../characters/直%20(char).md) "straight, fixed" — fixed stiff, unable to bend or adapt; used both physically (muscle rigidity) and figuratively (a rigid social atmosphere, 경직된 분위기). Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, and Korean.
