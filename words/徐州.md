## Notes
- [徐](../characters/徐.md) + [州 (char)](../characters/州%20(char).md) — Xu Province, the fourth of the [[九州]]

徐州 occupied the eastern region south of the Yellow River — roughly modern northern Jiangsu and southern Shandong — in the lower reaches of the Huai River valley. It was the land of the ancient state of Lu (魯), the homeland of Confucius, giving the region an outsized importance in the Confucian tradition. The city of Xuzhou in modern Jiangsu preserves the ancient name and has been continuously significant as a strategic junction between north and south China.
