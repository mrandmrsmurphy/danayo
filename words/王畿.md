## Notes
- [王 (char)](../characters/王%20(char).md) "king" + [畿](../characters/畿.md) "capital district" — the royal domain, the king's own central territory (traditionally a square of 1,000 *li* around the capital) at the center of the ancient Chinese concentric-zone model of the world (cf. the Nine/Five Domains 服 system: 王畿 → 侯服 → 甸服 → ... outward). Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (おうき), and Korean (왕기). No confirmed Vietnamese usage, so that field is left blank.
