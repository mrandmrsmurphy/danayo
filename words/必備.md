## Notes
- [必 (char)](../characters/必%20(char).md) "surely, must" + [備](../characters/備.md) "prepare, equip" — essential, indispensable, a must-have; attributive, as in 必備品 ("essential items") or 必備条件 ("required conditions"). Attested in Mandarin and Cantonese; no confirmed independent Japanese/Korean/Vietnamese dictionary entries, though the on'yomi/Sino-readings ひつび/필비/tất bị are regular and unproblematic. Forms half of the Dan'a'yo chengyu [[日用必備]].
