## Notes
- [来 (char)](../characters/来%20(char).md) "come" + [賓](../characters/賓.md) "guest"
- This word is the viable form of [賓](../characters/賓.md): the character's meaning (visitor, guest) is identical, but 賓 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 来賓 is its `stand_in` compound.
