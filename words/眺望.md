## Notes
- [眺](../characters/眺.md) "stare; gaze" + [望](../characters/望.md) "hope; view"
- This word is the viable form of [眺](../characters/眺.md): the character's meaning (to stare, gaze) is identical, but 眺 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 眺望 is its `stand_in` compound.
