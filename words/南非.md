## Notes
- [南](../characters/南.md) "south" + [非 (char)](../characters/非%20(char).md) "Africa" — South Africa, the country at the southern tip of the African continent. Standard in Mandarin (南非) and Vietnamese (Nam Phi); Japanese and Korean instead use the katakana/hangul loan 南アフリカ/남아프리카(공화국), so no 日本語/한국어 reading is given here.
