## Notes
- [亥](../characters/亥.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Tenth month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the twelfth and final Earthly Branch 亥 (pig).

Early winter. The year's energy goes to rest; the pig's association with abundance and contentment suits a month after the harvest is secured and stored. 亥 completes the cycle of twelve branches.
