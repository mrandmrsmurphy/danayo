## Notes
- [教](../characters/教.md) "teach" + [戒](../characters/戒.md) "warn, caution, admonish" — to instruct and admonish.

誨 (huì), "to instruct, to teach, to admonish," appears 5 times in the 《論語》, most famously in **述而**'s "誨人不倦" ("tireless in teaching others") and in **為政**'s discussion of true knowledge, where Confucius instructs (誨) Zi Lu on the difference between knowing and not knowing. 誨 lies outside Dan'a'yo's closed character set. Notably, [[戒]] itself already carries 誨 among its aliases (alongside 誡), reflecting how closely the senses of "to admonish/instruct" and "to warn/caution" overlap across these characters. The vault substitutes 教戒, joining 教 ("to teach") with 戒 ("to admonish, to caution") — a pure "Translation" substitution that captures both the instructive and admonitory senses of 誨, per [[translation/論語 (book)]]'s Vocabulary list.

The mandarin/cantonese/japanese/korean readings of 教戒 are compositional, built directly from 教 and 戒's own readings (jiào+jiè, gaau3+gaai3, KYOU+KAI, 교+계).
