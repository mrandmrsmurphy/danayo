## Notes
- [放](../characters/放.md) "release" + [蕩](../characters/蕩.md) "dissolute"
- This word is the viable form of [蕩](../characters/蕩.md): the character's meaning (dissolute, licentious) is identical, but 蕩 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 放蕩 is its `stand_in` compound.
