## Notes
- [啓](../characters/啓.md) "open" + [明 (char)](../characters/明%20(char).md) "bright"
- The classical name for the planet Venus when visible before dawn in the east. Modern astronomy identifies it with [[太白星]], the evening star — both are Venus.
