## Notes
- [複](../characters/複.md) "repeat, duplicate" + [雑 (char)](../characters/雑%20(char).md) "miscellaneous"

複雑 literally means "manifold and mixed" — many things jumbled together — hence "complicated, intricate." Note that the first character is 複 "multiple, duplicate" (ふく), not the near-homophone 復 "return, again" (also ふく): 複雑 is a frequent misspelling target in Japanese precisely because the two characters share a reading but not a meaning.
