## Notes
- Literally "rhinoceros-ox" — [[犀]] is the rhinoceros character, [[牛 (char)|牛]] provides the bovine classifier. The compound 犀牛 is the standard Mandarin and Cantonese word for rhinoceros; Japanese and Korean use different terms (サイ, 코뿔소). Vietnamese tê ngưu is the Sino-Vietnamese reading of the same compound.
- Stand-in for the bound character [[犀]].
