## Notes
- [邁](../characters/邁.md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Meitner*, after physicist Lise Meitner, who co-discovered nuclear fission but was denied a share of the Nobel Prize for it. 邁 is the first syllable of 邁特納, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Meitner," truncated rather than spelled out in full. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (マイトネリウム, 마이트너륨).
