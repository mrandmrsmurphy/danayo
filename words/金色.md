## Notes
- [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "gold, metal" + [色](../characters/色.md) "color"

金 in this compound specifically means gold the metal, narrowing from the character's broader sense of "metal." 金色 is the color of pure gold: a warm, lustrous yellow. It appears throughout CJKV visual culture in Buddhist iconography (gilded statues, gold leaf backgrounds in temple paintings), imperial regalia, and the traditional autumn color palette (金秋, golden autumn). In heraldry and design, 金色 contrasts with 銀色 as the two precious-metal colors.
