## Notes
- [同](../characters/同.md) "same" + [学](../characters/学.md) "learn; study" — neither character's own `stand_in` points here (同's is 同一, 学's is 学習). `kwin: true` per the AND-rule, since both are individually `kwin: true`.
- Filled a previously-blank `vietnamese` with đồng học, a real Sino-Vietnamese word (confirmed on Vietnamese Wiktionary), more formal than the everyday native equivalents học chung/bạn học.
- **Genuine homophone-across-different-hanja, not an error**: Korean 동학 is correct and standard for 同學 ("classmate; fellow student," verified via search), but it is an exact homophone of the unrelated 東學 ("Eastern Learning," the 19th-century Korean religious/political movement) — another instance of the recurring cross-linguistic pattern documented several times this sweep (cf. [[午飯]], [[羽翼]]/右翼).
