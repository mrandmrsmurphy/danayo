## Notes
- [地 (char)](../characters/地%20(char).md) "land" + [方](../characters/方.md) "direction" — neither character's own `stand_in` points here (地's is bare 地, 方's is 方向). `kwin: false` per the AND-rule, since both are individually `kwin: false`.
- **Real typo corrected**: `cantonese: di4 fang1` had two errors — 地 is never read di4 (its own character page confirms `dei6`), and 方 is never read fang1 (confirmed `fong1`); corrected to `dei6 fong1`.
- **Genuine Mandarin tone-based heteronym, not a comma-dump error**: dìfang (neutral tone, matching this word's primary listed sense "place; location") and dìfāng (full tone, meaning "local," as opposed to central/military — 地方政府 "local government" vs 中央 "central") are both real, distinct readings (verified via search) — kept both, matching the character's genuine 轻声/full-tone distinction.
- **Comma-dump untangled**: `korean` previously held `지방, 시골` — 지방 is the compound's own Sino-Korean reading, while 시골 is an unrelated native Korean word for "countryside." Kept only 지방.
- Filled a previously-blank `vietnamese` with địa phương, a real, extremely common Sino-Vietnamese word (confirmed on Vietnamese Wiktionary).
