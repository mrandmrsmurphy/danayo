## Notes
- [李](../characters/李.md) "plum" + [子](../characters/子.md) "son, child"
- [[李]] is primarily used as a surname in Dan'a'yo and cannot stand alone as common vocabulary; this word serves as its legitimizer, carrying the meaning of the plum fruit.
