## Notes
- [萎](../characters/萎.md) "wither" + [蕤](../characters/蕤.md) "green shoots"
- This word is the viable form of [蕤](../characters/蕤.md): the character's meaning (green shoots; Solomon's seal plant) is identical, but 蕤 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 萎蕤 is its `stand_in` compound.
