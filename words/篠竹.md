## Notes
- [篠](../characters/篠.md) "dwarf bamboo" + [竹 (char)](../characters/竹%20(char).md) "bamboo"
- 篠竹 is the stand-in word for [篠](../characters/篠.md); 篠 is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo discourse.
- Note: Japanese reads しのだけ (shinodake), a native reading rather than the expected on-yomi しょうちく.
