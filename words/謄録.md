## Notes
- [謄](../characters/謄.md) "transcribe; copy" + [録](../characters/録.md) "record; write down"
- 謄録 is the stand-in word for [謄](../characters/謄.md): the character's meaning (to transcribe by hand) is identical, but 謄 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 謄録 is its `stand_in` compound.
