## Notes
- [乳](../characters/乳.md) "milk" + [色](../characters/色.md) "color"

乳色 is the color of fresh milk or cream: a warm, slightly yellowish off-white. It occupies the space between pure white (白色) and ivory (象牙色), and is the recommended Dan'a'yo term for beige and cream tones. The character 乳's radical is 乙 (the second Heavenly Stem), though the character's phonetic component is 孚; its primary meanings are milk, breast, and suckling, making the color term a direct extension of the material's appearance.
