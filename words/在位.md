## Notes
- [在 (char)](../characters/在%20(char).md) "to exist, to be at" + [位](../characters/位.md) "position, rank, throne"

在位 means "to occupy [one's] position" — most often a monarch's reign ("during his 在位," "他在位三十年"), but extendable to any officeholder's tenure. 在 is a 形声 compound combining the semantic 才 with phonetic 士, meaning fundamentally "to be present at, to exist in [a place]" — already familiar from [[現在]] ("now, present," cross-linked from [[在 (char)]]'s Words section), where 在 marks temporal "presence."

在位 sits at the intersection of two clusters in this batch: it shares 位 with [[即位]] (the *act* of taking the throne), [[帝位]]/[[王位]] (the throne as an *object*), and describes the *duration* of holding that position — "在位中" (during [someone's] reign) is the standard way to anchor a historical date to a particular ruler's tenure, much as English uses "during the reign of."
