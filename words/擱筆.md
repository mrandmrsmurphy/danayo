## Notes
- [擱](../characters/擱.md) "put down; stop" + [筆 (char)](../characters/筆%20(char).md) "pen; brush"
- This word is the viable form of [擱](../characters/擱.md): the character's meaning (to stop writing) is identical, but 擱 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound specifies what is being put down — the pen.
- Classical closing formula used at the end of letters and essays to signal the author is setting down the brush.
