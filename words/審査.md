## Notes
- From [[審]] "examine carefully" + [[査]] "investigate": a compound meaning to formally evaluate or screen. Japanese しんさ (審査) is the primary source, used widely for contests, visa applications, and editorial review. Korean 심사 carries the same range. The Mandarin equivalent is 審查 (shěnchá); Dan'a'yo uses the Japanese-script 査 form.
- Stand-in for the bound character [[審]].
