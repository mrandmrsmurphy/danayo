## Notes
- [胡](../characters/胡.md) "reckless" + [乱](../characters/乱.md) "chaotic, confused"
- This word is the viable form of [胡](../characters/胡.md): the character's meaning (reckless; careless) is identical, but 胡 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 胡乱 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese reading うろん is a special established reading; the on-reading concatenation would be こらん. In Japanese, 胡乱 means "suspicious-looking" (a na-adjective), shifted from the Chinese sense of "careless/random."
