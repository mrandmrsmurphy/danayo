## Notes
- [網 (char)](../characters/網%20(char).md) "net" + [語](../characters/語.md) "language" + [体](../characters/体.md) "style, register" — the register of language specific to internet writing: netspeak.

The grammar book uses this term to mark a subgroup of ideophones so casual that they only ever appear in internet-style writing, never in formal registers. 網語 ("internet language/netspeak") is productively used in Mandarin discussions of online slang (网络用语, 网语), and 体 is the vocabulary's standard register-naming suffix, also seen in 口語体 and similar formations (not yet created). This compound's pedigree leans on Mandarin and Cantonese computing/internet-culture usage rather than a fixed dictionary term in any one language.
