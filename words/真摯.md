## Notes
- [真 (char)](../characters/真%20(char).md) "true" + [摯](../characters/摯.md) "sincere, cordial"
- This word is the viable form of [摯](../characters/摯.md): the character's meaning (sincere; earnest) is identical, but 摯 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 真摯 is its `stand_in` compound.
