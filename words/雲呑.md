## Notes
- [雲](characters/雲%20(char).md) "cloud" + [呑](characters/呑.md) "swallowing"
- This term is originally written as 餛飩／馄饨 (húntun) and the two terms are homophones in Cantonese. But 餛飩／馄饨 (húntun) usually refers to northern-style wontons while 雲吞／云吞 (yúntūn) refers to Cantonese-style wontons.
