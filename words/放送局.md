## Notes
- [放](../characters/放.md) "release, emit" + [送 (char)](../characters/送%20(char).md) "transmit, send" + [局 (char)](../characters/局%20(char).md) "station, bureau" — broadcast station; a station that transmits radio or television signals

放送 (broadcasting) combines the release of signal (放) with its directed transmission (送) — an apt description of the act of broadcasting, in which content is emitted into space and sent toward an audience. The compound was coined in Japan during the early radio era and spread to Korea; Chinese uses 播送 or 广播 for the activity but 电视台/广播电台 for the institution.

放送局 is the standard Japanese and Korean institutional term for a broadcasting company or station: NHK (日本放送協会) is the national public broadcaster; commercial 放送局 such as TBS, Fuji, and TV Asahi are private. Korean 방송국 follows the same pattern — KBS (한국방송공사), MBC, SBS. The compound implies a permanent licensed broadcaster rather than a one-time transmitter; 局 grounds the act of broadcasting in a recognized institutional presence.
