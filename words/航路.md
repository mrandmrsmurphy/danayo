## Notes
- [航](../characters/航.md) "to navigate, sail" + [路](../characters/路.md) "road, route" — a sea route or shipping lane (also used in Korean for an air route/airway). Used of maritime trade routes connecting island regions, as in 世界航路 ("global sea routes").
