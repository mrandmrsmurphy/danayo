## Notes
- [耽 (char)](../characters/耽%20(char).md) "indulge in" + [耽 (char)](../characters/耽%20(char).md) — (ideophonic) a fierce, covetous staring gaze. The real character is 眈 (not in the Dan'a'yo set); 耽 is the vault's established substitute, already used the same way in the chengyu [[虎視耽耽]] (real-world 虎視眈眈/虎视眈眈), the classical idiom this reduplication is drawn from — attested across Mandarin, Japanese (こしたんたん), and Korean (호시탐탐).
