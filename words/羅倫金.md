## Notes
- [羅](../characters/羅.md) + [倫](../characters/倫.md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Lawrence*, after physicist Ernest Lawrence, founder of the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory where the element was discovered. 羅倫 renders the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Lawrence" (as in 勞倫斯/羅倫斯), truncated to two syllables. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (ローレンシウム, 로렌슘).
