## Notes
- [利](../characters/利.md) + [佛 (char)](../characters/佛%20(char).md) + [素](../characters/素.md) "element" — a transliteration of *Livermore*, after Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, truncated to two syllables rather than spelled out in full the way Mandarin's borrowed-character system (利弗莫尔) would. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (リバモリウム, 리버모륨).
