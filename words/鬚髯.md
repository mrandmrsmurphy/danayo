## Notes
- [鬚](../characters/鬚.md) "beard" + [髯](../characters/髯.md) "beard"
- This word is the viable form of [髯](../characters/髯.md): the character's meaning (beard, whiskers) is identical, but 髯 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 鬚髯 is its `stand_in` compound.
