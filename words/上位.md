## Notes
- [上 (char)](../characters/上%20(char).md) "up, above" + [位](../characters/位.md) "position, rank"

上位 is the direct counterpart to [[下位]]: a high or superior position in any ranked system — the top spots in a leaderboard, the senior ranks of an organization, or a privileged place in a sequence. 上 is a 指事 ideogram, a short stroke above a long one marking "above" in the abstract, mirroring 下's construction exactly; the two characters form one of the most basic antonym pairs in the script, already extended in [[上 (char)]]'s Words section to compounds like [[上述]] ("the aforementioned," literally "stated above") and [[献上]] ("to present as tribute," literally "offer upward").

Together, 上位 and 下位 complete this batch's set of ten 位-compounds, which move from the most exalted positions (帝位, 王位) through office and tenure (即位, 在位, 爵位, 学位) to the everyday and the anatomical (坐位, 穴位) and finally to the abstract poles of any hierarchy at all (上位, 下位) — a small lexical map of what it means, across registers, to "have a position."
