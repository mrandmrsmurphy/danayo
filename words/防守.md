## Notes
- [防](../characters/防.md) "prevent" + [守](../characters/守.md) "protect"

防守 combines the sense of blocking (防, keeping something out) with that of holding ground (守, maintaining a position), together yielding the concept of active defense: not merely avoiding harm but standing firm against an advancing force. The word is used across military strategy, sport, and individual combat — a defender in football, a rearguard in battle, a parry in fencing all qualify as 防守.

The word sits in a natural pair with offense: 攻 (attack) vs. 守 (hold), which is why 攻守 and [[防護]] overlap in meaning but differ in emphasis — 防護 stresses protection of something, while 防守 stresses resistance against someone. See also [[守衛]].
