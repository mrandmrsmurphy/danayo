## Notes
- [石 (char)](../characters/石%20(char).md) "rock" + [版](../characters/版.md) "edition, version" — a stone printing plate; by extension, lithography, the printing technique that uses one.

石版/석판/せきばん/thạch bản all stay tightly consistent across the family as the standard technical term for lithography, with no register split or drift — Vietnamese thạch bản is even the term used in the Vietnamese Wikipedia article on the technique (in thạch bản).
