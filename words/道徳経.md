## Notes
- [道 (char)](../characters/道%20(char).md) "way, path" + [徳 (char)](../characters/徳%20(char).md) "virtue" + [経 (char)](../characters/経%20(char).md) "classic, scripture"

道徳経 is the *Tao Te Ching* (also romanized *Daodejing*), the foundational text of Daoism, attributed to the sage Laozi ([[老子]]). The title means "Classic of the Way and Virtue": 道 (the Way), 徳 (virtue, power), 経 (a canonical text). For the full text and commentary, see [[道徳経 (book)]].
