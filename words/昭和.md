## Notes
1. The Shōwa era in Japan, 1926–1989
	1. Literally, “glorious harmony”; from this passage in the 書経 (Shokyō, “Classic of History”):  百姓昭明，協和萬邦， The (nobles officials of the) hundred surnames are gloriously enlightened; the myriad polities are united and harmonious
	2. Calls to mind Japanese imperialism in the minds of many
2. a ward in Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture
