## Notes
惑 is 形声, built with [[或]] itself as its phonetic component, which is why the two are exact homophones in Dan'a'yo (and near-homophones throughout the family) despite naming unrelated concepts — 或 the disjunctive "or," 惑 "to be confused, bewildered, in doubt" (心, "heart," added to mark the emotional/mental sense). The core meaning holds steady across Chinese, Japanese, and Korean: 疑惑 "doubt, suspicion," 魅惑 "to charm, captivate" (to bewilder through allure), 惑星 (わくせい, "planet" — literally "a bewildering star," from the way planets appear to wander unpredictably against the fixed stars, unlike the old Chinese term 行星 which describes the same phenomenon more plainly as "a moving star").
