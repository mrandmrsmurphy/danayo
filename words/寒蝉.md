## Notes
- [寒](../characters/寒.md) "cold" + [蝉 (char)](../characters/蝉%20(char).md) "cicada" — a cicada chirping faintly in late autumn cold; figuratively, someone who dares not speak out, as in the idiom 噤若寒蟬 ("silent as a cicada in cold weather"). Attested in Mandarin; other languages not yet confirmed.
