## Notes
- [否 (char)](../characters/否%20(char).md) "no, not" + [定](../characters/定.md) "fix, settle, determine" — to settle a matter as "not so": negation, denial.

The grammar book uses 否定 specifically for the grammatical operation that the prefix 不– performs on a verb phrase (否定接頭辞, "negating prefix"), an operation it notes also extends to the [[繋辞]] copulas, which negate by suppletion (a different verb form entirely) rather than by ordinary prefixation — described as 否定[[交替]], "negation alternation." 否 itself is a fairly rare standalone root outside this and a handful of other compounds (否認, "to deny," not yet created), making 否定 one of its most important attestations. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, and Vietnamese.
