## Notes
- [多 (char)](../characters/多%20(char).md) "many" + [辺](../characters/辺.md) "side" — many-sided, or (of negotiation, diplomacy) multilateral, as in 多辺協議 ("multilateral negotiation"). Compare 多辺形 ("polygon"). Vietnamese diplomatic usage favors the synonym "đa phương" but "đa biên" is also attested.
