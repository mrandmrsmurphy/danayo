## Notes
- [加](../characters/加.md) "to add" + [多 (char)](../characters/多%20(char).md) "many, much" — "to add more; to augment, increase."

**加多** (*jiāduō*) is a transparent verb compound meaning "to increase the quantity of, to add more of" — combining 加 ("to add to") with 多 ("many, much") to give the resulting sense "add-to-make-more." It functions in ordinary Mandarin much like English "to add more" or "to increase," and the same two morphemes carry the same straightforward meaning when read in Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, or Vietnamese, without any special or idiomatic shift.

In this vault, 加多 additionally serves as the phonetic-semantic basis for **[[加多金]]** ("gadolinium"): 加多 approximates the *gado-* of "gadolinium" while its literal sense ("adding more, increasing") supplies an internally meaningful — if chemically arbitrary — gloss, with [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) ("metal") marking the element's category. See [[加多金]] for the full account of that formation.
