## Notes
- [津](../characters/津.md) "haven" + [波](../characters/波.md) "wave"
- 津波 is the stand-in for [津](../characters/津.md), which is too bounded to stand alone.
- Japanese reading つなみ is a fused form (津 = つ "inlet", 波 = なみ "wave"), not a concatenation of the on'yomi.
