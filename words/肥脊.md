## Notes
- [肥](../characters/肥.md) "fat, plump" + [脊](../characters/脊.md), standing in for its alias [[瘠]] "lean, thin, emaciated" — a paired antonym compound; fatness and leanness as a range or contrast. 瘠 (疒 + phonetic 脊, MC d͡z + iᴇk) shares 脊's Dan'a'yo syllable ㄐㄝㄎ exactly — the voicing distinction between their Middle Chinese initials (從 vs 精) doesn't survive into Dan'a'yo — and its meaning is a transparent metaphorical extension of 脊 "spine, ridge": a body thin enough to show its ridge. Too narrow to merit its own entry, it is aliased onto its phonetic and semantic parent 脊, with the real-world spelling 肥瘠 preserved as this word's alias.
