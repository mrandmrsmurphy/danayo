## Notes
- [太 (char)](../characters/太%20(char).md) "great" + [素](../characters/素.md) "element"

In this compound 素 carries the sense of "plainness" or "undifferentiated substance." 太素 is the fourth and final primordial stage in the _Liezi_ (列子) cosmogony — the beginning of substance or matter (質), when material stuff first appears but has not yet formed individual things. The sequence runs: [[太易]] → [[太初]] → [[太始]] → [[太素]]. See also [[天地]], [[混沌]].
