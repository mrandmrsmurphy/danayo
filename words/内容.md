## Notes
- [内](../characters/内.md) "inside" + [容 (char)](../characters/容%20(char).md) "look, appearance, form, figure" — an independent compound; neither character's own `stand_in` points here (内's is `内部`, 容's is bare `容`); together, "what is contained within [a form]" — content, substance

Mandarin nèiróng, Cantonese noi6 jung4, Japanese ないよう (naiyō), Korean 내용 (naeyong), and Vietnamese nội dung are all the standard, everyday word for "content, substance" (of a text, message, or container) across the sphere. No native displacement or homophone collision found.
