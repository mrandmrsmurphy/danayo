## Notes
- [使](../characters/使.md) "cause; send; use" + [用](../characters/用.md) "use; employ" — stand-in for [[用]], which cannot appear independently

Mandarin shǐyòng, Cantonese si2 jung6, Japanese しよう (shiyō), Korean 사용 (sayong), and Vietnamese sử dụng are all the standard, everyday word for "to use, utilize" across the sphere (使用説明書/사용설명서, "instruction manual"). No native displacement or homophone collision found — a clean compound with directly parallel usage in all five languages.
