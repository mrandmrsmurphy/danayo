## Notes
- [急 (char)](../characters/急%20(char).md) "quick" + [遽](../characters/遽.md) "in a hurry"
- This word is the viable form of [遽](../characters/遽.md): the character's meaning (in a hurry) is identical, but 遽 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 急遽 is its `stand_in` compound.
