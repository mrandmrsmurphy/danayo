## Notes
- [史](../characters/史.md) "history; chronicle" + [学](../characters/学.md) "learning; study"

**史学** is the academic study of history — both the events and processes of the past themselves, and the methods, interpretive traditions, and institutional frameworks through which those events are recorded and understood. In East Asian university contexts, 史学 is the standard disciplinary name equivalent to "History" as a faculty or department: 史学科 (history department), 史学者 (historian).

The word is consistent across the CJKV sphere — しがく, 사학, sử học — and contrasts with 歴史 (the subject matter, "history" as a sequence of past events) in the same way that English distinguishes "historiography" from "history." See also [[史学者]] for the person who practises it.
