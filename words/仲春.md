## Notes
- [仲](../characters/仲.md) + [春 (char)](../characters/春%20(char).md)
Second month of spring. 仲 means the middle of three — second-born, neither beginning nor end. Contains the spring equinox ([[春分]]). Corresponds to [[卯月]].
