## Notes
- [寅](../characters/寅.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
First month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the third Earthly Branch 寅 (tiger).

The traditional year begins not with 子 (rat) but with 寅 (tiger), following the convention established in the Zhou dynasty. The winter solstice falls in 子月 (11th month), making 寅月 the first month of spring — the season of new beginnings.
