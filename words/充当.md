## Notes
- [充](../characters/充.md) "to fill" + [当 (char)](../characters/当%20(char).md) "to correspond to, to serve as"

充当 means "to allocate, to assign, to apply [funds, personnel, or resources] toward [a purpose]" — most often in financial or administrative contexts, as in 予算を充当する ("to allocate from the budget"). 当, the shinjitai simplification of 當 (with phonetic component 尚), means "to correspond to, to be equivalent to, to serve as" — seen in [[当然]] ("of course, natural") and [[当世]] ("this present age," both cross-linked from [[当 (char)]]'s Words section).

充当 sits alongside [[充足]] and [[充電]] as the third of this batch's 充-compounds, and like 充足 happens to have a Korean reading (충당) that coincides exactly with its compositional reading (充 충 + 当 당) — reflected in `kwin: true`. Where 充足 describes a *state* (being adequately filled) and 充電 describes *replenishing a depleted store*, 充当 describes *directing* a filled store toward a specific designated use — the bureaucratic act of earmarking resources, completing this batch's small map of what can be done with something that has been "filled" (充).
