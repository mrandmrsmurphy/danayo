## Notes
- The conventional form 叛乱/叛亂 is written with 叛, but Dan'a'yo folds 叛 into [反 (char)](../characters/反%20(char).md), as Japanese does — a deliberate merge even though 叛's original reading (pàn, preserved in this word's own 注音 ㄅㄚㄋ) differs slightly from 反's (fǎn, ㄈㄛㄋ). Not a data error.
