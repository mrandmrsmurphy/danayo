## Notes
- [炎](../characters/炎.md) "flame, inflammation" + [色](../characters/色.md) "color"

Used primarily in the scientific compound 炎色反応 (flame test), the analytical chemistry technique of identifying metal ions by the characteristic color their salts impart to a flame.
