## Notes
- [厠](../characters/厠.md) "WC; toilet" + [所 (char)](../characters/所%20(char).md) "place"
- This word is the viable form of [厠](../characters/厠.md): the character's meaning (toilet) is identical, but 厠 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound adds 所 ("place"), making the location explicit.
- Japanese omitted: mechanical SHI+SHO reading ししょ collides with 司書 "librarian".
