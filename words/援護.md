## Notes
- [援](../characters/援.md) "aid, assist" + [護](../characters/護.md) "protect, safeguard" — support, backing, aid. Mandarin/Cantonese treat this compound as a Japanese borrowing rather than native vocabulary (Chinese Wiktionary lists it only under its Japanese entry), but Korean genuinely and independently attests it: 원호, "to help and look after," used specifically for state support of disabled veterans and national merit recipients (원호 기금, 상이군인 원호).
