## Notes
- [哥](../characters/哥.md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Copernicus*, after astronomer Nicolaus Copernicus. 哥 is the first syllable of 哥白尼, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Copernicus," truncated rather than spelled out in full, in the same pattern as 邁金 (Meitnerium) and 西博金 (Seaborgium). Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (コペルニシウム, 코페르니슘).
