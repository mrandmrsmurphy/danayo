## Notes
- [指](../characters/指.md) "finger; point" + [揮](../characters/揮.md) "command; conduct"
- This word is the viable form of [揮](../characters/揮.md): the character's meaning (to conduct, command) is identical, but 揮 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 指 ("to point; direct") supplies the first syllable, evoking the gesture of the conductor's baton.
