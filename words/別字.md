## Notes
- [別 (char)](../characters/別%20(char).md) "other, distinct" + [字 (char)](../characters/字%20(char).md) "character, glyph"

別字 names a "different/wrong character" — a typo, a misspelling, or a character mistakenly substituted for the intended one (often because the two are homophones or look similar). 別, a 会意 character built from 𠮠 and 刂 ("knife"), means "other, distinct, separate" — already prolific in [[別荘]] ("villa"), [[区別]] ("to differentiate"), [[離別]] ("to part ways"), and [[個別]] (the next word in this batch), all cross-linked from [[別 (char)]]'s Words section.

字 is the basic word for a written character or glyph, anchoring an enormous family of script-related vocabulary in [[字 (char)]]'s Words section — [[文字]] ("script"), [[字典]] ("dictionary"), [[正字]] ("correct character," the conceptual opposite of 別字), and [[識字]] ("literacy"). 別字 sits naturally alongside [[正字]]: where 正字 is the standard, correct form of a character, 別字 is "a different character [from the one that should be there]" — the everyday term a teacher might use marking a student's homework, or a proofreader catching a substitution error.
