## Notes
- [偈](../characters/偈.md) "Buddhist verse" + [陀](../characters/陀.md) "slope; uneven" — transliteration of Sanskrit गाथा (gāthā)
- This word is the viable form of [偈](../characters/偈.md): the character means a Buddhist hymn or gatha, but is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo. 偈陀 is its `stand_in` compound.
