## Notes
- [蜂 (char)](../characters/蜂%20(char).md) "bee, wasp, hornet"

蜂 is the general term for stinging hymenopteran insects — bees, wasps, and hornets alike — rather than being restricted to honeybees specifically (compare [[蜂蜜]] "honey" and [[蜂巣]] "beehive," both of which lean toward the bee sense). The Mandarin, Cantonese, and Japanese readings (fēng, fung1, はち) are all straightforward reflexes of the same Middle Chinese root, while Vietnamese distinguishes the native word *ong* "bee" (used here) from the Sino-Vietnamese reading *phong*, which surfaces instead in words built on the "seal/enfeoff" sense of the homophone character [[封]].
