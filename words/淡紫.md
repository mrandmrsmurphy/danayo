## Notes
- [淡](../characters/淡.md) "diluted, pale" + [紫](../characters/紫.md) "purple"

淡 (pale, diluted) modifies 紫 (purple) to produce lavender: the lighter, cooler range of the purple spectrum. The compound is transparent and analytically compositional — the same pattern as 淡紫 (pale purple), 淡紅 (pale red, pink), 淡青 (pale blue-green). In CJKV color vocabulary, lavender does not have a single canonical term; 淡紫 is the most widely interpretable option and the recommended Dan'a'yo form.
