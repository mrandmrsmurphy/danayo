## Notes
- [連 (char)](../characters/連%20(char).md) "joined" + [合 (char)](../characters/合%20(char).md) "united" + [国](../characters/国.md) "nation" — the United Nations, as named in Mandarin (联合国), Cantonese, Korean (연합국), and Vietnamese (Liên Hợp Quốc). In Japanese, 連合国 (rengōkoku) instead historically denotes the World War II Allied Powers; modern Japanese calls the UN itself 国際連合 (国連).
