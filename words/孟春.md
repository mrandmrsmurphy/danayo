## Notes
- [孟](../characters/孟.md) + [春 (char)](../characters/春%20(char).md)
First month of spring in the ancient seasonal calendar. 孟 denotes the eldest or first of three — the beginning of a season's arc. Corresponds to [[寅月]] and roughly to the modern first lunar month.
