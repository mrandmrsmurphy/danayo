## Notes
- [冒](../characters/冒.md) "take a risk" + [険 (char)](../characters/険%20(char).md) "precipitous"
- This word is the viable form of [冒](../characters/冒.md): the character's meaning (to take a risk, adventure) is identical, but 冒 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 冒険 is its `stand_in` compound.
