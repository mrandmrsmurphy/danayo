## Notes
- [畢](../characters/畢.md) "all in all" + [竟 (char)](../characters/竟%20(char).md) "finally"
- This word is the viable form of [畢](../characters/畢.md): the character's meaning (after all, all in all) is identical, but 畢 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 畢竟 is its `stand_in` compound.
