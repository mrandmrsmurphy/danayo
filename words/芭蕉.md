## Notes
These three _do_ refer to somewhat different things botanically and culturally:

- **[[香蕉]]** — the common Cavendish-type banana (Musa acuminata), the supermarket fruit
- **芭蕉** — the Japanese fiber banana (Musa basjoo) or related species, more ornamental/fibrous, Bashō's namesake
- **[[甘蕉]]** — a more classical/literary term, overlapping with banana generally but with a sweeter/archaic connotation
