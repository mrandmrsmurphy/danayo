## Notes
- [臘](../characters/臘.md) "Laba" + [八 (char)](../characters/八%20(char).md) "eight"

The 8th day of the 12th lunar month (臘月). This word serves as the stand-in for [臘](../characters/臘.md), which is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo discourse — the character's meaning is inseparable from this specific date. See [[臘八節]] for the festival itself.
