## Notes
**Form.** 杜金 is [杜](characters/杜.md) + [金 (char)](characters/金%20(char).md) “metal,” following the rule for transition elements.

**Motivation.** The international name _dubnium_ derives from **Dubna** (杜布納), the Russian research center associated with its discovery. A straightforward toponymic reduction would take 杜 from 杜布納, parallel to other place-based names.

**Type of formation.**  
A **toponymic abbreviation with semantic overlay**:

- source: 杜布納 (Dubna)
- reduction: 杜
- suffixation: 金
- added effect: lexical pun (杜 = “to fabricate”)

**Fit within the system.**

1. Preserves the naming origin (Dubna).
2. Maintains one-character uniqueness.
3. Uses 金 appropriately for a d-block element.
4. Introduces a mild semantic layer without breaking consistency.
