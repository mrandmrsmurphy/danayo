## Notes
- [鵉](../characters/鵉.md) "luan bird" + [鳳](../characters/鳳.md) "phoenix"
- This word is the viable form of [鵉](../characters/鵉.md): the character's meaning (luan, a mythical bird) is identical, but 鵉 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 鵉鳳 is its `stand_in` compound.
