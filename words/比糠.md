## Notes
- [比 (char)](../characters/比%20(char).md) (stand-in for 秕/粃) + [糠](../characters/糠.md) — chaff and bran, the worthless husks and dust left over from grain. The real-world word is 秕糠 (bǐkāng in Mandarin, 粃糠/hikō in Japanese, 비강 in Sino-Korean), also attested in the reversed order 糠秕 in Chinese, but 秕 — and its graphemic variant 粃, which uses 米 in place of 秕's 禾 — is not itself a Dan'a'yo character: a character that would anchor only this single compound is pruned per 保頭断尾 rather than given a full entry. 比, already a legal character and in fact 秕's own phonetic component (形声: semantic 禾 + phonetic 比), stands in for it here purely graphemically. 糠 is the same character used in [[糟糠]]; this word doubles as 糠's legitimizing stand-in, since 糠 is too bounded to appear alone. The 羅馬字/諺文/注音 columns are built mechanically from 比+糠's own bound forms; the foreign-language columns above record 秕糠's actual cross-linguistic readings, since that is the word this entry represents.
