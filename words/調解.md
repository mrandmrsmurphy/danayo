## Notes
- [調](../characters/調.md) "to adjust" + [解 (char)](../characters/解%20(char).md) "to resolve" — to mediate or settle a dispute, especially in legal contexts (人民調解, "people's mediation"). Chiefly a Mandarin/Cantonese legal term; Japanese instead favors 調停 (chōtei) for this sense, so no on'yomi reading is given here.
