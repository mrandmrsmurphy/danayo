## Notes
- [他 (char)](../characters/他%20(char).md) "another; other; additional"

他 is the quantifier of otherness in Dan'a'yo — "another; other; additional; else" — modifying a noun to indicate a different or separate entity from what is contextually established: 他人 (another person; a stranger), 他日 (another day; some other time), 他国 (another country; a foreign land). In the Dan'a'yo quantifier system, 他 marks distinction by exclusion — the modified referent is separate from, or other than, the one already given in context. In classical Chinese, 他 (OC *l̥ʰaːl) served both as a third-person pronoun ("he; she; it") and as an indefinite modifier "other; another"; Dan'a'yo retains only the latter function, using 他 strictly as a modifier. 他 also serves as the Dan'a'yo stand-in for the character [[他]].

Mandarin 他/别 (tā/bié, another; other): 其他 (qítā, other; the rest), 别人 (biérén, another person; others), 他处 (tāchù, elsewhere; another place), 另外 (lìngwài, in addition; besides). Cantonese 其他 (kei4 taa1, other; the rest), 另 (ling6, another; a different one), 其他人 (the other people). Japanese 他 (た/hoka): 他人 (tanin, another person; stranger), 他国 (takoku, another country), ほかの (hoka no, another; other — native), その他 (sonota, and so forth; others). Korean 타 (他): 타인 (他人, another person; stranger), 기타 (其他, and so forth; et cetera), 다른 (dareun, different; another — native). Vietnamese tha (他): tha nhân (他人, another person; outsider), nơi khác (another place — native).

kwin is false: Dan'a'yo 트 does not match Korean 타. 他 reads 트 (tǝ/ㄊㄜ) in Dan'a'yo — MC 透母 (tʰ, aspirated dental stop) + 歌韻 (ɑ). Both Dan'a'yo and Korean preserve the MC aspiration of 透母 (Dan'a'yo ㄊ th-; Korean ㅌ th-). The MC vowel 歌韻 (ɑ) diverges: Dan'a'yo gives -ə (ㄜ/트), while Korean gives -a (타). This Dan'a'yo -ə vs Korean -a for MC 歌韻 after consonantal initials is a recurring pattern — also seen in [[可]] (ㄎㄜ/크 vs 가). When 歌韻 follows a null initial (as in [[我]], MC 疑母+歌韻), both systems give -a; after consonantal initials, Dan'a'yo shifts to -ə while Korean retains -a.
