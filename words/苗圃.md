## Notes
- [苗](../characters/苗.md) "seedling" + [圃](../characters/圃.md) "seedbed"
- This word is the viable form of [圃](../characters/圃.md): the character's meaning (seedbed, nursery) is identical, but 圃 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 苗圃 is its `stand_in` compound.
