## Notes
- [爆](../characters/爆.md) "explode" + [発 (char)](../characters/発%20(char).md) "issue forth"
- This word is the viable form of [爆](../characters/爆.md): the character's meaning (to explode) is identical, but 爆 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound adds 発 ("to burst forth"), making the outward force of the explosion explicit.
