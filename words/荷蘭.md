## Notes
- [荷](../characters/荷.md) + [蘭](../characters/蘭.md) — a Mandarin/Cantonese phonetic transcription of "Holland," used as the standard name for the Netherlands. Japanese and Korean instead use the katakana/hangul loans オランダ (from Portuguese "Holanda," via early contact) and 네덜란드 (from English "Netherlands") rather than this kanji/hanja form. Vietnamese Hà Lan is the standard modern Vietnamese name for the country, directly cognate with this compound.
