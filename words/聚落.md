## Notes
- [聚](../characters/聚.md) "assemble" + [落](../characters/落.md) "settle" — a settlement, village (聚落考古學 "settlement archaeology"). The standard Mandarin and Korean (취락) term, preferred here over Japan's more common 集落 per the character page's own note. Also attested in Japanese as a secondary form.
