## Notes
- [公 (char)](../characters/公%20(char).md) "public" + [園](../characters/園.md) "garden, park, orchard" — an independent compound; neither character's own `stand_in` points here (公's is bare `公`, 園's is `庭園`); 公 here contributes its "public" sense (as in [[公共]]), not the "sir/lord" sense stored as its primary gloss

Mandarin gōngyuán, Cantonese gung1 jyun4, Japanese こうえん (kōen), Korean 공원 (gongwon), and Vietnamese công viên are all the standard, everyday word for "park" (a public garden) across the sphere. No native displacement or homophone collision found.
