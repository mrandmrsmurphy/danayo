## Notes
- [冬 (char)](../characters/冬%20(char).md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Eleventh month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Simply the "winter month" — the one that contains the winter solstice (冬至). Paired with 子月 in the Earthly Branch calendar, which carries the same significance: the darkest point, when hidden yang stirs beneath the cold.  Also sometimes called [[葭月]] "reed month"
