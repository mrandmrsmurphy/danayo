## Notes
- [別 (char)](../characters/別%20(char).md) "separate, other" + [野](../characters/野.md) (stand-in for 墅, "villa") — a villa, country house. The real character is 墅 (merged into 野 in this vault, per its own note: "by evil substitution, 別墅 must become 別野"). Attested in Mandarin (別墅), Cantonese, and Vietnamese (biệt thự). Distinct from [[別荘]], the parallel Japanese formation using a different second character (荘); Korean similarly uses 별장 (別莊) rather than this form.
