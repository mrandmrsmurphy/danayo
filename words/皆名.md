## Etymology
- [皆 (char)](../characters/皆%20(char).md) "all, every" + [名 (char)](../characters/名%20(char).md) "name" — the polite/honorific counterpart to [[皆人]], completing the 名-row of the correlative pronoun grid alongside the already-attested [[其名]] ("him, her"). Compare [[皆様]], the same root paired with 様 instead of 名, used for addressing a group directly rather than referring to it in the third person.
