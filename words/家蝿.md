## Notes
- [家](../characters/家.md) "house, family" + [蝿](../characters/蝿.md) "fly"
- 家蝿 is the stand-in word for [蝿](../characters/蝿.md); 蝿 is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo discourse.
- Note: Japanese reads いえばえ (iebae), a native compound rather than the Sino-Japanese on-yomi.
