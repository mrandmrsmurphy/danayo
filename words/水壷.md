## Notes
- [水 (char)](../characters/水%20(char).md) "water" + [壷](../characters/壷.md) "pot"
- This word is the viable form of [壷](../characters/壷.md): the character's meaning (pot; vessel) is identical, but 壷 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 水壷 is its `stand_in` compound.
