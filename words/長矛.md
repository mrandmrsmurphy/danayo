## Notes
- [長 (char)](../characters/長%20(char).md) "long" + [矛](../characters/矛.md) "halberd"
- This word is the viable form of [矛](../characters/矛.md): the character's meaning (spear, halberd) is identical, but 矛 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 長矛 is its `stand_in` compound.
