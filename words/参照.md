## Notes
- [参](../characters/参.md) "consult" + [照 (char)](../characters/照%20(char).md) "illuminate, compare against" — to refer to a specific source; to cross-reference

Where 参考 is general consultation — browsing a source for ideas — 参照 is a directed comparison: you are told to look at a specific thing and hold it up against what you are examining. 照 is the illuminating mirror that makes the comparison possible; 参照 is the instruction to perform that comparison. In written texts, 参照p.45 or 上記参照 (see above) directs the reader to a precise location. In legal and technical documents, 参照 is the standard word for cross-referencing provisions, clauses, or figures.

Vietnamese tham chiếu is particularly common in translated technical texts — software documentation, legal instruments, and scientific papers all use it heavily. The compound's clean semantic division of labor (参 = consult, 照 = compare against) makes it one of the more transparent Sinitic scholarly compounds.
