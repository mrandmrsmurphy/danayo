## Etymology
- [陶](../characters/陶.md) "pottery" + [瓷](../characters/瓷.md) "porcelain" + [器](../characters/器.md) "container, vessel"

## Notes
- Real Japanese and Korean orthography spell this word 陶磁器/도자기, using [[磁]] instead of [[瓷]] — see [[陶瓷]] for the historical Cizhou-kiln reasoning. Dan'a'yo composes this word with 瓷 to keep 磁 = magnetism and 瓷 = porcelain strictly separate (毎字明意); 陶磁器 is recorded as an alias for the real-world spelling.
