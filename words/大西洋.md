## Notes
- [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "great" + [西](../characters/西.md) "west" + [洋](../characters/洋.md) "ocean" — the Atlantic Ocean, literally "the great western ocean," a name coined by Jesuit missionaries (Matteo Ricci among them) after their arrival in China, naming the ocean that lay west of Europe from a Chinese vantage point.
