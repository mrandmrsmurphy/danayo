## Notes
- [巳](../characters/巳.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Fourth month of the ancient Chinese lunar calendar, named for the sixth Earthly Branch 巳 (snake).

Snakes emerge fully from hibernation as the earth warms into late spring. 巳 is also associated with the hour of 9–11 a.m., when the sun climbs high — mirroring the season's rising energy.
