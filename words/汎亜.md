## Notes
- [汎 (char)](../characters/汎%20(char).md) "broad, all-encompassing" + [亜](../characters/亜.md) "Asia" — spanning or uniting all of Asia, as in 汎亜主義 (Pan-Asianism). Attested in traditional-script Mandarin (汎亞), Cantonese, Japanese, and Korean (범아시아주의).
