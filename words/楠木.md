## Notes
- [楠](../characters/楠.md) "camphor tree" + [木 (char)](../characters/木%20(char).md) "tree"
- This word is the viable form of [楠](../characters/楠.md): the character's meaning (camphor/nanmu tree) is identical, but 楠 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 楠木 is its `stand_in` compound.
