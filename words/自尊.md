## Notes
- [自](../characters/自.md) "self" + [尊](../characters/尊.md) "revere, honored" — self-respect, dignity.

矜 (jīn), "pride, dignity, self-respect" (and, in other contexts, "to pity" or "spear-shaft"), appears 5 times in the 《論語》, including **衛靈公**'s description of the gentleman as "矜而不爭" ("dignified, but not contentious"). 矜 lies outside Dan'a'yo's closed character set, so the vault substitutes the attested compound 自尊 (zìzūn) — "self-respect, self-esteem, dignity" — a "Translation" substitution per [[translation/論語 (book)]]'s Vocabulary list, joining 自 ("self") with 尊 ("to revere, to honor").

自尊 is a widely attested modern word across all five languages: Sino-Japanese じそん, Sino-Korean 자존, and Sino-Vietnamese tự tôn all carry the same "self-esteem, self-respect" sense, making it a natural and unambiguous stand-in for 矜's dignified-pride meaning.
