## Notes
- [桧](../characters/桧.md) "(name character: cypress/juniper kind)" + [木 (char)](../characters/木%20(char).md) "tree, wood" — hinoki cypress (Chamaecyparis obtusa), the prized Japanese construction and shrine-building timber. Japanese ひのき is the everyday term; the compound 檜木 itself is also a genuine, if secondary, Korean name (회목), alongside 노송나무, for the same tree — clearing "more than Japanese pedigree" even though Mandarin uses unrelated names for this species (黃檜, 扁柏).
