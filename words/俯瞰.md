## Notes
- [[俯]] ("bow, look down") + [[瞰]] ("look down from a height") — to look down from above, or the resulting bird's-eye view; attested across Mandarin (俯瞰), Japanese (俯瞰/ふかん), and Korean (부감), most often in compounds like 俯瞰図/鳥瞰図 ("aerial view diagram") or 俯瞰撮影 ("overhead/bird's-eye shot").
