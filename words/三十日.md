## Notes
- [三十](words/三十.md) + [日 (char)](../characters/日%20(char).md)

Japanese has a special reading: みそか (misoka), not the expected さんじゅうにち. The last day of a month is 晦日 (みそか); the last day of the year specifically is 大晦日 (おおみそか).
