## Notes
- [天 (char)](../characters/天%20(char).md) "sky" + [地 (char)](../characters/地%20(char).md) "land" + [人 (char)](../characters/人%20(char).md) "person"

The three powers or "three talents" (三才) of classical East Asian cosmology: Heaven, Earth, and Humankind. Humans are understood as the mediating principle between celestial and terrestrial forces. See also [[天地]].
