## Notes
- [懸 (char)](../characters/懸%20(char).md) "hang" + [心 (char)](../characters/心%20(char).md) "heart"

Dan'a'yo follows SVO word order, so the verb precedes its object: 懸心 ("hang-heart"), not 心懸. Equivalent to Japanese <ruby>心懸ける<rt>こころがける</rt></ruby> — to keep something in mind, to be mindful of.
