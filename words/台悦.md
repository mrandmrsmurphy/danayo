## Notes
- [台 (char)](../characters/台%20(char).md) "platform, pedestal" + [悦](../characters/悦.md) "pleased, overjoyed" — happy, pleasant.

怡 (yí), "happy, joyful, content," appears 6 times in the 《論語》, including **子路**'s description of brothers getting along "兄弟怡怡" and **鄉黨**'s account of Confucius's own cheerful demeanor at home. 怡 lies outside Dan'a'yo's closed character set, so the vault substitutes 台悦 — pairing 台, which shares 怡's 台 phonetic component (both descend from Middle Chinese d-initial syllables), with 悦, whose meaning "pleased, joyous" supplies the translation. This is a "Component + Translation" substitution per [[translation/論語 (book)]]'s Vocabulary list.

The mandarin/cantonese/japanese/korean readings of 台悦 are drawn from the attested compound 怡悅/怡悦 (yíyuè), "happy, pleasant, cheerful." 諺文/羅馬字/注音 remain compositional from 台 and 悦's own fixed Dan'a'yo values (대+엳), diverging from 怡's Sino-Korean reading 이.
