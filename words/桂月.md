## Notes
- [桂](../characters/桂.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Eighth month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Osmanthus (桂花) blooms in early autumn with an intensely sweet fragrance. The Mid-Autumn Festival (中秋節) falls this month, and according to legend a cassia / osmanthus tree grows on the moon — making 桂 the quintessential moon-month.
