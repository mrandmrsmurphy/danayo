## Notes
- [毎 (char)](../characters/毎%20(char).md) "every; each; distributive" + [類](../characters/類.md) "kind; sort; category; type"

毎類 is the distributive kind/category correlative — "each kind; each type separately; distributed across categories one by one." As a 副用名詞, it applies a predicate to each category individually rather than to all categories as a collective: 毎類を別々に処理する ("process each type separately"); 毎類に対して異なる方法を使う ("use a different approach for each kind"). The distributive 毎 marks per-category application, contrasting with collective 皆類 (every kind together).

Mandarin 各種 (gèzhǒng, "each kind; various kinds") captures the per-category distribution. Japanese 種類ごとに (shurui goto ni, "for each type/category; type by type") is the most precise equivalent: 種類ごとに分類する ("classify by type, for each category"). Korean 종류별로 (jongryubyeollo, "by type; per category; classified by kind") is the standard category-distribution form. Vietnamese từng loại (each type/kind — distributing across types one at a time).

The original 毎類 file carried english: "totally, systematically" — adverbs of completeness and manner that belong to the 皆様/何様 domain, not the distributive-category domain. The correct meaning is category-distributive: "each type, one at a time." The original ROmaji moilui (毎=moi) has also been corrected to moylui (毎=moy), consistent with all other 毎X entries. The 毎類/皆類 distinction — "each kind separately" (distributive) vs "all kinds together" (collective universal) — mirrors the 毎/皆 split running through every column pair in the correlative table.
