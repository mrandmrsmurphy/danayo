## Notes
- [[対外]] "foreign, external" + [[戦争]] "war" — war against a foreign power or external state, the standard historiographical antonym of [[内乱]] ("internal disorder, civil strife"). Per Japanese reference dictionaries (Kotobank, Weblio), 対外戦争 is formally defined as war between two or more sovereign states (or coalitions of states) seeking to gain control over part or all of one another's territory or to secure an external advantage — as opposed to 内戦/内乱, fought between factions within a single state.
