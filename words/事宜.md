## Notes
- [事 (char)](../characters/事%20(char).md) "matter, affair" + [宜](../characters/宜.md) "fitting, suitable"

事宜 means "arrangements, fittings, matters that need to be properly handled" — the practical details and proper procedures surrounding some undertaking (籌備事宜, "preparation arrangements"; 注意事宜, "matters requiring attention"). 事 is a 会意 character combining 又 ("hand") with 中 ("a flagpole bearing a drum") — the image of holding a flag, hence "to perform one's duty, an affair, a matter" — already anchoring a large family in [[事 (char)]]'s Words section, from [[事実]] ("fact") to [[従事]] ("to engage in").

宜, a character of more complex 多 graphemic classification, means "suitable, fitting, appropriate" — the same core sense seen in its other two cross-linked compounds, [[時宜]] ("timely, opportune") and [[便宜]] ("convenient, expedient," both from [[宜]]'s Words section). 事宜 combines these into "matters that are properly/suitably [arranged]" — a slightly formal, bureaucratic-register word for the practical specifics that must be settled before or during some undertaking, as opposed to the undertaking's substance itself.
