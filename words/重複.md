## Notes
- [重 (char)](../characters/重%20(char).md) "heavy; repeated" + [複](../characters/複.md) "repeat"
- This word is the viable form of [複](../characters/複.md): the character's meaning (to repeat, duplicate) is identical, but 複 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 重複 is its `stand_in` compound.
