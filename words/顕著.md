## Notes
- [顕](../characters/顕.md) "prominent; clear" + [着 (char)](../characters/着%20(char).md) "wear", here carrying its 著 variant's "manifest; prominent" sense
- 顕著 is the stand-in word for [顕](../characters/顕.md) ("prominent"), which is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo.
- The Japanese reading けんちょ pairs 顕 with ちょ rather than 着's recorded on-yomi ちゃく/じゃく — the same 著/着 split that the word's second character reflects.
