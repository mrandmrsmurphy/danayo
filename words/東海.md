## Notes
- [東](../characters/東.md) "east" + [海](../characters/海.md) "sea"
- In Classical Chinese cosmology, the Eastern Sea is one of the 四海 — the four seas encircling the known world. This is the primary Dan'a'yo sense.
- Modern geopolitical usages are language-dependent and secondary: what Koreans call 東海 (동해) is what Japanese call 日本海; what Chinese call 東海 (东海) is the East China Sea. Disambiguation is needed in any modern geographic context.
