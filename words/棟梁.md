## Notes
- [棟](../characters/棟.md) "ridgepole" + [梁 (char)](../characters/梁%20(char).md) "girder"
- This word is the viable form of [棟](../characters/棟.md): the character's meaning (ridgepole) is identical, but 棟 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs two structural beam terms to make it usable.

1. (Architecture) **ridgepole and beam** — the main structural members of a roof
2. **pillar; mainstay** — a person or thing of central importance to an organization or society
3. (Japanese) **master carpenter** — a head craftsman who oversees a building project
