## Notes
- [廃](../characters/廃.md) "abrogate; terminate" + [黜](../characters/黜.md) "dethrone"
- 廃黜 is the stand-in word for [黜](../characters/黜.md): the character's meaning (to dethrone; to dismiss) is identical, but 黜 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 廃黜 is its `stand_in` compound.
