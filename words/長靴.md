## Notes
- [長 (char)](../characters/長%20(char).md) "long" + [靴](../characters/靴.md) "boot"
- This word is the viable form of [靴](../characters/靴.md): the character's meaning (boot) is identical, but 靴 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 長靴 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese reading ながぐつ uses the native kun-yomi (なが + ぐつ), not the on-reading concatenation ちょうか.
