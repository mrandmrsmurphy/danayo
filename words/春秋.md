## Definition
1. Spring and autumn
2. short for the whole year - 春夏秋冬
3. a year, one's age
4. The Spring and Autumn Period, fully [[春秋時代]] (770–476 BC)
5. The Spring and Autumn Annals, i.e. [[四書五経]]
	1. also called [[麟経]]/麟經 or [[麟史]]
	2. 呂氏春秋
	3. 晏子春秋
	4. 春秋三傳
### Chengyu
