## Notes
- [偉](../characters/偉.md) "great, extraordinary" + [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "big" — stand-in for [[偉]], which cannot appear independently

偉大 is "great, extraordinary" — a synonym compound intensifying 偉's own sense of greatness with 大 "big," used for people, achievements, and civilizations rather than mere physical size. 偉 cannot appear as an independent vault entry on its own, so 偉大 is what legitimizes it.

Mandarin 偉大/伟大 (wěidà). Cantonese 偉大 (wai5daai6). Japanese 偉大 (いだい, idai) is a common, slightly formal register word for greatness of character or achievement — 偉大な指導者 ("a great leader") — distinct from plain 大きい (ōkii, physical size). Korean 위대 (widae) patterns the same way, very common in 위대하다 ("to be great") and 위대한 지도자 ("a great leader"). Vietnamese vĩ đại carries the identical sense and register, one of the most common Sino-Vietnamese adjectives for greatness (vĩ đại như núi, "great as a mountain," a common simile).

kwin is true: both 偉 and 大 individually match their own Sino-Korean readings exactly (위=위, 대=대), so the compound comes out true as well.
