## Notes
- [仲](../characters/仲.md) + [冬 (char)](../characters/冬%20(char).md)
Second month of winter. Contains the winter solstice ([[冬至]]) — the darkest point of the year, when yang begins its hidden return. Corresponds to [[子月]] and [[冬月]].
