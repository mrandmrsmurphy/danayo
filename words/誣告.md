## Notes
- [誣](../characters/誣.md) "falsely accuse" + [告](../characters/告.md) "inform; report"
- This word is the viable form of [誣](../characters/誣.md): the character's meaning (to falsely accuse) is identical, but 誣 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound adds 告 (to report/charge), specifying the legal act of filing a false complaint.
- Japanese ふこく is the mechanical Sino reading (FU+KOKU); note it is a homophone of 布告 "proclamation" in Japanese — irrelevant to Dan'a'yo.
