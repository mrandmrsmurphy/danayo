## Notes
- [沙 (char)](../characters/沙%20(char).md) "sand" + [漠](../characters/漠.md) "desert"
- This word is the viable form of [漠](../characters/漠.md): the character's meaning (desert; vast and desolate) is identical, but 漠 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 沙漠 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese conventionally writes this 砂漠 rather than 沙漠; the reading さばく is the regular concatenation of SA + BAKU.
