## Notes
- [国](../characters/国.md) "nation" + [連 (char)](../characters/連%20(char).md) (stand-in for 聯, "league") — the League of Nations (国際聯盟), abbreviated 国联/國聯 in Mandarin. Not to be confused with [[連合国]] (United Nations) — Japanese 国連 is the everyday abbreviation for the UN (国際連合), but that referent is already covered by 連合国 in this vault; this entry covers the earlier, defunct interwar organization instead.
