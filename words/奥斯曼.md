## Notes
- [奥 (char)](../characters/奥%20(char).md) + [斯](../characters/斯.md) + [曼](../characters/曼.md) — the standard Mandarin phonetic transcription of "Osman/Ottoman" (奥斯曼帝国 "Ottoman Empire"), naming the dynasty and empire founded by Osman I. Japanese and Korean instead use the katakana/hangul loans オスマン/오스만 rather than a kanji/hanja form, so no 日本語/한국어 reading is given here.
