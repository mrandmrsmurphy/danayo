## Notes
- [使](../characters/使.md) "envoy, herald" + [徒](../characters/徒.md) "disciple, follower"
- The Protestant and Eastern Orthodox term for an apostle. Catholics use [[宗徒]] instead. See also 使徒信経 (Apostles' Creed) and 使徒[[行傳]] (Acts of the Apostles).
