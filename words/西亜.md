## Notes
- [西](../characters/西.md) "west" + [亜](../characters/亜.md) "Asia" — West Asia, the geographic region comprising Anatolia, the Arabian Peninsula, Iran, the Levant, Mesopotamia, and the Caucasus. Standard in Mandarin (西亚) and Vietnamese (Tây Á); Japanese and Korean more commonly use the katakana/hangul loan アジア/아시아 (西アジア, 서아시아) rather than this kanji/hanja form, though the reading given here is the regular on'yomi/Sino-Korean concatenation.
