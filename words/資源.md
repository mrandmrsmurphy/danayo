## Notes
- [資](../characters/資.md) "capital, means" + [源](../characters/源.md) "source, origin" — resources, especially natural resources (天然資源) or marine resources (世界資源). Not to be confused with the unrelated Vietnamese word "tài nguyên" ("resources"), which is a calque of a different Sino compound (財源) rather than a cognate of 資源.
