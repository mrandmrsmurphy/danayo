## Notes
- [大 (char)](../characters/大%20(char).md) "great" + [王 (char)](../characters/王%20(char).md) "king" — an honorific title appended to the names of especially revered monarchs, as in 世宗[[大王]] (King Sejong the Great) or 廣開土大王 (King Gwanggaeto the Great). Also used historically in Japan for early sovereigns before the title 天皇 was adopted (大王/オオキミ), and in Vietnamese folk religion for deified kings and generals (đại vương). Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (だいおう), Korean (대왕), and Vietnamese (đại vương).
