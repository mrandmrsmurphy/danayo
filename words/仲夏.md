## Notes
- [仲](../characters/仲.md) + [夏 (char)](../characters/夏%20(char).md)
Second month of summer. The apex of heat. Contains the summer solstice ([[夏至]]) and the Duanwu Festival (端午節). Corresponds to [[午月]]. Shakespeare's *A Midsummer Night's Dream* echoes the same concept in the Western tradition.
