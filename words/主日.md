## Notes
- [主](../characters/主.md) "lord; master" + [日 (char)](../characters/日%20(char).md) "day; sun"

主日 is the Christian liturgical term for Sunday — the Lord's Day, the first day of the week on which the resurrection is commemorated. The compound is used across Chinese, Korean, and Vietnamese Christian communities as the formal term for Sunday in a devotional register, as distinct from the secular weekday name (星期日, 일요일, etc.). It appears in compounds such as 主日崇拜 ("Sunday worship") and 主日學 ("Sunday school").
