## Notes
- [規](../characters/規.md) "rules; compass and square" + [定](../characters/定.md) "to fix, to decide" — to stipulate; a regulation; a fixed rule

規 originally depicted the compass and square (矩規) — the instruments used to draw circles and right angles, and by extension the standards that ensure things conform to a correct shape. 規定 is the act of using such an instrument on conduct or procedure: to fix, in rule form, what is required or permitted. The compound thus sits between the generality of 規則 (rule, general pattern) and the formality of 法律 (law, statutory norm).

In everyday usage, 規定 refers to the specific provisions of an organizational or contractual text — the clauses of a contract, the articles of a bylaw, the terms of a policy: 規定に従う (to follow the regulations), 規定通り (as stipulated). In mathematical and logical contexts, 規定 means to define or specify a domain or condition: 規定された範囲 (the specified range).

Vietnamese quy định is particularly common as an administrative term — it appears on official signage, notices, and policy documents as the standard word for "regulations" or "provisions," showing how the compound has been fully naturalized into modern Vietnamese bureaucratic language.
