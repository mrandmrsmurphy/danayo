## Notes
- [波](../characters/波.md) + [斯](../characters/斯.md) — a Tang-dynasty Chinese phonetic transcription of "Persia" (via Middle Persian Pārs), naming the historical Persian civilization and, in earlier usage, the Sasanian Empire specifically. Still the standard Mandarin and Vietnamese name for Persia/Iran (Ba Tư); Japanese historically read this 波斯 as はし (and later ペルシャ via Dutch Perzië), though modern Japanese now favors the katakana loan ペルシャ.
