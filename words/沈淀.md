## Notes
- [沈](../characters/沈.md) "sink" + [淀](../characters/淀.md) "swampy"
- This word is the viable form of [淀](../characters/淀.md): the character's meaning (sediment, settled matter) is identical, but 淀 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 沈淀 is its `stand_in` compound.
