## Notes
- [香](../characters/香.md) "incense" + [蒲](../characters/蒲.md) "bulrush"
- This word is the viable form of [蒲](../characters/蒲.md): the character's meaning (bulrush, cattail) is identical, but 蒲 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 香蒲 is its `stand_in` compound.
