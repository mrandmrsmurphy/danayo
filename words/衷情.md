## Notes
- [衷](../characters/衷.md) "inmost" + [情](../characters/情.md) "feelings"
- This word is the viable form of [衷](../characters/衷.md): the character's meaning (innermost, sincere) is identical, but 衷 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 衷情 is its `stand_in` compound.
