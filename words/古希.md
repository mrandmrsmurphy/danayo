## Notes
- [古](../characters/古.md) "ancient, old" + [希](../characters/希.md) "rare, hope"

From the classical proverb 人生七十古來稀 — "It has been rare since ancient times for people to live to seventy." The phrase alludes to the exceptional longevity of reaching age 70, drawn from Du Fu's poem 曲江.
