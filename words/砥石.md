## Notes
- [砥](../characters/砥.md) "whetstone" + [石 (char)](../characters/石%20(char).md) "rock"
- This word is the viable form of [砥](../characters/砥.md): the character's meaning (whetstone) is identical, but 砥 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 砥石 is its `stand_in` compound.
- Japanese reading といし uses native kun-yomi (と + いし); the on-reading concatenation would be しせき.
