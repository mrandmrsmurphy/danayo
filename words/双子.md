## Notes
- [双 (char)](../characters/双%20(char).md) "pair; double; even" + [子](../characters/子.md) "child; small suffix" — two children born at the same birth; a pair

The 子 suffix here functions as it does in 椅子 (chair) or 餃子 (dumpling): a noun-forming particle attached to a root that gains concreteness from it. 双子 (ふたご in Japanese native reading) is the natural word for twins across the Sinosphere. Identical twins are 一卵性双子, fraternal twins are 二卵性双子. In astrology, Gemini is 双子座.
