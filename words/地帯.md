## Notes
- [地 (char)](../characters/地%20(char).md) "land" + [帯 (char)](../characters/帯%20(char).md) "belt, sash" — a geographic zone or belt, as in 安全地帯 ("safety zone") or 心臓地帯 ("heartland"). The Vietnamese reading "địa đới" uses the same alternate reading of 帯 (đới rather than đái) seen in [[熱帯]] (nhiệt đới).
