## Notes
- [教](../characters/教.md) "teach" + [室](../characters/室.md) "room" — neither character's own `stand_in` points here (教's own points to `教授`, 室's own to `房室`); 教室 is an independent compound. `kwin: false` per the AND-rule: `characters/教.md` is `kwin: false`, `characters/室.md` is `kwin: true` → compound `false`.
- Mandarin jiàoshì, Cantonese gaau3 sat1, Japanese きょうしつ, and Korean 교실 all directly preserve the Sino compound as the standard, everyday word for "classroom." Vietnamese, however, does not use it — the real, standard modern term is the native construction lớp học or phòng học ("study room"); no independently attested Sino-Vietnamese reading of 教室 is in actual use, so `vietnamese` is left blank rather than fabricated.
