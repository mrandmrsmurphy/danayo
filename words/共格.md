## Notes
- [共 (char)](../characters/共%20(char).md) "together, with" + [格 (char)](../characters/格%20(char).md) "case (grammatical)" — the case marking accompaniment, "together with."

Note that the real Sino-Korean reading of this exact compound, 공격, is already firmly claimed by the everyday and unrelated word 攻擊/공격 ("attack, offense"), with no connection in meaning or derivation — the Korean reading shown above is the grammatical-case sense only, distinct from that homograph. Korean linguistics more often uses the fuller 공동격 (共同格) for this case to avoid exactly this ambiguity, which is one reason this entry's pedigree leans more on Mandarin and Japanese than on Korean.
