## Notes
- [逗](../characters/逗.md) "pause" + [留 (char)](../characters/留%20(char).md) "remain; stay"
- 逗留 is the stand-in word for [逗](../characters/逗.md): the character's meaning (to pause; to linger) is identical, but 逗 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 逗留 is its `stand_in` compound.
