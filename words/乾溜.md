## Notes
- [乾 (char)](../characters/乾%20(char).md) "dry" + [溜](../characters/溜.md) (stand-in for 餾, "distill") — dry distillation, pyrolysis (destructive distillation of solids, e.g. coal/wood, without water). Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, and Korean. Written 干溜 in Simplified Chinese; 乾 is the real character here, not 干 ("shield").
