## Notes
- [門](../characters/門.md) + [捷](../characters/捷.md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Mendeleev*, after chemist Dmitri Mendeleev, creator of the periodic table itself. 門捷 renders the first two syllables of 門捷列夫, the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Mendeleev," truncated rather than spelled out in full. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (メンデレビウム, 멘델레븀).
