## Notes
- [男](../characters/男.md) "male; man" + [爵](../characters/爵.md) "baron; noble rank"
- This word is the viable form of [爵](../characters/爵.md): the character's meaning (noble rank, baron) is identical, but 爵 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 男 ("male") specifies the gendered form of the title, making this the standard term for a baron (cf. 女爵 "baroness").
