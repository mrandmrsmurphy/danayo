## Notes
- [居](../characters/居.md) + [里 (char)](../characters/里%20(char).md) + [金 (char)](../characters/金%20(char).md) "metal" — a transliteration of *Curie*, after Marie and Pierre Curie. 居里 is the standard Mandarin transliteration of "Curie" (as in 居里夫人, "Madame Curie"), used here in full since it is already only two syllables. Japanese and Korean instead borrow the IUPAC name as a loanword (キュリウム, 퀴륨).
