## Notes
- [剝](../characters/剝.md) "peel" + [皮](../characters/皮.md) "skin"
- This word is the viable form of [剝](../characters/剝.md): the character's meaning (to peel) is identical, but 剝 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound is a verb-object construction — to peel (剝) the skin (皮).
- 皮 is itself bounded (its own `stand_in` is [[皮膚]]), lending itself here to 剝's legitimizer.
