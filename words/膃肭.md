## Notes
- [腽](../characters/腽.md) "fur seal" + [肭](../characters/肭.md) "fur seal"
- 膃肭 is the stand-in word for [腽](../characters/腽.md) ("fur seal"), which is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo. The term derives from Ainu *onnep* via phono-semantic matching.
