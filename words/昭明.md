## Notes
- [昭 (char)](../characters/昭%20(char).md) + [明 (char)](../characters/明%20(char).md) — a literary fixed compound, "shining and bright." Attested as a Mandarin/Cantonese dictionary headword distinct from the two characters merely standing side by side; also the basis of 昭明太子/昭明文選 (Crown Prince Zhaoming, the Liang-dynasty literary anthology). No Japanese or Korean dictionary entry found for the combined form, so those fields are left blank.
