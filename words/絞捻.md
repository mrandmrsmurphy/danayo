## Notes
- [絞](../characters/絞.md) "twist" + [捻 (char)](../characters/捻%20(char).md) "pinch; spin"
- This word is the viable form of [絞](../characters/絞.md): the character's meaning (to twist, turn) is identical, but 絞 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 絞捻 is its `stand_in` compound.
