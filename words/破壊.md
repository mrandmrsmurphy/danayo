## Notes
- [破](../characters/破%20(char).md) "break, rend" + [壊](../characters/壊.md) "demolish, ruin"
- This word is the stand-in for [[壊]], which is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo.
- A compound denoting forceful destruction or demolition. In Japanese, はかい (hakai) is the standard term covering destruction by natural disaster and deliberate sabotage alike; compounds include 環境破壊 "environmental destruction" and 破壊工作 "sabotage." In Chinese, 破坏 (pòhuài) skews toward deliberate damage or undermining — total annihilation uses 摧毀. The Korean 파괴 (破壞) and Vietnamese phá hoại inherit the same Sino-Vietnamese roots, with phá hoại carrying a particular connotation of wartime sabotage or subversion.
