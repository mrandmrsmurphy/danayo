## Notes
- [徽](../characters/徽.md) "badge; insignia" + [章 (char)](../characters/章%20(char).md) "chapter; emblem"
- This word is the viable form of [徽](../characters/徽.md): the character's meaning (badge, insignia) is identical, but 徽 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs it with 章 ("emblem; mark"), reinforcing the meaning.
- Japanese reads きしょう (KI+SHOU), the same reading as 記章 "decoration; medal."
