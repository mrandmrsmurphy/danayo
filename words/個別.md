## Notes
- [個 (char)](../characters/個%20(char).md) "individual; counter for objects" + [別 (char)](../characters/別%20(char).md) "other, distinct, separate"

個別 means "individual, separate, respective" — treating each item or person on its own terms rather than as part of a group (個別指導, "individual tutoring"; 個別対応, "case-by-case handling"). 個, a 量詞 (measure word/classifier), is the all-purpose counter for discrete objects in Mandarin, Cantonese, and (as こ, ko) Japanese — its core sense of "a single, discrete unit" combines with 別's "distinctness" (already seen in [[別字]], the previous word in this batch, and [[区別]]/[[離別]] from [[別 (char)]]'s Words section) to produce "each [unit], separately."

個別 is a useful structural counterpart to [[全部]] (cross-linked from [[部 (char)]]'s Words section, "all, the whole"): where 全部 treats a group as an undifferentiated totality, 個別 insists on examining its members one at a time. The compound is common across all administrative and educational registers in Mandarin, Cantonese, and Japanese wherever a process needs to distinguish "handling everyone the same way" from "handling each case on its own merits."
