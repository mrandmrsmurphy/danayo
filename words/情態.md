## Notes
- [情](../characters/情.md) "feeling" + [態](../characters/態.md) "manner"

1. (Linguistics) **grammatical mood** — the system of verb forms expressing the speaker's stance toward what is said: indicative, subjunctive, imperative, conditional, etc.
2. (Linguistics) **modality** — the broader domain of modal meaning: possibility, necessity, permission, obligation; the semantic field of modal verbs (情態動詞)
3. (Linguistics) **voice** — active, passive, middle — the relationship between verb and its arguments
4. **state; condition** — the general state or condition of a thing or situation
5. **emotional mood; feeling** — one's inner affective state at a given moment
6. **demeanor; mien; bearing** — the outward expression of one's character or mood
