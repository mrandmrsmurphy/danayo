## Notes
- [菲](../characters/菲.md) "meager, humble" + [薄](../characters/薄.md) "thin, weak" — meager, humble; as a verb, to belittle or think little of (oneself or one's own contribution). Attested in Mandarin and Cantonese; underlies the chengyu 妄自菲薄 ("to demean oneself unjustifiably"). No confirmed independent Japanese, Korean, or Vietnamese usage, so those fields are left blank.
