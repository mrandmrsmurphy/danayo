## Notes
- [熱 (char)](../characters/熱%20(char).md) "hot" + [帯 (char)](../characters/帯%20(char).md) "belt, zone" — the tropics, or "torrid zone": the geographic band around the equator characterized by year-round heat. The term was coined by Matteo Ricci in his 1602 world map (坤輿萬國全圖) and spread across the Sinosphere from there.
