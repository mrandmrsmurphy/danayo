## Notes
- [企](../characters/企.md) "plan; scheme" + [劃 (char)](../characters/劃%20(char).md) "mark off; delimit"
- This word is the viable form of [企](../characters/企.md): the character's meaning (to plan, scheme) is identical, but 企 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 劃 ("to demarcate; lay out") adds the sense of defining the scope of a plan.
- Japanese standard form is 企画 (きかく), with the same reading.
