## Notes
- [主](../characters/主.md) "master; host; main" + [要](../characters/要.md) "want; need; important" — an independent compound; neither character's own `stand_in` points here (主's is `主人`, 要's is `重要`)

Mandarin zhǔyào, Cantonese zyu2 jiu3, Japanese しゅよう (shuyō), Korean 주요 (juyo), and Vietnamese chủ yếu are all the standard, everyday word for "main, principal, central" across the sphere (主要な原因/주요 원인, "the main cause"; nguyên nhân chủ yếu). No native displacement or homophone collision found — a clean compound with directly parallel usage in all five languages.
