## Notes
- [北](../characters/北.md) "north" + [美 (char)](../characters/美%20(char).md) "America" — North America, the shortened form of [[北美洲]] omitting 洲 ("continent"), as commonly used in compounds like 北美自由貿易協定 ("NAFTA"). Japanese instead abbreviates with a different character, 北米 (hokubei, using 米 rather than 美), so no 日本語 reading is given here.
