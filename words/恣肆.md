## Notes
- [恣](../characters/恣.md) "selfish, arbitrary" + [肆](../characters/肆.md) "act brazenly"
- This word is the viable form of [肆](../characters/肆.md): the character's meaning (act brazenly; unrestrained) is identical, but 肆 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 恣肆 is its `stand_in` compound.
