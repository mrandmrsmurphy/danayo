## Notes
- From [[棲]] "nest/roost" + [[息]] "breath/rest": the place where a creature breathes and settles — its habitat. The full compound 棲息 (栖息 in simplified Chinese, せいそく in Japanese) is widely shared across all five source languages as a verb meaning "to inhabit" or "to dwell" (of animals). Korean 서식 is the direct Sino-Korean reading.
- Stand-in for the bound character [[棲]].
