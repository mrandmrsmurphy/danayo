## Notes
- [幇](../characters/幇.md) "help" + [助](../characters/助.md) "assist"
- This word is the viable form of [幇](../characters/幇.md): the character's meaning (to help) is identical, but 幇 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. The compound pairs it with a near-synonym 助 to make it usable.
