## Notes
- [江](../characters/江.md) "river" + [戸](../characters/戸.md) "door" + [川 (char)](../characters/川%20(char).md) "river"
- 江戸川 is the Edo River, a river in the Kantō region of Japan forming part of the border between Tokyo and Chiba Prefecture.
- Note: The Japanese name reads えどがわ (Edogawa) — a fully native compound where 戸 is the historical name for Edo (present-day Tokyo) and 川 is the native Japanese word for river (gawa). The Dan'a'yo reading ㄍㄚㄫㄏㄛㄑ⺢ㄋ derives instead from the Middle Chinese phonological values of each character, and bears no resemblance to the Japanese.
