## Notes
- [季](../characters/季.md) + [秋 (char)](../characters/秋%20(char).md)
Third and final month of autumn. Chrysanthemums are in full bloom; the Double Ninth Festival (重陽節) falls here. Corresponds to [[戌月]] and [[菊月]].
