## Notes
1. The way to say **御** in Dan'a'yo, one of the [[六芸]].
- **鳴和鸞 (_Míng hé luán_):** Making the bells on the chariot and the horse's bridle ring in perfect harmony.
- **逐水曲 (_Zhú shuǐ qū_):** Driving along a curving shoreline without falling in.
- **過君表 (_Guò jūn biǎo_):** Driving past the lord’s ceremonial gate with precision.
- **舞交衢 (_Wǔ jiāo qú_):** Maneuvering the chariot through a busy intersection as if performing a dance.
- **逐禽左 (_Zhú qín zuǒ_):** Chasing a quarry so that the hunter (usually on the right) could easily shoot from the left.
