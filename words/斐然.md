## Notes
- [斐](../characters/斐.md) "graceful" + [然 (char)](../characters/然%20(char).md) "so"
- This word is the viable form of [斐](../characters/斐.md): the character's meaning (graceful, elegant) is identical, but 斐 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 斐然 is its `stand_in` compound.
