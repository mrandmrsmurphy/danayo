## Notes
- [南](../characters/南.md) "south" + [美 (char)](../characters/美%20(char).md) "America" — South America, the shortened form of [[南美洲]] omitting 洲 ("continent"). Japanese instead abbreviates with a different character, 南米 (nanbei, using 米 rather than 美), so no 日本語 reading is given here.
