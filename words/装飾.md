## Notes
- [装 (char)](../characters/装%20(char).md) "dress up, clothe" + [飾](../characters/飾.md) "decorate" — stand-in for [[飾]], which cannot appear independently.

装 and 裝 are simplified/traditional variants of the same character with identical readings in every language, so unlike most words in this batch there is no phonetic divergence to reconcile. 裝飾/装飾 (zhuāngshì) is the general word for "decoration" or "ornament" — anything added to an object, room, or person to make it more attractive, from architectural detail to jewelry and clothing accessories. The Vietnamese trang sức has narrowed in common usage to mean "jewelry" specifically, while the Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, and Korean forms retain the broader sense of decoration in general.

Because 装 and 飾 are the vault's own characters and 装/裝 are true variants, the 羅馬字, 諺文, 注音, mandarin, cantonese, japanese, korean, and vietnamese fields are all a straightforward concatenation of 装's and 飾's own values, with no divergence adjustment needed.
