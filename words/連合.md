## Notes
- [連 (char)](../characters/連%20(char).md) "connected; mutual; in series" + [合 (char)](../characters/合%20(char).md) "to join; to fit together; to combine"

連合 is "to unite; to ally with; alliance; union" — 連 (connected, mutual, linked in series) + 合 (to join, to fit together, to combine). The compound joins two complementary joining concepts: 連 emphasises continuity of connection (things strung together, remaining linked), while 合 emphasises the act of convergence (things brought together into one). As a Dan'a'yo 事詞, 連合 functions as a verbal noun — both "to ally/unite" as a verb and "an alliance/union" as a noun, following the Japanese and Korean pattern of Sino-Japanese compound-verb-nouns operating freely in both grammatical roles.

Mandarin 聯合 (liánhé; 聯合 is the standard form in Mandarin, with 聯 preferred over 連) covers a wide range: 聯合國 (United Nations), 聯合聲明 (joint statement), 聯合行動 (joint action). In Dan'a'yo, 連 absorbs 聯 — the 連 character file notes this merger explicitly, following Japanese orthographic practice: Dan'a'yo writes 連合 (not 聯合), 連盟 (not 聯盟), 国連 (not 國聯). Cantonese 連合 (lin4 hap6). Japanese 連合 (れんごう, rengō) is widely used: 連合国 (the Allied Powers / United Nations), 連合艦隊 (Combined Fleet — the Imperial Japanese Navy's main battle formation in WWII). Korean 연합 (連合, yeonhap): 연합군 (Allied Forces), 연합국 (Allied Nations). Vietnamese liên hiệp (Sino-Vietnamese: 連=liên, 合=hiệp).

kwin is false: Dan'a'yo 련곱 diverges from Korean 연합 in both syllables. 連 reads 련 (lyen/ㄌ⼶ㄋ) in Dan'a'yo but 연 (yeon) in modern South Korean — the initial ㄹ drops before ㅕ by the 頭音法則 (initial-sound rule), which prohibits ㄹ- and ㄴ- in syllable-initial position before certain vowels in South Korean. 合 reads 곱 (gob/ㄍㄛㄆ) in Dan'a'yo but 합 (hap) in Sino-Korean — MC initial k (見) before the ʌp final gives Dan'a'yo ㄍ (g-), while Sino-Korean gives ㅎ (h-) through a different development path for MC 見 before back rounded vowels. The aliases 聯合 and 联合 are the Mandarin-preferred and PRC-simplified forms respectively; 连合 is the PRC-simplified 連合.
