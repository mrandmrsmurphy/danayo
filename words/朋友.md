## Notes
- [朋](../characters/朋.md) "friend, acquaintance" + [友](../characters/友.md) "friend" — both characters' own `stand_in` fields point here (a #cranberry double bound-morpheme case, like [[建設]]): neither 朋 nor 友 stands independently outside this compound. `kwin: false` per the AND-rule: `characters/朋.md` is `kwin: true`, `characters/友.md` is `kwin: false` → compound `false`.
- 朋友 is one of the 天常 (the constant relationships of Confucian ethics, also called 五倫 "Five Relationships"): 朋友有信, "between friends, there should be trust" — the fifth and final of the Five Relationships, the only one not defined by hierarchy.
- `korean` corrected: previously comma-jammed `붕우, 친구` — 붕우 is the genuine Sino-Korean reading of this compound (though formal/literary in modern usage), while 친구 is a different, unrelated native/hybrid word (親舊) that happens to be the everyday Korean word for "friend" — not a reading of 朋友 itself, so removed from the field. Likewise, Japanese ほうゆう is a real but formal/literary reading; everyday Japanese instead says 友達/友人.
