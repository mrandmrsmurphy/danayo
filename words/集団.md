## Notes
- [集](../characters/集.md) "collect" + [団](../characters/団.md) "group"
- This word is the viable form of [団](../characters/団.md): the character's meaning (group) is identical, but 団 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 集団 is its `stand_in` compound.
