**基礎** (foundation, fundamentals):

- The underlying principles, elementary knowledge, or essential prerequisites for understanding or building something
- Focus: _conceptual/educational_ foundation
- Examples: 基礎知識 (basic knowledge), 基礎教育 (foundational education), 基礎理論 (fundamental theory), 基礎科學 (basic science)
- Metaphor: the bedrock of understanding

**[[基盤]]** (base, platform, infrastructure):

- The structural support system, material infrastructure, or organized framework that enables activity or development
- Focus: _systemic/structural_ foundation
- Examples: 經濟基盤 (economic base/infrastructure), 社會基盤 (social infrastructure), 技術基盤 (technological platform), 生活基盤 (livelihood foundation)
- Metaphor: the platform you stand on

**Distinguishing test**:

- 基礎: "What do I need to _learn/understand_ first?"
- 基盤: "What _system/structure_ must be in place?"
