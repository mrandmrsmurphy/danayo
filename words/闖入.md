## Notes
- [闖](../characters/闖.md) "intrude" + [入 (char)](../characters/入%20(char).md) "enter"
- 闖入 is the stand-in word for [闖](../characters/闖.md): the character's meaning (to intrude; to barge in) is identical, but 闖 alone is too bounded to stand as a Dan'a'yo word. 闖入 is its `stand_in` compound.
