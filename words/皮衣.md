## Notes
- [皮](../characters/皮.md) "skin, hide" + [衣](../characters/衣.md) "clothes" — a garment made of fur or hide.

裘 (qiú), "fur robe, fur coat," is an obscure character appearing 6 times in the 《論語》, most notably in **鄉黨** describing the furs worn by Confucius according to season and occasion (e.g. 緇衣羔裘, 素衣麑裘, 黃衣狐裘). 裘 lies outside Dan'a'yo's closed character set, so the vault substitutes 皮衣 — joining 衣, whose meaning "clothes, garment" supplies the translation, with 皮, the "skin, hide, fur" component describing what the garment is made of. This is a "Translation + Component" substitution per [[translation/論語 (book)]]'s Vocabulary list.

The Japanese reading かわごろも (lit. "hide robe") is the natural native compound for fur clothing, distinct from the on-yomi readings of the individual characters. The Korean 피의 and the mandarin/cantonese readings píyī/pei4 ji1 follow directly from 皮 and 衣's own readings.
