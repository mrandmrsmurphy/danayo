## Notes
- [絢](../characters/絢.md) "gorgeous, variegated" + [乱](../characters/乱.md) "chaotic, riotous"
- This word is the stand-in for [[絢]], which is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo.
- Describes brilliant, dazzling visual display — elaborate patterns, vivid costumes, floral excess. The standard Japanese compound is 絢爛 (けんらん), which uses 爛 "gleaming"; Dan'a'yo substitutes 乱 (ㄌㄚㄋ) for its phonetic match. Korean 현란 (絢爛) carries connotations of gaudiness as well as splendor — a word that can praise or criticize depending on context. Chinese 絢爛/絢麗 (xuànlì) tends toward the purely positive: the brilliant hues of a sunset, a resplendent tapestry.
