## Notes
- [貫](../characters/貫.md) "to pierce, to string together" + [通 (char)](../characters/通%20(char).md) "to pass through"
- This word is the viable form of [貫](../characters/貫.md): the character means to pierce or thread through, but is too bounded to stand alone in Dan'a'yo. 貫通 is its `stand_in` compound.
