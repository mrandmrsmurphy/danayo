## Notes
- [太 (char)](../characters/太%20(char).md) "great" + [易](../characters/易.md) "easy"

In this compound 易 carries the sense of "change" rather than "easy". 太易 is the first of four primordial stages in the _Liezi_ (列子) cosmogony — the state before even qi (氣) has appeared. The sequence runs: [[太易]] → [[太初]] → [[太始]] → [[太素]]. See also [[天地]], [[混沌]].
