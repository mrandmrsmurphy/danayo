## Notes


## Ancient
As one of the [[六芸]], there were 
- **吉禮 (_Jílǐ_):** Sacrificial rites for gods and ancestors (Auspicious Rites).
- **凶禮 (_Xiōnglǐ_):** Funeral and mourning rites, or rituals for disasters like famine (Ominous Rites).
- **賓禮 (_Bīnlǐ_):** Etiquette for hosting guests, diplomats, or feudal lords (Guest Rites).
- **軍禮 (_Jūnlǐ_):** Military protocols, including troop movements and victory celebrations (Army Rites).
- **嘉禮 (_Jiālǐ_):** Rites for weddings, births, and coming-of-age ceremonies (Festive Rites).
