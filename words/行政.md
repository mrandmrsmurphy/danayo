## Notes
- [行 (char)](../characters/行%20(char).md) "go, conduct" + [政](../characters/政.md) "government" — administration; the executive branch of government. Attested across Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese (ぎょうせい), Korean (행정), and Vietnamese (hành chính). Forms compounds such as 行政[[区域]] ("administrative region") and 行政官 ("administrator, official").
