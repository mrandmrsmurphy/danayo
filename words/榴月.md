## Notes
- [榴](../characters/榴.md) + [月 (char)](../characters/月%20(char).md)
Fifth month of the ancient Chinese poetic calendar. Pomegranate flowers (榴花) burst open in vivid scarlet in early summer. The fifth month contains the Duanwu Festival (端午節), and the brilliant red of pomegranate blossoms was said to ward off evil — a natural match for the apotropaic spirit of that festival.
