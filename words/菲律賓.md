## Notes
- [菲](../characters/菲.md) + [律](../characters/律.md) + [賓](../characters/賓.md) — a Mandarin/Cantonese phonetic transcription of "Philippines." Japanese and Korean instead use the katakana/hangul loans フィリピン/필리핀 rather than this kanji/hanja form (Wiktionary marks 菲律賓 as an obsolete Japanese spelling), so no on'yomi/Sino-Korean reading is given here. Vietnamese retains the older Sino-Vietnamese name Phi Luật Tân. The alternate spelling 比律賓 is also attested.
