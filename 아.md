**·ㄚ/아/'a** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] which means "I/me" (我) or the ideophone "ah" (阿).  In advanced situations it can mean "mute" (唖) or a "moth" (蛾).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters
### Common
- [[我]] I, me
- [[阿]] ah, {determiner for names}
- [[亜]] Asia, secondary; but requires <ruby>亜細亜<rt>·ㄚㄒㄝㄨ·ㄚ</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[唖]] mute
### Naming
- [[蛾]] moth
