**김** is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "now" (今) or "gold" (金).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters
### Common
- [[今]] - "now"
- [[金]] - "gold, metal"
- [[禁]] - forbid, but requires <ruby>禁止<rt>김지</rt></ruby>
- ### Advanced
- [[襟]] - "collar", but requires <ruby>衣襟<rt>의김</rt></ruby>
### Naming
none
