**·ㄛㄨ/옷/'ou** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] which means nothing on its own.  With introduction, it also means "seagull" (鴎).  For other uses, see below
## Characters
### Common
- [[偶]] - idol, image, but requires <ruby>偶像<rt>·ㄛㄨㄒㄞㄫ</rt></ruby>
- [[殴]] - assault, but requires <ruby>殴打<rt>·ㄛㄨㄉㄚ</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[呕]] - vomit, but requires <ruby>呕吐<rt>·ㄛㄨㄊㄛ</rt></ruby>
- [[𧦅]] - eulogize, but requires 𧦅歌
### Naming
- [[鴎]] - seagull
