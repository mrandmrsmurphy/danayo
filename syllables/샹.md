## Dan'a'yo
**syang** means "top, above" [[上]], "still, more" [[尚]], or "to award, reward" [[賞]]. For other uses in compounds, see below.
### Forms
*Roman*: **syang** or **syaŋ**; *IPA*: /ʃjaŋ/
### Characters
- [[上]] (n) - *above, top, over*
- [[尚]] (adv) - *still, even more*
- [[賞]] (v.tr) - *to reward, grant, bestow*
- [[傷]] (v.tr) - *to wound, injure* - **requires** <ruby>傷害<rt>샹해</rt></ruby>
- [[像]] (n) - *statue* - **requires** <ruby>彫像<rt>촛샹</rt></ruby>
- [[償]] (v.tr) - *to repay, recompense* - **requires** <ruby>報償<rt>밧샹</rt></ruby>
- [[商]] (n) - *commerce* - **requires** <ruby>商業<rt>샹업</rt></ruby>
- [[常]] (adj) - *common, normal, frequent, regular, often* - **requires** <ruby>恒常<rt>훙샹</rt></ruby>
- [[象]] (n) - *elephant* - **requires** <ruby>大象<rt>대샹</rt></ruby>

#### Advanced 
- [[翔]] (v.intr) - *fly* - **requires** <ruby>飛翔<rt>피샹</rt></ruby>
- [[裳]] (n) - *clothes, skirt* - **requires** <ruby>衣裳<rt>의샹</rt></ruby>
- [[嘗]] (n) - *taste, experience, experiment* - **requires** <ruby>嘗試<rt>샹시</rt></ruby>

#### Name 
- [[湘]] (n) - *Xiang* - must be part of a name only
- [[㐮]] (v) - *help, assist* - must be part of a name only
