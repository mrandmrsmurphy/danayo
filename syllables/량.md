**ㄌㄞㄫ/량/lyang** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "both" (両) or "provisions" (糧).  In Advanced contexts, it may also mean "girder" (梁).  For other uses in compounds, see below
## Characters
### Common
- [[両]] - both
- [[糧]] - provisions
- [[涼]] - refreshing, but requires <ruby>清涼<rt>ㄑㄧㄫㄌㄞㄫ</rt></ruby>
- [[良]] - kind, but requires <ruby>良好<rt>ㄌㄞㄫㄏㄚㄨ</rt></ruby>
- [[量]] - measure, but requires <ruby>数量<rt>ㄒㄨㄌㄞㄫ</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[梁]] - girder
- [[諒]] - excuse, forgive, but requires <ruby>諒解<rt>ㄌㄞㄫㄍㄚㄧ</rt></ruby>
### Naming
- [[椋]] - starling, requires <ruby>椋鳥<rt>ㄌㄞㄫㄑㄛㄨ</rt></ruby>
