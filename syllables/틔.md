**틔**/tii is a syllable in Dan'a'yo meaning "to withdraw" (退).  For other uses in compounds, see below.

## Pronunciation

- In Pinyin, it is close to *tie*.
- In Korean, it would be closest to 티으, but said as one syllable.
- In Japanese, it would something be like チー/ちー, but without the affricate
- In Vietnamese, it is something like *thia*, *thiê*, *thiêu*, or *thươu*.
