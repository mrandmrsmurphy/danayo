**줏**/juu is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "let" (就) or "eternity" (宙) or "state" (州).  In certain situations, it can mean "islet" (洲).  For other uses in compounds, see below.

## Characters
### Common
- [[酒]] alcohol --> <ruby>[[酒精]]<rt>줏정</rt></ruby>
- [[調]] to transfer --> <ruby>[[調整]]<rt>줏정</rt></ruby>
- [[就]] "let"
- [[昼]] daytime --> <ruby>[[昼間]]<rt>줏간</rt></ruby>
- [[宙]] eternity
- [[周]] circumference --> <ruby>[[圓周]]<rt>원줏</rt></ruby>
- [[州]] state
- [[週]] week, cycle --> <ruby>[[週日]]<rt>줏닏</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[呪]] spell, curse --> <ruby>[[呪詛]]<rt>줏조</rt></ruby>
- [[洲]] islet
### Names
- [[鷲]] vulture, eagle --> <ruby>[[鷲鳥]]<rt>줏촛</rt></ruby>
- [[酎]] saké, soju --> <ruby>[[焼酎]]<rt>숏줏</rt></ruby>
