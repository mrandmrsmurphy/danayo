**'an** is a [[Syllables|syllable]] mean "bank, shore" (岸).  In advanced situations, it can mean "counterfeit" (贋).  It is also the name of "wild geese" (雁).  For other uses in compounds see below.
## Characters
### Common
- [[岸 (char)]] "bank, shore", [[岸]]
- [[眼]] "eyeball, pupil", but requires <ruby>[[眼球]]<rt>ㄚㄋㄍ·ㄨ</rt></ruby>
- [[案]] "propose", but requires [[提案]]
- [[安]] "peaceful", but requires [[平安]]
- [[恩]] "kindness, mercy", but requires [[恩恵]]
- [[顔]] "face, reputation]], but requires [[顔面]]
### Advanced
- [[贋 (char)]] "counterfeit, false" [[贋]]
- [[按]] "put hands on", but requires [[按摩]]
- [[鞍]] "saddle", but requires [[鞍裝]]
### Naming
- [[雁 (char)]] "wild goose", [[雁]]
- [[鴦]] "mandarin duck", but requires [[鴛鴦]]
