**˙ㄛ/'yo/요** is a <ruby>[[文言現代]]<rt>ㄇㄨㄋㄝㄋㄏㄧㄝㄋㄉㄚㄧ</rt></ruby> [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "and" (与), or "fish" (魚).  It may mean, with introduction, "palanquin" (輿).  For other uses in compounds, see below.

- [[余]] "surplus", but requires <ruby>[[剰余]]<rt>ㄐㄧㄥ·˙ㄛ</rt></ruby>
