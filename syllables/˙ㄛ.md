**⼄/'yo/요** is a <ruby>単亜語<rt>ㄉㄚㄋ·ㄚ·⼄</rt></ruby> [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "and" (与), or "fish" (魚).  It may mean, with introduction, "palanquin" (輿).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
