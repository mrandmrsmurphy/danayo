**취** is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "to cook" (炊) or "to push" (推) or "to blow/puff" (吹).  For other uses, see below.

### Forms
*Roman*: cui; *IPA*: /t͡ɕʰɥi/
### Characters
- [[炊]] "to cook"
- [[推]] "to push"
- [[吹]] "to blow"

#### Advanced
* [[錘]] "spindle", but requires <ruby>[[紡錘]]<rt>팡취</rt></ruby>
* [[聚]] "to assemble", but requires <ruby>[[聚集]]<rt>취집</rt></ruby>
 
#### Name
* [[嘴]] "bill, beak", but requires <ruby>[[鳥嘴]]<rt>촛취</rt></ruby>
* [[脆]] "brittle, fragile", but names only
* [[翠]] "blue green, emerald", but requires <ruby>[[翠色]]<rt>취식</rt></ruby>
* [[椎]] "vertebrae, spine", but requires <ruby>[[脊椎]]<rt>적취</rt></ruby>
