**г„‘г„ЁЛҷ/м·Ё/cui** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "to cook" (зӮҠ) or "to push" (жҺЁ) or "to blow/puff" (еҗ№).  For other uses, see below.
## Characters
- [[зӮҠ]] "to cook"
- [[жҺЁ]] "to push"
- [[еҗ№]] "to blow"
### Advanced
* [[йҢҳ]] "spindle", but requires <ruby>[[зҙЎйҢҳ]]<rt>г„Ҷг„ҡг„Ҙг„‘г„ЁЛҷ</rt></ruby>
* [[иҒҡ]] "to assemble", but requires <ruby>[[иҒҡйӣҶ]]<rt>г„‘г„ЁЛҷг„җг„§г„Ҷ</rt></ruby>
### Name
* [[еҳҙ]] "bill, beak", but requires <ruby>[[йіҘеҳҙ]]<rt>г„‘г„ӣЛҮг„‘г„ЁЛҷ</rt></ruby>
* [[и„Ҷ]] "brittle, fragile", but names only
* [[зҝ ]] "blue green, emerald", but requires <ruby>[[зҝ иүІ]]<rt>г„‘г„ЁЛҷг„ҷг„§г„Һ</rt></ruby>
* [[жӨҺ]] "vertebrae, spine", but requires <ruby>[[и„ҠжӨҺ]]<rt>г„җг„қг„Һг„‘г„ЁЛҷ</rt></ruby>
