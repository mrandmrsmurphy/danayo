**바** is a Dan'a'yo syllable without any intrinsic meaning.  There are several characters with this pronunciation, but none of which that can stand alone.  It appears in many other words as a component.

### Forms
*Roman*: ba; *IPA*: /ba/
### Characters
- [[婆]] (noun) - '(paternal) grammy',  requires <ruby>[[婆婆]]<rt>바바</rt></ruby>
- [[把]] (noun) - 'grip, handle', requires <ruby>[[把手]]<rt>바슈</rt></ruby>
- [[波]] (noun) - 'wave', requires <ruby>[[波浪]]<rt>바랑</rt></ruby>
- [[播]] (v.tr) - 'sow, spread', requires <ruby>[[播種]]<rt>바종</rt></ruby>
#### Advanced
* [[覇]] (adv. noun) - 'hegemony', requires <ruby>[[覇権]]<rt>바권</rt></ruby>
* [[爬]] (adv. v.intr) - 'crawl', requires <ruby>[[爬行]]<rt>바항</rt></ruby>
#### Name
* [[琶]] (name, noun) - 'zither', requires <ruby>[[琵琶]]<rt>비바</rt></ruby>
* [[芭]] (name, noun) - 'Japanese banana', requires <ruby>[[芭蕉]]<rt>바좃</rt></ruby>
* [[擺]] (name, noun) - 'pendulum', but use <ruby>[[振子]]<rt>진지</rt></ruby>
