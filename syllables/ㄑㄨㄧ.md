**ㄑㄨㄧ/취/cui** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "to cook" (炊) or "to push" (推) or "to blow/puff" (吹).  For other uses, see below.
## Characters
- [[炊]] "to cook"
- [[推]] "to push"
- [[吹]] "to blow"

* [[聚]] "to assemble", but requires <ruby>[[聚集]]<rt>ㄑㄨㄧㄐㄧㄆ</rt></ruby>
### Name
* [[嘴]] "bill, beak", but requires <ruby>[[鳥嘴]]<rt>ㄑㄛㄨㄑㄨㄧ</rt></ruby>
* [[脆]] "brittle, fragile", but names only
* [[翠]] "blue green, emerald", but requires <ruby>[[翠色]]<rt>ㄑㄨㄧㄙㄧㄎ</rt></ruby>
* [[椎]] "vertebrae, spine", but requires <ruby>[[脊椎]]<rt>ㄐㄝㄎㄑㄨㄧ</rt></ruby>
