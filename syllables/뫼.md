**뫼** is a dan'a'yo syllable meaning "sheets (measure word)" (枚) or "each" (毎).  In advanced or special circumstances, it can mean "soot" (煤).  For other uses in compounds, see below.

## Characters
### Common
- [[枚]] - "sheet" (measure word)
- [[毎]] - each
- [[妹]] - younger sister, but requires <ruby>[[細妹]]<rt>세뫼</rt></ruby>
- [[媒]] - mediator, but requires <ruby>[[仲媒]]<rt>둥뫼</rt></ruby>
- [[梅]] - plum blossom, but requires <ruby>[[梅花]]<rt>뫼화</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[煤]] - soot
