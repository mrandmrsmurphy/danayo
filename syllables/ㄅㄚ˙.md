**ㄅㄚ˙/배/bai** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] that means "ugh" (唄) or "-times, -fold" (倍).  For advanced users, it can also mean "playing card, tile" (牌).  It may also appear in polysyllabic words with other means.  See below.
