**ㄅㄚ˙/배/bai** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] that means "ugh" (唄) or "-times, -fold" (倍).  For advanced users, it can also mean "playing card, tile" (牌).  It may also appear in polysyllabic words with other means.  See below.

## Characters
### Common
- [[唄]] - "ugh" (SFP)
- [[倍]] - "times" (class)
- [[貝]] - "shellfish", but requires <ruby>[[貝類]]<rt>ㄅㄚ˙ㄌㄨ˙</rt></ruby>
- [[罷]] - "quit", but requires <ruby>[[罷官]]<rt>ㄅㄚ˙ㄍˇㄚㄋ</rt></ruby>
- [[敗]] - "be defeated", but requires <ruby>[[失敗]]<rt>ㄙㄧㄊㄅㄚ˙</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[牌]] - "playing card, tile"
### Name
- [[狽]] - "werewolf", but requires <ruby>狼狽<rt>ㄌㄚㄥㄅㄚ˙</rt></ruby>
