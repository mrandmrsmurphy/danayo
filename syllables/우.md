**우** is a [[Syllables|syllable]] in Dan'a'yo that means "rain" (雨), "stupid" (愚), the dative case (于), or "to meet" (遇).  For other uses in compounds, see below

## Pronunciation
우 is identical to the Korean syllable *우*, and the Pinyin spelling *wu*.  Japanese speakers should try very hard to articulate it, not as う, but as /u/.  Vietnamese *U* is essentially correct.
