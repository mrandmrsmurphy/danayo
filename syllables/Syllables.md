- ˇ on-glide may not have a ˇ off-glide, except ˇㄚˇ.
- ˇ on-glide may not go with an initial labial (ㄅ, ㄆ, or ㄇ)
- No ㄝ-vowel may have a ˇ off-glide
- ˙ on-glide may not have a ˙ off-glide, except for velars (ㄍ, ㄎ, ㄥ, ㄏ) + ㄚ/ㄝ and ㄙ˙ㄝ˙
- ㄉ and ㄊ may not have a ˙ or ˇ on-glide, except for the ㄉˇㄝ-series
- ㄐ and ㄑ may not have a ˙ or ˇ on-glide, except for the ㄐˇㄚ and ㄐˇㄝ-series
- ㄋ and ㄌ may not have a ˇ on-glide


Many, many examples with final -ㄆ,-ㄊ,-ㄎ,-ㄋ,-ㄇ, -ㄥ do not exist, but are not technically forbidden.
