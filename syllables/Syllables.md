- w on-glide generally does not have a w off-glide.
- w on-glide may not go with an initial labial (г„…, г„Ҷ, or г„Ү)
- No e-type-vowel may have a w off-glide
- г„ү and г„Ҡ may not have a y or w on-glide, except for the г„үвј”-series
- г„җ and г„‘ may not have a y or w on-glide, except for the г„җвәў and г„җвј”-series
- г„Ӣ and г„Ң may not have a w on-glide


Many, many examples with final -г„Ҷ,-г„Ҡ,-г„Һ,-г„Ӣ,-г„Ү, -г„« do not exist, but are not technically forbidden.
