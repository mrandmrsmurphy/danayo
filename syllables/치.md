**치** is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "tooth" (歯) or "to put, to place" (置).  It may also mean "pennant" (幟).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters

- [[歯]] - (n) tooth
- [[置]] - (v) put, place
- [[恥]] - (n) shame --> [[恥辱]]
- [[治]] - (v) govern --> [[統治]]
- [[痴]] - (n) fool, idiot --> [[痴漢]]
- [[辞]] - (v) resign --> [[辞職]]
### Advanced

- [[祉]] - well-being --> [[福祉]]
