** 'o ** means 五/*five*, 於/*in, at*, 汚/dirty, or sometimes 伍/*troops*.
### Forms
- Roman: 'o 
- IPA: /o/
- Kana: オ
- Chữ: ô
### Common
- [[五]] (num) five
- [[於]] (post) in, at
- [[汚]] (adj) "dirty"

- [[烏]] (noun) "crow, raven" - **requires** <ruby>[[烏鳥]]<rt>오촛</rt></ruby>
- [[嗚]] (v.intr) "sob, wimper" - **requires** <ruby>[[嗚咽]]<rt>오언</rt></ruby>
