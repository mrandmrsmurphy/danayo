**jam** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "silkworm" (蚕) or "to cut" (斬).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters
### Common
1. [[蚕]] - (n) silkworm
2. [[斬]] - (v) cut, chop
3. [[暫]] - temporary --> [[暫時]]
### Advanced
- [[慙]] ashamed --> [[慙愧]]
### Naming
- [[站]] - station (name only)
