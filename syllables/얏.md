**얏** is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "to bite/gnaw" (咬).  For other uses, see below.

### Forms
*Roman*: 'yau; *IPA*: /jau/
### Characters
- [[妖]] "goblin, monster", but requires <ruby>[[妖怪]]<rt>얏괘</rt></ruby>
- [[咬]] "to bite, gnaw"
#### Advanced
* none
#### Name
* [[尭]] "lofty", but for names only
