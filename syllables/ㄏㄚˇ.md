**ㄏㄚˇ** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "good" (好).  In advanced situations, it can mean "fine hair" (毫).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters
### Common
- [[好 (char)]] good
- [[号]] symbol, but requires  <ruby>符号<rt>ㄅㄨㄏㄚˇ</rt></ruby>
- [[豪]] magnificent, bold, but requires <ruby>豪華<rt>ㄏㄚˇㄏㄨˇ</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[毫]] fine hair, milli-
- [[浩]] great, numerous, but requires [[浩大]]
- [[耗]] deplete, waste, but requires [[消耗]]
### Naming
- [[皓]] luminous, clear
- [[昊]] summertime, sky, heaven
- [[鎬]] stove, but requires [[鎬京]]
