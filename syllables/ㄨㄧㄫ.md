**ㄨㄧㄫ/윙/wing** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "to swim" (泳) or "to recite, chant" (詠).  For other uses in compounds, see below.

## Characters
### Common
- [[詠]] - "to recite, chant"
- [[泳]] - "to swim"
- [[永]] - "perpetual, eternal", but requires <ruby>[[永遠]]<rt>ㄨㄧㄫ·ㄛㄋ</rt></ruby>
- [[栄]] - "honorable, glorious", but requires <ruby>[[光栄]]<rt>ㄎㄠㄫㄨㄧㄫ</rt></ruby>
