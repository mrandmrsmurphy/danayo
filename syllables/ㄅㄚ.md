**ㄅㄚ/바/ba** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] without any intrinsic meaning.  There are several characters with this pronunciation, but none of which that can stand alone.  It appears in many other words as a component.

- [[波]] (noun) - 'wave', requires <ruby>[[波浪]]<rt>ㄅㄚㄌㄚㄥ</rt></ruby>
