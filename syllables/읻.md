**읻** ('id) is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "one" (一) or "to escape" (逸).  In advanced contexts, it may also mean "loyal, faithful" (壱).   It can also appear in compounds.

## Characters
### Common
- [[一 (char)]] - "one"
- [[逸]] - "to escape, flee"
### Advanced
- [[壱]] "loyal, faithful"
### Naming
none
