**г„‘г„ҡЛҷ/мұ„/cai** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "fork" (еҸү).  For other uses, see below.

## Characters
- [[еҸү]] "fork, prong"
- [[иҸң]] "vegetable, edible herb" but requires <ruby>[[йҮҺиҸң]]<rt>В·Лҷг„ҡг„‘г„ҡЛҷ</rt></ruby>
- [[жҺЎ]] "to gather" but requires <ruby>[[жҺЎйӣҶ]]<rt>г„‘г„ҡЛҷг„җг„§г„Ҷ</rt></ruby>
- [[еҪ©]] "vibrant, multicolored", but requires <ruby>[[еӨҡеҪ©]]<rt>г„үг„ҡг„‘г„ҡЛҷ</rt></ruby>
- [[жқҗ]] "material, stuff", but requires <ruby>[[жқҗж–ҷ]]<rt>г„‘г„ҡЛҷг„ҢЛҷг„ҡЛҮ</rt></ruby>
- [[иЈҒ]] "to tailor, to sew", but requires <ruby>[[иЈҒзё«]]<rt>г„‘г„ҡЛҷг„…г„ӣг„Ҙ</rt></ruby>
- [[йҮҮ]] "form, demeanor", but requires <ruby>[[йўЁйҮҮ]]<rt>г„Ҷг„Ёг„Ҙг„‘г„ҡЛҷ</rt></ruby>
