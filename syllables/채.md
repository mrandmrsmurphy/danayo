**채** is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "fork" (叉).  For other uses, see below.

### Forms
*Roman*: cai; *IPA*: /caj/
### Characters
- [[叉]] "fork, prong"
- [[菜]] "vegetable, edible herb" but requires <ruby>[[野菜]]<rt>야채</rt></ruby>
- [[採]] "to gather" but requires <ruby>[[採集]]<rt>채집</rt></ruby>
- [[彩]] "vibrant, multicolored", but requires <ruby>[[多彩]]<rt>다채</rt></ruby>
- [[材]] "material, stuff", but requires <ruby>[[材料]]<rt>채럇</rt></ruby>
- [[裁]] "to tailor, to sew", but requires <ruby>[[裁縫]]<rt>채봉</rt></ruby>
- [[采]] "form, demeanor", but requires <ruby>[[風采]]<rt>풍채</rt></ruby>
