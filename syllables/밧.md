**밧/bau** is a Dan'a'yo syllable meaning "to report, tell" (報).  For other uses, see below.

### Forms
*Roman*: bau; *IPA*: /bau/
### Characters
- [[報]] "to report, tell"
- [[抱]] "to embrace", but requires <ruby>[[抱擁]]<rt>밧옹</rt></ruby>
- [[宝]] "treasure, treasury", but requires <ruby>[[宝物]]<rt>밧묻</rt></ruby>
- [[保]] "to defend, protect", but requires <ruby>[[保護]]<rt>밧호</rt></ruby>
