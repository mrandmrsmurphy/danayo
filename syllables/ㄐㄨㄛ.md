**ㄐㄨㄛ/줏**/juo is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "let" (就) or "eternity" (宙) or "state" (州).  In certain situations, it can mean "islet" (洲).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
