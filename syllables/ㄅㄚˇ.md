**ㄅㄚˇ/밧/bau** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "to report, tell" (報).  With introduction, it can mean "abalone" (鮑).  For other uses, see below.

## Characters
- [[報 (char)]] "to report, tell"
- [[抱]] "to embrace", but requires <ruby>[[抱擁]]<rt>ㄅㄚˇ·ㄛㄥ</rt></ruby>
- [[宝]] "treasure, treasury", but requires <ruby>[[宝物]]<rt>ㄅㄚˇㄇㄨㄊ</rt></ruby>
- [[保]] "to defend, protect", but requires <ruby>[[保護]]<rt>ㄅㄚˇㄏㄛ</rt></ruby>
