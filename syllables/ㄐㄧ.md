**ㄐㄧ/지/ji** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "-ing" (事) or "character, glyph" (字).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters
'''ji''' means "character" or "-ing"
### Common
- [[事 (char)]] - (post) -ing
- [[字]] - (n) character, glyph
- [[子]] - (n) child/son --> [[児子]]
- [[志]] - (n) will, intention --> [[意志]]
- [[慈]] - (adj) kind --> [[慈善]]
- [[止]] - (v) stop --> [[停止]]
- [[滋]] - (v) thrive --> [[滋生]]
- [[磁]] - (n) magnetism --> [[磁性]] 
- [[誌]] - (v) symbolize --> [[標誌]] 
### Naming
- [[玆]] - (name) now
- [[梓]] - (name) yellow catalpa --> [[梓木]]
- [[芝]] - (name) sesame
- [[蜘]] - (name) spider --> [[蜘蛛]]
