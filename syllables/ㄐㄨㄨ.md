**ㄐㄨㄨ/줏**/juu is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "let" (就) or "eternity" (宙) or "state" (州).  In certain situations, it can mean "islet" (洲).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters
### Common
- [[就]] "let"
- [[宙]] eternity
- [[州]] state
- [[酒]] alcohol --> <ruby>[[酒精]]<rt>ㄐㄨㄨㄐㄝㄥ</rt></ruby>
- [[調]] to transfer --> <ruby>[[調整]]<rt>ㄐㄨㄨㄐㄝㄥ</rt></ruby>
- [[昼]] daytime --> <ruby>[[昼間]]<rt>ㄐㄨㄨㄍㄚㄋ</rt></ruby>
- [[周]] circumference --> <ruby>[[圓周]]<rt>·ㄨㄝㄋㄐㄨㄨ</rt></ruby>
- [[週]] week, cycle --> <ruby>[[週日]]<rt>ㄐㄨㄨㄋㄧㄊ</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[洲]] islet
- [[呪]] spell, curse --> <ruby>[[呪詛]]<rt>ㄐㄨㄨㄐㄧㄚ</rt></ruby>
### Names
- 鷲 vulture, eagle --> <ruby>[[就鳥]]<rt>ㄐㄨㄨㄑㄛㄨ</rt></ruby>
- [[酎]] saké, soju --> <ruby>[[焼酎]]<rt>ㄙㄧㄛㄨㄐㄨㄨ</rt></ruby>
