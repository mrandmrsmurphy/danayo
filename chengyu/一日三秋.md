## Classical Source

The earliest textual source is the **《诗经·王风·采葛》** (_Book of Odes / Book of Songs_, “Airs of the States – Odes of Wei, _Gathering Kudzu_”).

Text of stanza 2:

> 彼采葛兮，  
> 一日不见，如三月兮。

Translation (Legge):

> “He gathers the kudzu;  
> One day without seeing (her)  
> Is like three months.”
