**ㄅㄧㄝㄥ/byeng/병** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "third" (丙) or "design" (柄).  In advanced situations, it can mean "level ground" (坪).
## Characters
### Common
- [[丙]] third
- [[柄]] design
- [[兵]] soldier, but requires <ruby>兵士<rt>ㄅㄧㄝㄥㄙㄚㄧ</rt></ruby>
- [[平]] level, even, but requires <ruby>平坦<rt>ㄅㄧㄝㄥㄊㄚㄋ</rt></ruby>
- [[病]] sickness, but requires <ruby>疾病<rt>ㄐㄧㄊㄅㄧㄝㄥ</rt></ruby>
- [[評]] evaluate, but requires <ruby>評価<rt>ㄅㄧㄝㄥㄍㄚ</rt></ruby>
### Advanced
- [[坪]] level ground
### Naming
- [[苹]] apple, but requires <ruby>苹果<rt>ㄅㄧㄝㄥㄍㄨㄚ</rt></ruby>
