## 地官·司徒
_Offices of Earth, Office of the Tutors_
113. [[保]][[姓氏]]：[[主掌]][[諌議]][[王]]<ruby>[[之]]<rt>티</rt></ruby>[[悪]]，[[而]][[養育]][[国子]][[道]][[以]]。[[乃]][[教授]]<ruby>[[之]]<rt>티</rt></ruby>[[六芸]]：一曰[[五礼]]，二曰[[六楽]]，三曰[[五射]]，四曰[[五馭]]，五曰[[六書]]，六曰[[九数]]。
- 五禮
	1. 吉禮 (_jí lǐ_) – Auspicious rites (sacrifices to Heaven, Earth, and ancestors).
	2. 凶禮 (_xiōng lǐ_) – Inauspicious rites (mourning and funerals).
	3. 賓禮 (_bīn lǐ_) – Guest rites (hospitality, receiving envoys).
	4. 軍禮 (_jūn lǐ_) – Military rites (mustering troops, declaring war).
	5. 嘉禮 (_jiā lǐ_) – Auspicious/celebratory rites (marriages, coming-of-age, investitures).
- 六樂
	- 雲門 (_Yúnmén_) – attributed to the Yellow Emperor.
	- 咸池 (_Xiánchí_) – attributed to Emperor Yao.
	- 大韶 (_Dàsháo_) – music of Emperor Shun.
	- 大夏 (_Dàxià_) – music of Yu the Great.
	- 大濩 (_Dàhù_) – music of King Tang of Shang.
	- 大武 (_Dàwǔ_) – music of King Wu of Zhou.
- 五射
	- 白矢 (_Báishǐ_) – “White arrow,” judged for straightness (morality).
	- 参連 (_Cānlián_) – “Triple succession,” shooting multiple arrows without pause.
	- 剡注 (_Yǎnzhù_) – “Oblique aim,” shooting at an angle.
	- 襄尺 (_Xiāngchǐ_) – shooting at a short distance with precision.
	- 井儀 (_Jǐngyí_) – “Well-formation,” shooting in orderly rows like the wells-field pattern.
- 五御
	- 鳴和鸞 (_Míng hé luán_) – coordinating bells and music with the horses.
	- 逐水曲 (_Zhú shuǐ qū_) – driving along winding paths near rivers.
	- 過君表 (_Guò jūn biǎo_) – maneuvering past markers.
	- 舞交衢 (_Wǔ jiāo qú_) – “dancing” at crossroads, intricate turns.
	- 逐禽左 (_Zhú qín zuǒ_) – pursuing game or enemy on the left flank.
- 六書
	- 象形 (_xiàngxíng_) – pictographs.
	- 指事 (_zhǐshì_) – simple indicatives.
	- 會意 (_huìyì_) – compound ideograms.
	- 形聲 (_xíngshēng_) – phono-semantic compounds.
	- 轉注 (_zhuǎnzhù_) – mutually explanatory characters.
	- 假借 (_jiǎjiè_) – phonetic loans.
- 九數
	- 方田 (_fāng tián_) – surveying fields.
	- 粟米 (_sù mǐ_) – grain calculations.
	- 差分 (_chā fēn_) – proportion and differences.
	- 少廣 (_shǎo guǎng_) – volumetrics (depth and breadth).
	- 商功 (_shāng gōng_) – engineering and work projects.
	- 均輸 (_jūn shū_) – fair distribution (tax, transport).
	- 盈不足 (_yíng bùzú_) – surpluses and deficits (balancing).
	- 方程 (_fāngchéng_) – equations of many unknowns.
	- 勾股 (_gōugǔ_) – the Pythagorean theorem.
