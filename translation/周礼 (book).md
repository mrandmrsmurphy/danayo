113. [[保]][[姓氏]]：[[主掌]][[諌議]][[王]]<ruby>[[之]]<rt>티</rt></ruby>[[悪]]，[[而]][[養育]][[国子]][[道]][[以]]。[[乃]][[教授]]<ruby>[[之]]<rt>티</rt></ruby>[[六芸]]：一曰[[五礼]]，二曰[[六楽]]，三曰[[五射]]，四曰[[五馭]]，五曰[[六書]]，六曰[[九数]]。
- 五禮
	1. 吉禮** (_jí lǐ_) – Auspicious rites (sacrifices to Heaven, Earth, and ancestors).
    
- **凶禮** (_xiōng lǐ_) – Inauspicious rites (mourning and funerals).
    
- **賓禮** (_bīn lǐ_) – Guest rites (hospitality, receiving envoys).
    
- **軍禮** (_jūn lǐ_) – Military rites (mustering troops, declaring war).
    
- **嘉禮** (_jiā lǐ_) – Auspicious/celebratory rites (marriages, coming-of-age, investitures).
