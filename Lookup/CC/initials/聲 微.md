> **Initial 微** evolved into ㄇ

## CJKV Evolution
微 [ɱ] is the labiodental nasal that split off from [[聲 明|明]] before certain division-3 rounded finals — the nasal member of the light-labial series (輕唇音: [[聲 非|非]]/[[聲 敷|敷]]/[[聲 奉|奉]]/微), parallel to how 非/敷/奉 split off from [[聲 幫|幫]]/[[聲 滂|滂]]/[[聲 並|並]]. Despite the different historical starting point, it lands back on exactly the same outcome as its heavy-labial cousin: all 36 characters land on **ㄇ (m)**, with no exceptions — the light/heavy labial distinction that matters for the obstruents doesn't survive for the nasal.
