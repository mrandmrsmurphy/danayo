> [Classical Chinese](../Classical%20Chinese.md)
> **Initial 徹** evolved into ㄑ or ㄊ, roughly evenly split (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
徹 [ʈʰ] is the 次清 (aspirated) counterpart of [[聲 知|知]], and it's the one initial in [[文法 - 99韻図]]'s own master table whose Dan'a'yo column is written as a genuine dual value (`t/c`) rather than one letter — this isn't a polling exception to explain, it's the documented expected outcome. Since 徹 is already aspirated throughout, the split between **ㄑ (aspirated affricate, 14 of 25)** and **ㄊ (aspirated dental, 11 of 25)** is a place-of-articulation split, not an aspiration one — the same affricate-vs-dental fork already seen as an unexplained minority pocket on [[聲 知|知]]'s own page, except here it's roughly even and expected rather than a small exception.
