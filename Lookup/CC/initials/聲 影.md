> **Initial 影** evolved into ø (null initial)

## CJKV Evolution
影 [ʔ] is the glottal stop, the "zero" member of the laryngeal series alongside [[聲 曉|曉]]/[[聲 匣|匣]]/[[聲 云|云]] — and like [[聲 疑|疑]], it doesn't survive as an audible initial in any daughter language. [[文法 - 99韻図]]'s own table records the outcome as **ø** across the board, and all 141 characters confirm it: each keeps only its vowel, with an apostrophe-prefixed 羅馬字 marking the absent initial, the same pattern already seen on [[聲 疑|疑]]'s page. No consonant outcome to group characters by — every entry sits in a single ø group.
