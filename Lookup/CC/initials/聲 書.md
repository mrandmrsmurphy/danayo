> **Initial 書** mostly evolved into ㄙ, with two singleton outcomes (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
書 [ɕ] is the 清擦 (voiceless fricative) member of the palatal series, pairing with [[聲 章|章]]/[[聲 昌|昌]]/[[聲 禪|禪]] the way [[聲 心|心]] pairs with [[聲 精|精]]/[[聲 清|清]]/[[聲 從|從]]. As expected for a fricative, it's almost entirely regular: **ㄙ for 69 of 71 characters**. Of the two singletons, **沈 → ㄑ** is fully explained once both Mandarin (chén) and Korean (침) are checked — both are genuinely aspirated, even though Cantonese (sam2) is not. **弒 → ㄊ**, by contrast, has no support anywhere (shì/si3/시 are all unaspirated sibilants) — a real, unexplained one-off reassignment to a completely different place of articulation.
