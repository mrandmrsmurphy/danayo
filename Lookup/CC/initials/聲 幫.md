## CJKV Evolution
幫 [p] is a 清 initial — plain voiceless, neither aspirated nor voiced — the class of the 三十六字母 with the least distinctive daughter-language behavior. Unlike 次清/濁 initials (whose aspiration or voicing tends to survive as a stable, predictable feature across Mandarin, Cantonese, Vietnamese, Japanese, and Korean), 清 initials carry no such built-in signal, so Dan'a'yo's outcome for each 幫-initial character was decided individually by polling the daughter readings rather than by one deterministic rule — the same MC initial + final pair can still land on different Dan'a'yo results for different characters. For 幫 specifically, the poll comes out **ㄅ (b) for the large majority (86 of 109)**, with two real minority outcomes: **ㄆ (p, 20 characters)** and **ㄈ (f, 3 characters — a reassignment to the light-labial series, not just an aspiration difference)**.
