> **Initial 群** mostly evolved into ㄍ, with a ㄎ minority outcome (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
群 [g] is the 濁 (voiced) counterpart of [[聲 見|見]] and [[聲 溪|溪]], the same 平送氣仄不送氣 tone-conditioned devoicing story as [[聲 並|並]]/[[聲 定|定]]/[[聲 從|從]]. It resolves mostly to **ㄍ (unaspirated, 75 of 79)**, with a small **ㄎ minority (4 characters)**: 期/旗 (sharing the same final `ɨ`), 埼, and 禽 are all straightforwardly aspirated in Mandarin (qī/qí/qí/qín) — a clean, fully-explained exception, unlike the murkier minority pockets on some of the other 濁 initials.
