## CJKV Evolution
зҫӨ [g] is the жҝҒ (voiced) counterpart of [[иҒІ иҰӢ|иҰӢ]] and [[иҒІ жәӘ|жәӘ]], the same е№ійҖҒж°Јд»„дёҚйҖҒж°Ј tone-conditioned devoicing story as [[иҒІ дёҰ|дёҰ]]/[[иҒІ е®ҡ|е®ҡ]]/[[иҒІ еҫһ|еҫһ]]. It resolves mostly to **г„Қ (unaspirated, 75 of 79)**, with a small **г„Һ minority (4 characters)**: жңҹ/ж—— (sharing the same final `ЙЁ`), еҹј, and зҰҪ are all straightforwardly aspirated in Mandarin (qД«/qГӯ/qГӯ/qГӯn) вҖ” a clean, fully-explained exception, unlike the murkier minority pockets on some of the other жҝҒ initials.
