> **Initial 奉** mostly evolved into ㄅ, with ㄈ/ㄆ/ㄇ minority outcomes (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
奉 [v] is the 濁 (voiced) member of the light-labial series, paired with [[聲 非|非]] (清)/[[聲 敷|敷]] (次清)/[[聲 微|微]] (nasal). Like [[聲 並|並]]/[[聲 定|定]]/[[聲 從|從]], it lost its MC tone and was fixed per-character rather than by rule: **ㄅ (reverting to heavy-labial, unaspirated) for 34 of 50**, a **ㄈ minority (9, staying light-labial)**, a **ㄆ minority (6, heavy-labial aspirated)**, and a lone **ㄇ singleton**: 柉, reassigned all the way to the nasal series despite Mandarin (fán), Cantonese (faan4), and Korean (범, itself plain b-) giving no hint of a nasal outcome anywhere.
