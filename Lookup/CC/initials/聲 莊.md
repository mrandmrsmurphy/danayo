## CJKV Evolution
иҺҠ [tНЎКғ] is the жё… (plain) member of the retroflex series (иҺҠзө„), paired with [[иҒІ еҲқ|еҲқ]] (ж¬Ўжё…)/[[иҒІ еҙҮ|еҙҮ]] (жҝҒ)/[[иҒІ з”ҹ|з”ҹ]] (жё…ж“Ұ)/[[иҒІ дҝҹ|дҝҹ]] (жҝҒж“Ұ). It's almost entirely uniform: **г„җ for 26 of 27 characters**. The lone exception, **й„’ вҶ’ г„‘**, is unaspirated in both Mandarin (zЕҚu) and Cantonese (zau1), but its Korean reading (м¶”, aspirated г…Ҡ) explains the outcome вҖ” the same Korean-over-Mandarin pattern now seen repeatedly across this series.
