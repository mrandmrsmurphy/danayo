## CJKV Evolution
知 [ʈ] is the 清 (plain) member of the retroflex-stop series (知組: 知/[[聲 徹|徹]]/[[聲 澄|澄]]/[[聲 娘|娘]], the historical "tongue-raised" 舌上音 counterpart of [[聲 端|端]]'s 舌頭音). It splits four ways, the messiest yet: **ㄐ for 26 of 47**, an **8-character ㄑ** (aspirated-affricate) pocket, a **7-character ㄉ** pocket, and a **6-character ㄊ** pocket. Checking all daughter languages (not just Mandarin) explains most of the aspirated (ㄑ/ㄊ) entries: 卓/琢, 忠/衷, and 追 (each sharing a final with at least one sibling) are aspirated in Korean (탁, 충, 추) though not Mandarin; 置/致 are aspirated in Korean (치) alone; 塚 is aspirated in both Cantonese (cung2) and Korean (총); 嘲 is aspirated in Mandarin itself (cháo). Three entries — 珍/鎮 and 張, 貞 — have no aspiration support in any daughter language and are simple poll exceptions. The **ㄉ pocket is a different phenomenon entirely**: a manner shift to a plain (non-affricate) dental rather than an aspiration difference, and none of its seven members (嫡, 摘, 株, 箸, 徴, 砧, 駐) show any daughter-language pattern that predicts it.
