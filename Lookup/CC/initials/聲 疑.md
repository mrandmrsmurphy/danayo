> [Classical Chinese](../Classical%20Chinese.md)
> **Initial 疑** evolved into ø (null initial)

## CJKV Evolution
疑 [ŋ] is the 清濁 (sonorant) member of the velar series (見組), the nasal counterpart of [[聲 見|見]]/[[聲 溪|溪]]/[[聲 群|群]] — but unlike every other nasal documented in this project ([[聲 泥|泥]], [[聲 娘|娘]], [[聲 明|明]] pending), 疑 doesn't survive as a consonant at all. [[文法 - 99韻図]]'s own table records its Dan'a'yo outcome as **ø**, matching a well-known cross-linguistic pattern: word-initial velar nasals are the least stable nasal class, routinely weakening to zero or a glide (visible directly in Mandarin, where 疑-initial characters like 疑 itself surface as plain vowel-initial `yí`). All 112 characters here confirm it: each keeps only its vowel, with an apostrophe-prefixed 羅馬字 (e.g. 疑 itself: `'i`) marking the absent initial — no consonant outcome to group by, unlike every other initial page in this set.
