> **Initial 溪** evolved into ㄎ

## CJKV Evolution
溪 [kʰ] is the 次清 (aspirated) counterpart of [[聲 見|見]], and like every aspirated stop/affricate documented so far ([[聲 初|初]], [[聲 滂|滂]], [[聲 清|清]]), aspiration carries a stable, well-preserved signal across the daughter languages. The result is the largest fully clean page in this project: all 121 characters land on **ㄎ (kʰ)**, with no exceptions.
