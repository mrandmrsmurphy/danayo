## CJKV Evolution
еЁҳ [Йі] is the жё…жҝҒ (sonorant) member of the retroflex-stop series (зҹҘзө„), the nasal counterpart of [[иҒІ зҹҘ|зҹҘ]]/[[иҒІ еҫ№|еҫ№]]/[[иҒІ жҫ„|жҫ„]] the way [[иҒІ жіҘ|жіҘ]] is for [[иҒІ з«Ҝ|з«Ҝ]]/[[иҒІ йҖҸ|йҖҸ]]/[[иҒІ е®ҡ|е®ҡ]]. As with every nasal documented so far, it's completely uniform among genuinely-inherited vocabulary: 12 of 13 characters land on **г„Ӣ (n)** вҖ” nasals keep proving the most stable class in this whole project. The lone exception, **жӘё**, isn't a natural counterexample: it's unattested in the Classical Chinese corpus (`mc_id: 0`), confirmed `#cranberry` (its only usage is жӘёжӘ¬ "lemon"), and its Dan'a'yo reading was deliberately overridden to **г„Ңг„қ** rather than following this initial's regular г„Ӣ outcome вҖ” see [[AIOS/memory/feedback_cranberry_transitivity.md]].
