> **Initial 心** evolved into ㄙ

## CJKV Evolution
心 [s] is the 清擦 (voiceless fricative) member of the dental sibilant series (精組), pairing with [[聲 從|從]]/[[聲 精|精]]/[[聲 清|清]] the same way [[聲 生|生]] pairs with [[聲 莊|莊]]/[[聲 初|初]]/[[聲 崇|崇]] and [[聲 書|書]] pairs with [[聲 章|章]]/[[聲 昌|昌]]/[[聲 禪|禪]]. As with every fricative and sonorant documented so far ([[聲 泥|泥]], [[聲 船|船]]), the outcome is completely uniform: all 139 characters — the largest fully clean page yet — land on **ㄙ (s)**, with no exceptions to explain.
