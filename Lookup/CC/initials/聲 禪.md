> **Initial 禪** mostly evolved into ㄙ, with a ㄐ minority outcome (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
禪 [d͡ʑ] is the 濁 (voiced) member of the palatal series, paired with [[聲 章|章]]/[[聲 昌|昌]]/[[聲 書|書]] and — per this vault's own convention — the affricate counterpart of [[聲 船|船]]'s fricative (see 船's page for the correction that untangled which character belongs where). Unlike its dental/labial 濁 cousins ([[聲 從|從]], [[聲 並|並]], [[聲 定|定]]), this series leans toward the sibilant outcome rather than the affricate one: **ㄙ for 32 of 34 characters**. The two-character **ㄐ** minority, 盾 and 垂, don't share a clean daughter-language explanation between them — 盾 (dùn/teon5/순) has no aspiration signal anywhere, while 垂 (chuí/seoi4/수) is aspirated in Mandarin but not Cantonese or Korean — so both are treated as poll-only exceptions.
