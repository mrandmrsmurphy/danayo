> **Initial 清** evolved into ㄑ

## CJKV Evolution
清 [t͡sʰ] is the 次清 (aspirated) counterpart of [[聲 精|精]], and like every other aspirated-affricate initial documented so far ([[聲 初|初]], [[聲 昌|昌]] pending) it's fully regular: all 69 characters land on a single outcome, **ㄑ (c)**, with no minority pocket. Aspiration keeps carrying a far more stable cross-linguistic signal than the plain/voiced members of the same series ([[聲 精|精]] and [[聲 從|從]] both split), and unlike 精's exceptions this series doesn't even have a rule-governed remainder to note — it's simply uniform.
