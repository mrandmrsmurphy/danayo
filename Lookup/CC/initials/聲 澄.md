> [Classical Chinese](../Classical%20Chinese.md)
> **Initial 澄** splits four ways: ㄐ/ㄉ/ㄑ/ㄊ (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
澄 [ɖ] is the 濁 (voiced) counterpart of [[聲 知|知]] (清) and [[聲 徹|徹]] (次清), and it inherits both of its neighbors' splits at once. [[聲 知|知]] fractures by place (affricate ㄐ vs. dental ㄉ); [[聲 徹|徹]] fractures the same way once aspiration is already fixed (ㄑ vs. ㄊ). Since 澄 is voiced and lost its MC tone — the feature that would normally decide aspiration outcome in Mandarin's 平送氣仄不送氣 rule — it fractures on *both* axes simultaneously: **ㄐ (unaspirated affricate, 33 of 71)**, **ㄉ (unaspirated dental, 19)**, **ㄑ (aspirated affricate, 17)**, and **ㄊ (aspirated dental, 2)**. The unaspirated pair (ㄐ+ㄉ, 52 characters) outnumbers the aspirated pair (ㄑ+ㄊ, 19) roughly the same way [[聲 從|從]] and [[聲 崇|崇]]'s own majorities did — the tone-conditioned split behaving as expected even without tone to condition it directly.
