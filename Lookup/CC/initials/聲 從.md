> **Initial 從** mostly evolved into ㄐ, with a ㄑ minority outcome (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
從 [d͡z] is the 濁 (voiced) counterpart of [[聲 精|精]], the same 平送氣仄不送氣 story as [[聲 並|並]] and [[聲 定|定]]: with tone gone, each character was fixed individually rather than by one rule. The result: **ㄐ for 51 of 65 characters**, with a **ㄑ minority of 14**. Most of the ㄑ group is rule-governed once both Mandarin and Korean are checked, not just Mandarin: 情/晴 (qíng), 牆/薔 (qiáng), 酋/遒 (qiú), 材/裁 (cái), and 層 (céng) all keep real Mandarin aspiration (each pair also sharing an exact MC final: `iᴇŋ`, `ɨɐŋ`, `ɨu`, `ʌi`); 就 and 聚 look unaspirated in Mandarin (jiù/jù) but their Korean readings (both 취, aspirated ㅊ) explain the ㄑ outcome instead. Only **匠, 蔵, and 造** (jiàng/zāng/zào, Korean 장/장/조, all unaspirated both ways) remain genuine poll-only exceptions with no daughter-language support, the same kind of unexplained minority seen in 並/定's pockets.
