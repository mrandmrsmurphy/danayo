## CJKV Evolution
敷 [fʰ] is the aspirated member of the light-labial series, paired with [[聲 非|非]] (plain)/[[聲 奉|奉]] (voiced)/[[聲 微|微]] (nasal). Unlike the stop-series aspiration contrasts (幫/滂, 見/溪, 知/徹), which stay distinct in Dan'a'yo, the fricative aspiration contrast between 敷 and 非 doesn't survive — both converge on the same outcome. All 36 characters here land on **ㄈ (f)**, identical to [[聲 非|非]]'s own result, with no exceptions.
