> **Initial еҢЈ** mostly evolved into г„Ҹ, with small г„Һ and null/glide outcomes (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
еҢЈ [ЙЈ] is the voiced counterpart of [[иҒІ жӣү|жӣү]], and the two initials mirror each other almost exactly. The overwhelming majority вҖ” **г„Ҹ (h) for 167 of 174 characters** вҖ” matches жӣү's own outcome (voicing doesn't survive as a separate contrast here any more than aspiration did for the light-labial fricatives). Three characters вҖ” иҢҺ, еҠҰ, йӣҮ вҖ” shift to the stop **г„Һ**, the direct parallel of жӣү's own зЎ…/и»Ј/йңҚ trio, the same fricative-to-stop manner reassignment recurring in the same initial pair. The remaining four вҖ” зӣӮ, йҹӢ, дҪ‘, зІӨ вҖ” lose the initial entirely to a **null/glide** outcome, and unlike most of this project's minority pockets, all four are fully regular: Mandarin, Cantonese, and Korean agree unanimously that none of them ever had an h- to begin with.
