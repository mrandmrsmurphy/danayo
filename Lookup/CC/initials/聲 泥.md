> **Initial 泥** evolved into ㄋ

## CJKV Evolution
泥 [n] is the 清濁 (sonorant) member of the dental series, the nasal counterpart of [[聲 端|端]]/[[聲 透|透]]/[[聲 定|定]]. Like [[聲 船|船]]'s fricative and unlike any of the stop/affricate initials documented so far, nasals carry an extremely stable cross-linguistic signal — all 44 characters land on a single, exceptionless outcome, **ㄋ (n)**, with no minority pocket at all. This tracks a pattern now visible across the whole project: sonorants and fricatives resist the per-character splitting that plosives and affricates are prone to.
