> **Initial 明** evolved into ㄇ

## CJKV Evolution
明 [m] is the 清濁 (sonorant) member of the labial series, the nasal counterpart of [[聲 幫|幫]]/[[聲 滂|滂]]/[[聲 並|並]]. Bilabial nasals are the most cross-linguistically stable consonant class of all — unlike [[聲 疑|疑]]'s velar nasal, which weakens to zero, /m/ survives everywhere. The result is the largest fully clean page in this project: all 141 characters land on **ㄇ (m)**, with no exceptions.
