## CJKV Evolution
з”ҹ [Кғ] is the жё…ж“Ұ (voiceless fricative) member of the retroflex series (иҺҠзө„), pairing with [[иҒІ иҺҠ|иҺҠ]]/[[иҒІ еҲқ|еҲқ]]/[[иҒІ еҙҮ|еҙҮ]] the way [[иҒІ еҝғ|еҝғ]] and [[иҒІ жӣё|жӣё]] pair with their own series' stops/affricates. As with every fricative documented so far, it's completely uniform: all 44 characters land on **г„ҷ (s)**, with no exceptions.
