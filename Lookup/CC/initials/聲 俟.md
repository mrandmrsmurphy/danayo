## CJKV Evolution
俟 [ʒ] is a famously marginal member of the 三十六字母 — a 37th-initial-adjacent category some reconstructions barely distinguish from [[聲 崇|崇]] or [[聲 生|生]], with almost no real character inventory in Middle Chinese at all. This vault's corpus reflects that directly: **zero characters carry `middle_chinese_initial: ʒ`**. Even 俟 itself — the character that names this initial — is deliberately tagged `z` and lives on [[聲 邪|邪]]'s own roster instead, a choice documented on 俟's own page ("俟 is the sole character in the corpus with 邪母 initial /z/"). This page exists to record that the category is real but empty, not to list a placeholder entry.
