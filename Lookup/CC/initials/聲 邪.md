> **Initial йӮӘ** mostly evolved into г„ҷ, with three singleton outcomes (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
йӮӘ [z] is the жҝҒж“Ұ (voiced fricative) counterpart of [[иҒІ еҝғ|еҝғ]], and like every fricative documented so far it's overwhelmingly regular: **г„ҷ for 46 of 49 characters**. The three singleton exceptions are each explainable on their own terms rather than forming a pattern: **иҫһ вҶ’ г„‘** matches its own Mandarin reading (cГӯ, genuinely aspirated); **йұҸ вҶ’ г„Ҹ** tracks Mandarin xГәn, whose `x-` happens to match [[иҒІ жӣү|жӣү]]'s own IPA symbol rather than staying a plain sibilant; and **дҝҹ вҶ’ г„җ** is the deliberate, already-documented case from [[иҒІ дҝҹ|дҝҹ]]'s own page вҖ” дҝҹжҜҚ has essentially no real character inventory of its own, so this vault's sole дҝҹ-initial character was assigned here instead, explaining why this page's true ground-truth count (49) exceeds what a strict "z only" reading would suggest.
