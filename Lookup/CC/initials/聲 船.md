> [Classical Chinese](../Classical%20Chinese.md)
> **Initial 船** evolved into ㄙ

## CJKV Evolution
船 [ʑ] is the 濁擦 (voiced fricative) member of the palatal 正齒音 series (章組), pairing with [[聲 書|書]] (its voiceless counterpart) the same way [[聲 邪|邪]] pairs with 心 and [[聲 俟|俟]] pairs with 生 in the other two sibilant series. Unlike the stop/affricate members of these series — 章/昌/禪, 精/清/從, 見/溪/群, all of which have shown real per-character splits elsewhere in this project — the fricative members carry a far more stable cross-linguistic signal: all 51 characters here land on a single, exceptionless outcome, **ㄙ (s)**. Mandarin still shows some internal variation between retroflex `ʂ-` and its aspirated affricate `tʂʰ-` depending on rime class, but Cantonese and Dan'a'yo both converge cleanly on plain `s-`. The character 船 itself (which lends this initial its traditional name) was actually mistagged as `d͡ʑ` until now — cross-checking every other 三十六字母 exemplar character against its own namesake page found this as the only undocumented mismatch in the corpus (俟's z/ʒ divergence is deliberate and already explained on its own page); corrected here, so 船 now rightly appears on its own roster below.
