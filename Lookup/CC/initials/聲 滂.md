> **Initial 滂** mostly evolved into ㄆ, with a ㄈ minority outcome (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
滂 [pʰ] is the 次清 (aspirated) member of the labial series, the exact counterpart of [[聲 幫|幫]] (清) and [[聲 並|並]] (濁) — aspiration is generally a stable, cross-linguistically well-preserved feature (as seen with [[聲 初|初]]), so 滂 comes out about as clean as [[聲 見|見]] did: **ㄆ for 49 of 50 characters**, with a single **ㄈ exception**: 頗 (pō/po2 in Mandarin and Cantonese alike — still plain bilabial in the daughter readings, yet the poll still moved it to the light-labial series), the same kind of one-off reassignment already seen scattered through 幫 and 並.
