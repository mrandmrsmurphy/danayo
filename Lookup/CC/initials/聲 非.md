> **Initial 非** mostly evolved into ㄈ, with one singleton outcome (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
非 [f] is the 清 (plain) member of the light-labial series (輕唇音: 非/[[聲 敷|敷]]/[[聲 奉|奉]]/[[聲 微|微]]), the labiodental fricative that split off from [[聲 幫|幫]] before division-3 rounded finals. It's almost entirely uniform: **ㄈ for 42 of 43 characters**. The lone exception, **傅 → ㄅ**, runs the split in reverse — reassigned back to the heavy-labial series despite Mandarin (fù), Cantonese (fu6), and Korean (부) all agreeing on a plain light-labial f-, an unexplained one-off.
