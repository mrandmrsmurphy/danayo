> **Initial д»Ҙ** evolved into y (glide)

## CJKV Evolution
д»Ҙ [j] is the palatal glide/semivowel, distinct from the true zero initials ([[иҒІ з–‘|з–‘]], [[иҒІ еҪұ|еҪұ]], [[иҒІ дә‘|дә‘]]) in that it keeps an actual y- onset rather than losing the initial entirely. All 120 characters here confirm a genuine y- glide in Mandarin (yГ¬, yЗҗ, yГЎn, yГЁ, and so on throughout); the apparent variety of bopomofo onset symbols across entries (вј¶/вјң/вј„/вјҳ/г„§/г„ң/вј”/г„Ё) is purely a matter of which vowel follows the glide, not a different consonant outcome вҖ” unlike every other initial page in this set, there's no real place/manner split to group by, just one y- onset realized before many different vowels.
