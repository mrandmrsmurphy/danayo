> **Initial 昌** mostly evolved into ㄑ, with one singleton outcome (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
昌 [t͡ɕʰ] is the 次清 (aspirated) counterpart of [[聲 章|章]], and like every aspirated-affricate initial documented so far ([[聲 初|初]], [[聲 清|清]]), it's almost fully uniform: **ㄑ for 32 of 33 characters**. The lone exception, **串 → ㄐ**, runs against the grain rather than filling a predictable gap: both Mandarin (chuàn) and Cantonese (cyun3) are clearly aspirated, yet the Dan'a'yo outcome went unaspirated anyway — a genuine, unexplained poll-only exception, the mirror image of 昌's usual pattern.
