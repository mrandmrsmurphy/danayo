> **Initial 透** mostly evolved into ㄊ, with a ㄑ minority outcome (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
透 [tʰ] is the 次清 (aspirated) counterpart of [[聲 端|端]], and like [[聲 滂|滂]] it comes out clean: **ㄊ for 62 of 66 characters**. The 4-character **ㄑ** minority (推, 聴, 庁, 替) isn't a real Mandarin palatalization the way 端's ㄑ pocket was — all four keep a plain `t-` in Mandarin (tuī, tīng, tīng, tì) — it's simply a same-manner reassignment from aspirated dental to aspirated palatal. Two of the four, 聴 and 庁, again cluster by final rather than phonetic component (different components 𡈼/丁, identical final `eŋ`), the same pattern now confirmed across 並, 見, and 端.
