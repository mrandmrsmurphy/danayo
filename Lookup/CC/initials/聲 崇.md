## CJKV Evolution
еҙҮ [dНЎК’] is the жҝҒ (voiced) member of the retroflex series (иҺҠзө„), the affricate counterpart of [[иҒІ иҺҠ|иҺҠ]] (жё…)/[[иҒІ еҲқ|еҲқ]] (ж¬Ўжё…), and вҖ” like [[иҒІ еҫһ|еҫһ]]/[[иҒІ зҰӘ|зҰӘ]] вҖ” mostly resolves to the affricate outcome: **г„җ for 14 of 19 characters**. Four characters (еЈ«, еәҠ, ж„Ғ, й©ҹ) instead landed on the fricative **г„ҷ** rather than the affricate вҖ” a manner reassignment rather than an aspiration difference, and one without a clean single-language explanation (й©ҹ's Korean м·Ё is aspirated, but the others show no such support). The lone **г„‘** singleton, **йӣӣ**, is aspirated in both Cantonese (co1) and Korean (м¶”), fully explaining that jump.
