## CJKV Evolution
麥開 [ɣɛk] converges mostly: 11 of 13 characters land on **ㄚㄎ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ak`).

**厄** is a near-homophone of ʔ-initial 軛 (both Mandarin *è*) and dodges the collision with a vowel-shift, landing on **ㄜㄎ**. **革** shares its initial (k) and Mandarin/Cantonese readings (*gé*/*gaak3*) with 隔, differing only in Korean (혁 vs 격) — a reading-tradition-only distinction — and dodges via vowel-shift to **ㄧㄎ**.
