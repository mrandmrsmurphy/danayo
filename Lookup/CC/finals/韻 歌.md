> **Final 歌** evolved into ㄚ, with a k-initial and tʰ-initial split among the exceptions (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
歌 [ɑ] lands 30 of 37 characters on **ㄚ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`a`).

The 6-member **ㄜ** group (他, 可, 多, 哥, 歌, 個) is another instance of the "same initial, arbitrary outcome" pattern seen throughout this sweep: k-initial 珂 and 柯 stay on ㄚ while k-initial 哥, 歌, and 個 (including the final's own namesake character) land on ㄜ; tʰ-initial 蛇 and 拖 stay on ㄚ while tʰ-initial 他 lands on ㄜ.

**羅** dodges a 3-member ㄌㄚ (邏, 蘿, 鑼, all l-initial like 羅) via a vowel-shift, landing on **ㄛ**.
