## CJKV Evolution
唐開 [ɑŋ] converges cleanly: 44 of 49 characters land on **ㄚㄫ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ang`).

The **⺢ㄫ** group splits into two distinct stories. 蒼, 艙, and 倉 all share the identical aspirated affricate initial (t͡sʰ), and no t͡sʰ-initial character appears anywhere in the main ㄚㄫ group — the three land together with no crowding to explain it, another instance of aspiration cleanly determining the outcome (echoing the plain-k/aspirated-kʰ split confirmed on [[韻 肴|肴]]). 糖 and 唐, both d-initial, dodge a genuinely crowded 6-member ㄉㄚㄫ (螳, 蕩, 当, 宕, 堂, 党) by adding the same w-glide.
