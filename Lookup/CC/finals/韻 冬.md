## CJKV Evolution
冬 [uoŋ] is overwhelmingly regular: 7 of 8 characters land on **ㄛㄫ**. The lone exception is 彤, which lands on **ㄛㄇ** (m-coda) instead — and none of its daughter-language readings predict this (Mandarin tóng, Cantonese tung4, Korean 동 all point straight at a velar-nasal coda, same as every other member of the group). The likely explanation is homophony avoidance rather than any phonological conditioning: 彤's own phonetic component is [[同]], and the ㄉㄛㄫ slot is already extremely crowded — 動, 銅, 凍, 筒, 同, 東, 桐, 棟, 瞳, 洞, 童, 董, 胴 all occupy it. 彤 is quite literally dodging its own phonetic family.
