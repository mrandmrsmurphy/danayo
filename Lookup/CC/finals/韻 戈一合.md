## CJKV Evolution
戈一合 [uɑ] lands 20 of 34 characters on **⺢** (bare w-glide), matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`wa`).

This final gives the cleanest confirmation yet of the phonotactic rule documented in [[skill_cc_phonology]] — "w-glide cannot co-occur with ㄋㄌㄅㄆㄇ": every one of the 7 labial-initial characters (玻, 叵, 磨, 播, 波, 頗, 婆) and both l-initial characters (螺, 裸) land on plain **ㄚ**, with zero exceptions on either side. Meanwhile d-initial characters (惰, 堕) — not barred by that rule — do carry the glide, staying in the main ⺢ group. **破**, a third pʰ-initial character, dodges the 3-member ㄆㄚ crowd (玻, 叵, 頗) via a vowel-shift to **ㄜ**, still glide-free as the rule predicts.

**課** adds a coda to its glide, landing on **⺢ㄇ** — dodging a modest collision with 顆, the only other kʰ-initial character on this final.
