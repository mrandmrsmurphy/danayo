## CJKV Evolution
йҷҪй–Ӣ [ЙЁЙҗЕӢ] is the largest and most fractured final documented in this sweep. 45 of 87 characters land on **вјҳг„«**, matching the y-glide quality of the Vowels table's documented winner (`yang`).

The retroflex and affricate-initial characters (tНЎЙ•/КҲ/Й–/tНЎКғ/dНЎz/dНЎК’-type, 38 characters combined) split between two groups with no findable conditioning factor вҖ” the largest-scale confirmation of the "same initial, arbitrary outcome" pattern found anywhere in this sweep. **еёі** and **ејө** share the identical MC initial (КҲ) yet land on opposite sides: еёі on the w-glide-bearing **вәўг„«**, ејө on the plain **г„ҡг„«**. **е”ұ** and **жҳҢ** (both tНЎЙ•К°) land on вәўг„«, while **е» ** (also tНЎЙ•К°) lands on г„ҡг„« вҖ” the same initial, split three ways across just two characters' worth of company.

Two small coda-shift subgroups round out the page. **з–Ҷ** and **е§ң** (both k-initial) dodge a 2-member вјҳг„« crowd (еғө, еј·) by changing coda, landing on **вјҳг„Ӣ**. **жҳң**, sharing its j-initial with the large 7-member y-glide cluster (жҙӢ, жҘҠ, йҷҪ, жҸҡ, йӨҠ, зҫҠ, зҳҚ), dodges the same way, landing on **вјҳг„Ү** alongside еәҠ.
