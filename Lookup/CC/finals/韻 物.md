## CJKV Evolution
物 [ɨut] is nearly uniform: 13 of 15 characters land on **ㄨㄊ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ut`). **欝** shares its null initial (ʔ) with 尉, which already occupies ㄨㄊ, and dodges by landing on **⼜ㄊ**. **不**, the only p-initial character on this final, lands on **ㄛㄊ** with no same-initial neighbor to collide with — an unconditioned singleton.
