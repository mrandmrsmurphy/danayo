> **Final 添** evolved into ㄝㄇ, with two t/d-initial escapes (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
添 [em] converges mostly: 8 of 10 characters land on **ㄝㄇ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`em`).

**忝** (tʰ) and **甜** (d) both belong to the t/d/tʰ cluster that already crowds the main group (点, 店, 添), and both dodge via vowel-shift, landing together on **ㄧㄇ**.
