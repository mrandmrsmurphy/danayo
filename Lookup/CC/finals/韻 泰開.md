## CJKV Evolution
泰開 [ɑi] is a division-1 final and converges as expected: 18 of 19 characters land on **ㄚㄧ**, matching the pattern already confirmed on its rounded counterpart [[韻 泰合|泰合]].

The lone exception is 奈, which lands on **⼘ㄧ** instead. Unlike most exceptions in this sweep, this doesn't trace to a crowded slot — no other character on this final shares 奈's n-initial, so there's no collision to dodge. Its daughter readings (Mandarin nài, Cantonese noi6, Korean 내) are unremarkable and don't point toward this outcome either. This looks like a clean case of the vault's "simplicity principle" picking an arbitrary vowel with no findable conditioning factor — worth documenting as such rather than searching further for a rule that isn't there.
