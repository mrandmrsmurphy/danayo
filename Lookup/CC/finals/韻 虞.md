> **Final 虞** evolved into ㄨ, with its labiodental-initial cohort scattering across four outcomes (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
虞 [ɨo] is the largest final documented in this sweep (109 characters) and converges well: 85 land on **ㄨ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`u`).

The headline finding here is even bigger than the labiodental scatter already seen on [[韻 微合|微合]]: this final's 15 f/fʰ-initial characters split across **four** vowel outcomes. The main ㄈㄨ slot alone holds 10 (賦, 斧, 黼, 敷, 撫, 俯, 府, 孚, 付, 俘) — under that pressure, 訃 and 赴 overflow to **ㄨㄛ**, 膚 and 夫 overflow to **ㄜ**, and 尃 overflows alone to bare **ㄛ**. Fifteen characters needing distinct syllables from one initial class, spread across four vowel qualities, is the largest single-initial scatter found on this project so far.

Several singletons round out the page, each dodging a heavily crowded slot: **聚** would regularly join ㄐㄨ, which already holds 11 characters (鋳, 誅, 注, 住, 主, 邾, 珠, 柱, 朱, 侏, 蛛) — the second most crowded slot found in this sweep, after 模's 13-member ㄏㄛ — and instead lands on **ㄨㄧ**. **矩** dodges an 8-member ㄍㄨ (衢, 惧, 句, 具, 駒, 拘, 邱, 倶) by dropping the vowel entirely, landing on bare **⼄**. **輸** dodges a 9-member ㄙㄨ (須, 鬚, 樹, 洙, 需, 戍, 殊, 数, 豎) via a coda-shift to **ㄨㄇ** (m-coda), the same overflow strategy already seen on 魯, 彤, and 刪合.

The final's own yod-initial (j) characters also split unconditioned, matching the recurring "same initial, arbitrary outcome" pattern: 8 land on **⼜ㄇ** (逾, 喩, 蝓, 揄, 兪, 愈, 諭, 愉) while 3 land on plain **⼜** (庾, 癒, 裕) — identical initial, no findable distinguishing factor.
