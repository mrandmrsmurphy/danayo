## CJKV Evolution
麻二開 [ɣa] converges cleanly: 45 of 48 characters land on plain **ㄚ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`a`).

The 3 exceptions all dodge crowded slots by adding a glide rather than shifting vowel. **夏** dodges the most crowded slot on this page — ㄏㄚ already holds 5 characters (下, 蝦, 霞, 廈, 暇) — landing on **⼘**. **紗** dodges a smaller ㄙㄚ (shared with 沙) the same way. **渣** dodges a 3-member ㄐㄚ (査, 詐, 乍) with a fuller w-glide, landing on **⺢** rather than the lighter y-glide the other two use.
