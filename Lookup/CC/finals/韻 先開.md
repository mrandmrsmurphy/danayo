## CJKV Evolution
先開 [en] is a division-4 final and converges cleanly: 41 of 46 characters land on **ㄝㄋ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`en`).

**殿** dodges the most crowded slot on this page — ㄉㄝㄋ already holds 9 characters (顚, 典, 填, 鈿, 淀, 田, 電, 槙, 佃) — by shifting its vowel and landing on **ㄧㄋ**. **眠** dodges a smaller ㄇㄝㄋ crowd (麺) via the familiar coda-shift, landing on **ㄝㄇ**. **現** and **見** each dodge modestly crowded slots (顕/弦/賢/舷 on ㄏㄝㄋ; 肩/堅 on ㄍㄝㄋ) by adding a glide, landing together on **⼶ㄋ**. **繭**, also dodging the ㄍㄝㄋ crowd that 見 escaped, takes a third route entirely — adding a glide *and* changing its coda — landing on **⼶ㄫ**.
