> **Final 清開** evolved into ㄧㄫ, with a large same-initial unconditioned split into ㄝㄫ (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
清開 [iᴇŋ] lands 35 of 43 characters on **ㄧㄫ**, matching the y-quality of the Vowels table's documented winner (`ing/eng`).

The remaining 6 — 整, 征, 浄, 精, 鄭, 静 — all share the ㄐ initial letter (from t͡ɕ/d͡z/t͡s) with 8 members of the main group (正, 井, 政, 晶, 睛, 禎, 鉦, 靖), yet land on a separate **ㄝㄫ** instead. This is a large-scale confirmation of the "same initial, arbitrary outcome" pattern seen elsewhere in this sweep (most dramatically on [[韻 陽開|陽開]]): 征 (t͡ɕ) vs. 正 (t͡ɕ), and 浄/静 (d͡z) vs. 靖 (d͡z), are pairs with identical initials that diverge in vowel with no apparent conditioning factor.

The 2-member **⼶ㄫ** group (嬴, 盈, both y-initial true homophones, Mandarin *yíng*/Cantonese *jing4*) isn't really an exception — ⼶ is this vault's standard spelling for the 以 y-initial, matching the same convention confirmed on [[韻 庚三開|庚三開]].
