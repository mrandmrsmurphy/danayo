## CJKV Evolution
陌二開 [ɣæk] converges almost completely: 18 of 19 characters land on **ㄚㄎ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ak`).

The b/p slot **ㄅㄚㄎ** is the most crowded, holding six characters (白, 百, 伯, 帛, 舶, 迫). **柏** shares near-identical readings with 百 (both p-initial, both Cantonese *paak3*, both Korean 백) and dodges the collision by shifting its vowel, landing alone on **⼘ㄎ**.

Separately, the retroflex ɖ initial produces an unconditioned split: **択** and **沢** (a trad/simp-style variant pair, both ɖ) land on ㄉㄚㄎ alongside ʈ-initial 嫡, while **宅** — also plain ɖ, otherwise identical in kind — lands instead on ㄐㄚㄎ alongside d͡ʒ-initial 炸 and t͡ʃ-initial 窄. Same initial, arbitrary outcome, same pattern documented repeatedly elsewhere in this sweep (e.g. [[韻 陽開|陽開]]).
