## CJKV Evolution
藥合 [ʉɐk] has only one character in the corpus, 縛, which lands on **ㄚㄎ** — glide-free, consistent with the labial w-glide ban confirmed repeatedly elsewhere in this sweep ([[韻 戈一合|戈一合]], [[韻 鈬合|鈬合]], [[韻 陽合|陽合]]), since 縛's initial (v) is labial. Despite this, the Vowels table's documented winner for this final is `wak` — worth noting the mismatch, though a sample of one can't confirm which is representative.
