> **Final 夬合** evolved into ⺢ㄧ (wai)

## CJKV Evolution
夬合 [ɣuai] has only 2 characters, 快 and 話, and both carry the w-glide (**⺢ㄧ**) as expected for a 合 (rounded) final — matching the 合→w-glide rule confirmed across every other 開/合 pair in this sweep, and the genuine source of the `wai` D value that its 開 counterpart's table row appears to have duplicated (see the flag on [[韻 夬開|夬開]]).
