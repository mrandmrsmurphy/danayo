> **Final йҷҪеҗҲ** splits perfectly by labial vs non-labial initial (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
йҷҪеҗҲ [КүЙҗЕӢ] gives the cleanest confirmation yet of the labial w-glide ban (previously confirmed on [[йҹ» жҲҲдёҖеҗҲ|жҲҲдёҖеҗҲ]] and [[йҹ» йҲ¬еҗҲ|йҲ¬еҗҲ]]): all 16 labial-initial characters (f/Йұ-type: еҰЁ, иҠі, еқҠ, зҪ”, иЁӘ, жңӣ, еҖЈ, иӮӘ, еҰ„, дәЎ, зҙЎ, ж–№, йҳІ, ж”ҫ, еҝҳ, з¶І) land glide-free on **г„ҡг„«**, while all 8 non-labial characters (еҢЎ, жһү, ж—ә, зҺӢ, зӢӮ, жіҒ, зӯҗ, еҫҖ) carry the w-glide and land on **вәўг„«**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`wang`). Zero exceptions on either side.
