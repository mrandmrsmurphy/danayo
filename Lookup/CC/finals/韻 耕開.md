## CJKV Evolution
耕開 [ɣɛŋ] lands 8 of 12 characters on **ㄚㄫ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ang`).

The remaining four scatter to four different destinations — 茎 (ɣ) to ㄧㄫ, 噌 (t͡ʃʰ) to ㄨㄫ, 箏 (t͡ʃ) to ㄝㄫ, and 萌 (m) to ㄜㄫ — with no crowding to explain any of them: the busiest slot in the main group is p (拼, 繃), and none of the four escapees would have collided with an existing member had they stayed on ㄚㄫ. Unlike the cleaner finals elsewhere in this sweep, 耕開 looks like a genuine multi-way historical split (the 庚/耕/清/青 rime classes are independently known to have merged unevenly across Sinoxenic strata) rather than a single dominant outcome with a few motivated exceptions.
