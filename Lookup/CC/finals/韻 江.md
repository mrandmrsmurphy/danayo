## CJKV Evolution
жұҹ [ЙЈКҢЕӢ] splits 10-3 between **г„ҡг„«** and **вәўг„«** (w-glide) вҖ” and unlike most splits documented so far, this one has a clean, findable conditioning factor. All three вәўг„« members (еҸҢ, ж’һ, зӘ“) have a retroflex or palatal-affricate MC initial (Кғ, Й–, tНЎКғК° respectively), while all ten plain г„ҡг„« members have a velar or labial initial (k, kК°, ЙЈ, p, b). This matches a well-known Mandarin sound change вҖ” retroflex initials (zh/ch/sh) trigger rounding of a following -ang to -uang (hence йӣҷ shuДҒng, ж’һ zhuГ ng, зӘ— chuДҒng) вҖ” and Dan'a'yo's split tracks it directly. The Vowels table's own D-column entry for this final, `ang/wang`, already anticipated exactly this dual outcome.
