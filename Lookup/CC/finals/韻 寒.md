## CJKV Evolution
寒 [ɑn] converges almost completely: 47 of 48 characters land on **ㄚㄋ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`an`).

**䦨** is a true homophone of 蘭/欄 (all l-initial, Mandarin *lán*, Cantonese *laan4*, Korean 란) and dodges the collision with a full escape — shifting both vowel and coda at once, landing alone on **ㄛㄇ** rather than the milder single-feature shifts seen elsewhere in this sweep.
