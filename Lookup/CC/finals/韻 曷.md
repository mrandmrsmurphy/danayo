## CJKV Evolution
жӣ· [Й‘t] converges mostly: 9 of 11 characters land on **г„ҡг„Ҡ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`at`).

**е–қ** and **и‘ӣ** both escape, and both are phonetic relatives of the mainline characters вҖ” е–қ (x) shares the same phonetic series as ЙЈ-initial иӨҗ, and и‘ӣ (k) shares it with k-initial еүІ. Neither escape is crowding-driven (their would-be slots, г„Ҹг„ҡг„Ҡ and г„Қг„ҡг„Ҡ, each hold only one other member), so these read as unconditioned same-family, same-or-similar-initial splits: е–қ shifts vowel to **г„ӣг„Ҡ**, и‘ӣ shifts vowel to **вјҳг„Ҡ**.
