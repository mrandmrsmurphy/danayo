> **Final 蒸** evolved into ㄧㄫ, with a large sibilant scatter across three vowel groups (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
蒸 [ɨŋ] lands 15 of 28 characters on **ㄧㄫ**, matching the y-quality of the Vowels table's documented winner (`ing`).

The palatal sibilant class (d͡ʑ/ɕ/ʑ, all spelled ㄙ) scatters across all three of this final's vowel destinations rather than converging: 剰/烝 land on ㄙㄧㄫ, 縄/丞 land on ㄙㄜㄫ, and 乗/升/勝/承/昇 land on ㄙㄨㄫ — 9 members total, same initial letter, three different outcomes. This is a smaller-scale echo of the massive sibilant scatter documented on [[韻 之|之]].

**夌** is a true homophone of the l-initial trio 菱/凌/陵 (all Mandarin *líng*, Cantonese *ling4*, Korean 릉), which already crowd **ㄜㄫ**; 夌 dodges a fourth collision via vowel-shift, landing alone on **⼜ㄫ**.
