## CJKV Evolution
錫開 [ek] converges heavily: 21 of 23 characters land on **ㄝㄎ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ek`).

**冪** is a true homophone of 覓 (both m-initial, Mandarin *mì*, Cantonese *mik6*, Korean 멱) and dodges the collision via vowel-shift, landing on **ㄧㄎ**. **荻** is a true homophone of the d-initial trio 敵/翟/迪 (all *dí*/*dik6*/적), which already crowd ㄉㄝㄎ with a fourth member (笛); 荻 dodges the same way, landing alongside 冪.
