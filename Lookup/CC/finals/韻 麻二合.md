## CJKV Evolution
йә»дәҢеҗҲ [ЙЈua] is nearly uniform: 14 of 16 characters land on **вәў**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`wa`).

The 2 exceptions are the richest concentration of "identical daughter readings, different outcome" found in this sweep. **дҪӨ** and **з“Ұ** share the *exact same* Mandarin, Cantonese, and Korean readings (wЗҺ, ngaa5, мҷҖ) вҖ” true homophones in every checked daughter language вҖ” yet з“Ұ stays on вәў while дҪӨ lands on **вәўг„Ҡ**. Separately, **е“Ү** shares its readings (wДҒ, waa1, мҷҖ) with зӘӘ and иӣҷ, both of which stay on вәў; е“Ү alone dodges what would otherwise be a three-way pileup, landing on **вәўг„Ё**.
