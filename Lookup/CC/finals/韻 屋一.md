## CJKV Evolution
屋一 [uk] is the division-1 entering-tone counterpart of [[韻 屋三|屋三]]'s division-3 final, and matches the pattern already confirmed for division-1 finals ([[韻 東一|東一]], 寒): it's completely uniform. All 20 characters land on **ㄛㄎ (ok)**, with no exceptions — a clean contrast with 屋三's own two real exceptions (尗, 偪), consistent with the division-1-converges/division-3-fractures pattern already seen across this project.
