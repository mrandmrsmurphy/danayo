## CJKV Evolution
東一 [uŋ] is the first-division (一等) reading of the 東 rime group: Mandarin `-ong` (東 dōng, 公 gōng), Cantonese `-ung`, Japanese `-ou`, and Korean `-ong` all converge cleanly, and Dan'a'yo follows the same convergence to **ong (ㄛㄫ)**. This corpus-wide check bears that out exactly: all 46 characters land on ㄛㄫ, with zero exceptions — one of the simplest finals in the whole system.
