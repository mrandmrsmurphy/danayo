> **Final 支三合** evolved into ㄨㄧ, with one homophony-driven exception (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
支三合 [iuᴇ] is the rounded counterpart of [[韻 支三開|支三開]], and carries the expected w-glide: 12 of 13 characters land on **ㄨㄧ**, matching the Vowels table's own documented winner (`ui`) for this final.

The lone exception is **瑞**, which lands on **⼔** instead. Its regular output would be ㄙㄨㄧ — but that slot is already held by 睡, 随, and 髄, all sharing the same sibilant-type MC initial family (ʑ/z/s) as 瑞's own ʑ. 瑞 is also the only character on this page with a populated `vietnamese` field (*thụy*), consistent with the pattern already confirmed on [[韻 東三|東三]]: when a character needs a novel-but-justified escape from a crowded slot, Vietnamese sometimes supplies the pathway.
