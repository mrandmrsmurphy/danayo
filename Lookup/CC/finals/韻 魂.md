## CJKV Evolution
魂 [uən] lands 34 of 41 characters on **ㄛㄋ**, matching the Vowels table's tentative winner (`~on~`).

The 4-member **ㄨㄋ** group is a shared overflow bucket, collecting escapees from three separately crowded slots within the main group: 豚 and 屯 (both d-initial) dodge a 6-member ㄉㄛㄋ (惇, 鈍, 沌, 敦, 遁, 頓); 樽 dodges a 3-member ㄐㄛㄋ (蹲, 尊, 存); 盆 dodges a 2-member ㄅㄛㄋ (本, 奔). Unlike the four-way scatter documented on [[韻 諄|諄]], these three independent escapees all converge on the *same* alternate vowel rather than each finding a distinct one.

**昷** and **温** (both null ʔ-initial) dodge a smaller 2-member ㄛㄋ crowd (穏, 鴛, also null-initial) via the familiar nasal→stop coda-shift, landing on **ㄛㄆ**. **滾** dodges a 2-member ㄍㄛㄋ (鯤, 昆) by adding a w-glide instead, landing on **⺢ㄋ**.
