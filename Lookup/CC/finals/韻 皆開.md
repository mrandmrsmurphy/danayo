## CJKV Evolution
皆開 [ɣɛi] lands 13 of 19 characters on **⼶**, matching the `e`-type half of the Vowels table's documented dual winner (`e/ye`).

The main slot within that group, ㄍ⼶ (k-initial), is by far the most crowded on this page — 6 characters (戒, 届, 偕, 介, 皆, 界) already occupy it. Two more k-initial characters dodge it via different routes: **階** adds a trailing ㄧ, landing on **⼶ㄧ**; **芥** drops the glide marker entirely, landing on plain **ㄝ**. Same crowded slot, same initial, two different escapes.

矣 similarly dodges a crowded ㄏ⼶ (3 members: 械, 骸, 諧, all x/ɣ-initial like 矣 itself), landing on the wholly distinct **⼔**.

祭 and 斎 (both t͡ʃ-initial, unrepresented in the main ⼶ group at all) land on two different outcomes of their own — ㄝ and ㄚㄧ respectively — an unconditioned split with no crowded slot to explain it, consistent with the "same initial, arbitrary outcome" pattern seen elsewhere in this sweep. 排, the final's other ㄚㄧ member, dodges a more modest 2-member ⼶ slot (拝, 俳, both labial-initial).
