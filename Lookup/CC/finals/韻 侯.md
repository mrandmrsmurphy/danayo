> **Final 侯** evolved into ㄛㄨ, with four scattered escapes (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
侯 [əu] converges heavily: 42 of 46 characters land on **ㄛㄨ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ou`).

**後** is a true homophone of 候 (both x-initial, Mandarin *hòu*, Cantonese *hau6*) — which already shares its 3-member x-initial slot with 吼 and 后 — and dodges via coda-add, landing on **ㄨㄛ**. The remaining three escapees scatter individually: **兜** (d) dodges the 4-member d/t slot (投, 豆, 頭, 痘) via a full vowel change to **ㄨ**; **偷** (tʰ) shifts to **ㄛㄇ**; **咅** (pʰ), the only pʰ-initial character in this final, lands alone on **⼄ㄨ**.

Data-quality flag: 咅's Cantonese field (*tau3*) doesn't fit its own pʰ initial — a p/pʰ-initial character would be expected to start with *p-* or *f-* in Cantonese, not *t-*. Possibly miscopied from a d-initial neighbor like 兜 (*dau1*).
