## CJKV Evolution
дҪіеҗҲ [ЙЈuЙӣ] has only 2 characters, both carrying a glide as expected for a еҗҲ (rounded) final. жҺӣ (plain k-initial) lands on **вәўг„§**; е’ј (aspirated kК°-initial) lands on **вј¶г„§** instead. With only one member each, there's no crowding to explain the split вҖ” it simply tracks the aspiration difference between the two, though with a sample of 2 this isn't enough to generalize into a rule.
