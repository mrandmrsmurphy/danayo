> **Final 青開** evolved into ㄝㄫ, with two massively-crowded slots producing a four-route overflow scatter (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
青開 [eŋ] converges heavily: 48 of 51 characters land on **ㄝㄫ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`eng`).

Two slots inside that group are exceptionally crowded, and their overflow produces four different escape routes:

The d/t slot **ㄉㄝㄫ** holds 8 members (丁, 艇, 釘, 亭, 停, 庭, 廷, 挺). **定** and **頂**, both also d/t, dodge it by shifting the initial letter to ㄉ→ㄐ, landing on **ㄐㄝㄫ** alongside d-initial 錠 — a third overflow slot rather than a single escape. **鼎** (t) dodges the same crowd via vowel-shift instead, to **ㄧㄫ**. **𡈼** (tʰ) goes furthest, shifting both vowel and coda to **ㄧㄋ**, bypassing even the smaller 3-member ㄊㄝㄫ aspirated slot (汀, 訂, 町) it would otherwise have joined.

The l slot **ㄌㄝㄫ** holds 9 members (令, 鈴, 零, 伶, 澪, 玲, 羚, 霊, 齢) — the largest in this final. **笭**, a true homophone of all of them (*líng*/*ling4*/령), dodges via glide-add, landing on **⼶ㄫ**.

Each escapee lands on a different destination, avoiding convergence with the others — the same deliberate-scatter pattern documented on 諄 and 蕭 earlier in this sweep.
