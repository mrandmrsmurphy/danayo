> **Final 痕** evolved into ㄚㄋ, with two phonetic-family escapees (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
痕 [ən] lands 6 of 8 characters on **ㄚㄋ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`an`).

This final is unusual in that nearly its entire roster shares one phonetic component, 艮: 恨, 痕, 根, 艮, 懇, 墾, and 齦 all derive from it. The 2 exceptions — **根** and **痕** itself — each dodge their closest phonetic relative rather than a crowded slot: 根 (k-initial) would regularly match 艮's own ㄍㄚㄋ; 痕 (ɣ-initial) would regularly match 恨's ㄏㄚㄋ. Both instead land on **ㄜㄋ**, the same phenomenon already documented for the 皮/疲/被 family on [[聲 並|聲並]], just concentrated within a single small final this time.
