## CJKV Evolution
и•ӯ [eu] splits 12-9 between **вјҳг„Ё** and **г„ӣг„Ё**, roughly matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`au`).

The г„ӣг„Ё group is dominated by dental stops (d/t/tК°: и·і, йіҘ, зӘ•, еҪ«, зңә, йҮЈ вҖ” 6 of 9 members), and four further dental-stop characters dodge that crowd via four completely different routes: **жқЎ** and **иІӮ** both drop the -г„Ё entirely, landing on bare **г„ӣ**; **йө°** adds a w-glide instead, landing on **г„Ёг„ӣ**; **жҢ‘** shifts vowel, landing on **г„ҡг„Ё**. Four characters, one crowded initial class, four distinct escapes вҖ” echoing the multi-route scatter already seen on [[йҹ» и«„|и«„]] and [[йҹ» иұӘ|иұӘ]].

Two further singletons: **зӘҲ** (null-initial) lands on **вј„г„Ё**, and **еҸ«** (the only k-initial character on this final) drops the -г„Ё, landing on bare **вјҳ**.
