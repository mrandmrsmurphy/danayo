> **Final еұ‘й–Ӣ** evolved into г„қг„Ҡ, with two exceptions (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
еұ‘й–Ӣ [et] is a division-4 final and converges cleanly: 20 of 22 characters land on **г„қг„Ҡ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`et`).

**иҝӯ** dodges a 3-member г„үг„қг„Ҡ (зӘ’, е§Ә, и·Ң, all d/t-initial like иҝӯitself) via a vowel-shift, landing on **г„ҡг„Ҡ**. **жҲӘ**, the only dНЎК’-initial character on this final, lands on **г„ӣг„Ҡ** with no same-initial neighbor to collide with вҖ” an unconditioned singleton.
