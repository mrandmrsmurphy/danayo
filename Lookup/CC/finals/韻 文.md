> **Final 文** evolved into ㄨㄋ, with three labial-initial escapees (see below)

## CJKV Evolution
文 [ɨun] converges cleanly: 28 of 31 characters land on **ㄨㄋ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`un`).

The 3 exceptions are all labial-initial and each dodges a different crowded slot within the main group. **糞** (f-initial) dodges a 4-member ㄈㄨㄋ (奮, 芬, 粉, 雰) via the familiar coda-shift, landing on **ㄨㄇ**. **紛** (also f-initial, sharing that same crowded slot) dodges via a vowel-shift instead, landing on **ㄜㄋ**.

**扮** is the richest case: its own initial is a plain bilabial p, which would regularly land it on the 5-member ㄅㄨㄋ (賁, 分, 焚, 憤, 墳) — the most crowded slot on this page. Rather than a single dodge, 扮 combines *two* escape mechanisms already seen separately elsewhere in this sweep: it borrows the labiodental ㄈ letter (the same initial-letter-reassignment trick documented on [[韻 灰|灰]]'s 徘/佩/培/輩) *and* shifts its vowel, landing on **ㄈㄧㄋ** — doubly displaced from where its own MC initial would predict.
