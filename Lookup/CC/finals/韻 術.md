## CJKV Evolution
術 [iuɪt] is nearly uniform: 10 of 11 characters land on **ㄨㄊ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ut`). **橘**, the sole k-initial character on this final with no same-initial neighbor to collide with, lands on **⼜ㄊ** — an unconditioned singleton pick.
