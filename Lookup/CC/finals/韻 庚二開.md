## CJKV Evolution
庚二開 [ɣæŋ] converges cleanly: 22 of 25 characters land on **ㄚㄫ**, matching the Vowels table's documented winner (`ang`).

**荇** dodges a 3-member ㄏㄚㄫ (亨, 杏, 衡, all x/ɣ-initial like 荇) by adding a y-glide, landing on **⼶ㄫ**, an unconditioned singleton with no same-initial neighbor. **槍**, also unconditioned, adds a w-glide instead, landing on its own **⺢ㄫ**. **檸** also belongs to this final by (borrowed-analogy) MC classification, but is a confirmed `#cranberry` unattested in the CC corpus (`mc_id: 0`); its Dan'a'yo reading was overridden to **ㄌㄝ** rather than following the ⼶ㄫ derivation — see [[AIOS/memory/feedback_cranberry_transitivity.md]].
