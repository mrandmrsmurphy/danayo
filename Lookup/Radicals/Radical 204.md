> Radical 204 is 黹, embroidery — 黹 itself is not used as a standalone character in the corpus. It is a pictogram (象形) depicting a cloud-like decorative pattern stitched onto bronzes and ceremonial dress, and the compounds under it name specific woven or embroidered patterns.
