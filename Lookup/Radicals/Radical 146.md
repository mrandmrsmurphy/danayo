> Radical 146 is 襾, a roof or cover — the top and sides of an open container, suggesting shelter and enclosure. Originally associated with the west (where the sun sets), it evolved to mean covering and concealment. This radical appears in compounds relating to covers, roofs, clothing, concealment, and protective coverings.
