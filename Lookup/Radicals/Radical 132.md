> [[Radicals]]
> Radical 132 is 自, self or nose — depicting a nose in profile, which originally meant "self" due to ancient Chinese philosophy where the nose represented the individual. This radical appears in compounds relating to self, identity, one's own nature, and things that arise spontaneously or naturally.
