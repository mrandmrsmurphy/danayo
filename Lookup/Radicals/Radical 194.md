> [[Radicals]]
> Radical 194 is 鬼, ghost or demon — a pictograph of a large, frightening masked head atop a kneeling figure, associated with death, the uncanny, and the spirit world. Compounds under it distinguish kinds of soul and spirit in classical cosmology (魂 the yang-soul, 魄 the yin-soul) and cover the supernatural more broadly, from 魅 "enchant, bewitching spirit" to 魔, itself a phonetic borrowing used to render Sanskrit *māra* in Buddhist translation.
