> Radical 203 is 黒/黑, black — depicting a chimney window blackened by soot, or by another account a person's face marked by a tattoo of punishment. It covers dark colors and things darkened, from 黛 (a black pigment used to paint eyebrows) to 黙 (silent, mouth held dark and still) and 黜 (to demote or dismiss, figuratively "blacken" someone's standing).
