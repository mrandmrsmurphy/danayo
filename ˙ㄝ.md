**˙ㄝ/'yo/요** is a Dan'a'yo [[Syllables|syllable]] meaning "and" (与), or "fish" (魚).  It may mean, with introduction, "palanquin" (輿).  For other uses in compounds, see below.
## Characters
### Common
- [[与]] "and" (for nouns)
- [[魚]] "fish"
### Advanced
- [[輿]] "palanquin"
- [[圄]] "prison", but requires <ruby>[[囹圄]]<rt>˙ㄝ</rt></ruby> [[囹]]
### Naming
- [[馭]] "drive", but name only
