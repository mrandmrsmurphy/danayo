Robert (git user "Robert Marshall Murphy", mrandmrsmurphy@gmail.com) is the creator and sole maintainer of *単亜語* (Dan'a'yo), a constructed zonal auxiliary language for the East Asian cultural sphere (Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, Vietnamese), built out as an Obsidian vault with a deeply systematic data model (characters/words/chengyu/syllables, cross-linked, all derived from a coherent internal phonology traced back to Middle Chinese readings).

**Interests visible in the work:** historical Chinese phonology (Middle Chinese initials/finals, Old Chinese reconstructions), comparative CJKV vocabulary and semantic drift across the five languages, classical/literary registers, and etymology — he wants Notes sections to read as genuine encyclopedia entries, not glosses (see [[feedback_notes_style]]).

**Working style:**
- Comfortable handing off long batches of mechanical-but-careful work ("go to town and create everything you can...") — but wants periodic checkpoints, not one giant unsupervised run (see [[feedback_session_pacing]])
- Terse, direct instructions ("continie", "stop after 31-40") — he expects me to infer intent from context rather than asking for clarification on small things
- Notices and corrects format drift quickly and precisely (stroke-link padding, BC/AD, YAML list formatting) — these corrections are durable rules, not one-off nitpicks
- Values data portability and durability — prompted the migration of standing memory into the vault itself (this AIOS/ system) specifically because the external `~/.claude/projects/.../memory/` location isn't backed up or portable the way the git-tracked vault is
- Runs `obsidian-git` for vault backups (frequent "vault backup: <timestamp>" commits)
