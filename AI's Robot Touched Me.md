###   Character : 周  (周.md)

  Wiktionary: 象形

  Description: Oracle bone script: Pictogram (象形): a jade artifact with carved patterns. Origin

  

  Proposed bullet:

  - [List of 象形](lookup/List%20of%20象形.md): Oracle bone script: Pictogram (象形): a jade artifact with carved patterns. Original form of 彫 (OC *tɯːw, “to carve”).
