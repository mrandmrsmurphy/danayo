## Parts

1. Content Words - <ruby>実詞<rt>ㄙㄧㄊㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - able to be topic or comment
	1. Nouns - <ruby>名詞<rt>ㄇㄧㄥㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - need copulas to be comments
	2. Proper Nouns - <ruby>固有名詞<rt>ㄍㄛ·˙ㄨㄇㄧㄥㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - Nouns + cannot take modification
	3. Eventives - [[事詞]] - need an object (basically transitive verbs)
	4. Statives - [[性詞]] - expected to modify (like adjectives and stative verbs, adnominal)
	5. Copula - [[繋詞]] - a closed set of six verbs
		1. 是 - to equal
		2. 非 - to not equal
		3. 有 - to have
		4. 無 - to not have
		5. 在 - to exist
		6. 莫 - to not exist
2. Function Words - <ruby>助詞<rt>ㄐㄛㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - attach to or stand-in for words
	1. Relationals - <ruby>関詞<rt>ㄍˇㄚㄇㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - suffixing
		1. Frame-forming (<ruby>域<rt>ˇㄧㄎ</rt></ruby><ruby>化<rt>ㄏˇㄚ</rt></ruby>) - **case particles - <ruby>格助詞<rt>ㄍㄚㄎㄐㄛㄙㄚ</rt></ruby>**
			1. 于 (animate dative)
			2. 於 (locative)
			3. 以 (instrumental)
			4. 共 (comitative)
			5. 比 (comparative)
			6. 由 (elative)
			7. 至 (terminative)
			8. 似 (similative)
			9. 哉 (vocative)
			10. 様 (adverbial)
		2. Topic-retaining (<ruby>主題<rt>ㄐㄨㄊㄝㄧ</rt></ruby><ruby>保持<rt>ㄅㄚㄨㄉㄧ</rt></ruby>)
			1. 丈 (“only”)
			2. 且 (“also”)
			3. 公 (polite suffix)
	2. Modifiers - [[修飾語]]
		1. "Tense" ([[時制]])
			1. 已 - perfective
			2. 未 - negative perfective
			3. 将 - future
			4. 中 - continuous
		2. Demonstratives - [[指示詞]]
			1. 此 - near
			2. 其 - middle
			3. 彼 - far
		3. Quantifiers - [[量化詞]]
			1. 某 - some
			2. 皆 - all, every
			3. 多 - many
			4. 少 - few
			5. 全 - whole, entire
			6. 他 - another, additional
		4. Moods - [[情態]]
			1. 可 - able
			2. 不可 - unable
			3. 可不 - able to not
			4. 不可不 - unable to not
			5. 不 - not
			6. 可以 - permissive
			7. 不可以 - impermissive
		5. Ideophones - [[擬詞]] - a subgroup that only modifies comments and are typically the result of reduplication.  Some are so casual as to be 網語体 only.
	3. Correlatives - <ruby>代詞<rt>ㄉㄚ˙ㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - pronouns
		1. Correlative Pronouns - <ruby>対応<rt>ㄉㄛ˙·ㄧㄥ</rt></ruby><ruby>代詞<rt>ㄉㄚ˙ㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - see table
		2. Personal Pronouns - <ruby>人称<rt>ㄋㄧㄋㄑㄧㄥ</rt></ruby><ruby>代詞<rt>ㄉㄚ˙ㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - namely:
			1. I - 我
			2. Thou - 君
			3. We - 我等
			4. You - 君等
			5. I (humble) ARCHAIC - 俺
			6. We (royal) - 朕
	4. Modals - <ruby>語気<rt>·˙ㄛㄎㄧ˙</rt></ruby><ruby>助詞<rt>ㄐㄛㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - Sentence Final Particles
		1. Dependent ([[依存]]) - Can be free-standing
		2. Interjections ([[自立]]) - [[感詞]] - These can be SFP or stand alone as a complete utterance
	5. Linkers - <ruby>連接詞<rt>ㄌ˙ㄝㄋㄐㄛㄆㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - conjunctions
3. Special Words - <ruby>特詞<rt>ㄉㄨㄎㄙㄚ</rt></ruby>
	1. Numerals - <ruby>数詞<rt>ㄙㄨㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - make count phrases
		1. One 一
		2. Two
		3. Three
		4. Four
		5. Five
		6. Six
		7. Seven
		8. Eight
		9. Nine
		10. Ten
		11. Hundred - 百
		12. Thousand
		13. Myriad - 万
		14. Hundred million - 億
		15. Trillion - 兆
		16. Nonillion - 一溝
		17. Half - 半
	2. Classifiers - <ruby>量詞<rt>ㄌ˙ㄚㄥㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - make count phrases
		1. People - 人, any person without reference to status, etc.
		2. Honorific - 名, deferential term for person, etc.
		3. Machines - 台, computers, cars, blenders, nanobots, etc.
		4. Animals - 匹, cat, elephants, mice, platypuses, etc.
		5. Books - 冊, books, magazines, newspapers, e-zines, etc.
		6. Cups - 杯, glass of water, shots of alcohol, etc.
		7. Flats - 枚, pizza, paper, plywood, etc.
		8. Long-Thins - 条, pencils, noodles, fingers, etc.
		9. Times - 段, seconds, days, years, millennia, etc.
		10. Events - 番, turns, happenings, repeats, etc.
		11. Bundles - 束, groups, bouquets, bales, classes, etc.
		12. Individual - 個, misc.
	3. Determiners - <ruby>限定詞<rt>ㄏㄚㄋㄐㄝㄥㄙㄚ</rt></ruby> - used only in writing, not actually closed
		1. Names - 阿
		2. Amphibians - 蛙
		3. Birds - 鳥
		4. Sea-animals - 魚
		5. Creepy-crawlies - 虫
		6. Mammals - 獣
		7. Reptiles - 龍
		8. Flowers - 花
		9. Grasses - 草
		10. Trees - 木
		11. Clothes - 服
		12. Food - 飯
		13. Devices - 機
		14. Ideas - 義
		15. Structures - 構
